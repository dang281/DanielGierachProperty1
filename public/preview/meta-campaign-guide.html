Meta Ads Campaign Guide

Seven Hills Property Report
Lead Generation Campaign

Prepared for Daniel Gierach · April 2026 · Use alongside the Seven Hills video ad

Before you start: You need your Meta Pixel ID from Meta Business Suite → Events Manager. Once you have it, replace YOUR_PIXEL_ID in both Layout.astro and LandingLayout.astro — it's already wired up and ready to fire Lead events.

1. Campaign Setup in Meta Ads Manager

1

Create a new Campaign

Objective: Leads (if using instant form) or Website Conversions (recommended — builds your pixel audience for retargeting). Campaign name: Seven Hills — Property Report — April 2026

2

Enable Advantage Campaign Budget

Start at $45/day AUD total. Meta will distribute across ad sets. Review after 5–7 days once the pixel has enough data.

3

Create 3 Ad Sets (audiences)

See the audience table below. Run all three simultaneously — let Meta optimise toward the best performer.

4. Destination URL

Point both ad variations to the dedicated Seven Hills landing page:

https://danielgierach.com/lp/seven-hills?utm_source=meta&utm_medium=paid_social&utm_campaign=seven_hills_apr26&utm_content=variation_a
https://danielgierach.com/lp/seven-hills?utm_source=meta&utm_medium=paid_social&utm_campaign=seven_hills_apr26&utm_content=variation_b
Why a dedicated landing page? The /lp/seven-hills page has no nav, one CTA, and suburb-specific copy. It will convert significantly better than sending people to the homepage or the general property-worth page.

5. Pixel Setup Checklist

  • Get your Pixel ID from Meta Business Suite → Events Manager
  • Replace YOUR_PIXEL_ID in src/layouts/Layout.astro (covers all site pages)
  • Replace YOUR_PIXEL_ID in src/layouts/LandingLayout.astro (covers the landing page)
  • Deploy to Vercel using the dang281 remote
  • Verify pixel is firing using Meta Pixel Helper Chrome extension
  • Confirm Lead event fires when address is submitted on /lp/seven-hills
  • Set up Lead as a conversion event in Meta Ads Manager → Events Manager → Configure

Review at day 7: Pause the worst-performing ad set. Put that budget into the winner. Don't make changes before day 5 — the algorithm needs time to learn.

7. Retargeting (Start Week 3)

Once you have 100+ website visitors in your pixel, build these retargeting audiences:

  • Hot: Visited /lp/seven-hills but did NOT submit — show a different ad with more urgency ("Still curious what your home is worth?")
  • Warm: Visited any page on danielgierach.com in last 30 days — show the video ad
  • Lookalike: Upload your existing lead/client list to Meta → create a 1% Lookalike Audience in QLD → scale budget here once CPL is proven

8. Quick-Expand Template

Once the Seven Hills campaign is profitable, clone the campaign for each suburb. Swap out:

  • The video (use the suburb-specific version you recorded)
  • The landing page URL (/lp/murarrie, /lp/camp-hill, etc.)
  • The location targeting
  • The suburb name in the ad copy

Everything else — structure, budgets, pixel setup — stays the same.
