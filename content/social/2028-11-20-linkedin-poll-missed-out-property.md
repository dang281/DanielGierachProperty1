## Caption

Losing a property you were genuinely serious about is one of the more frustrating experiences in real estate. It is also one of the most common. The inner east buyer pool is deep in spring, and many of the best properties attract more than one committed buyer.

The reasons buyers miss out vary. Sometimes it is price: the other buyer had more capacity or was willing to pay more to secure it. Sometimes it is conditions: an unconditional offer beat a conditional one at the same price. Sometimes it is speed: the buyer who had their finance pre-approved and their solicitor ready moved in 24 hours while others were still organising paperwork.

The buyers who tend to miss out repeatedly in Brisbane's inner east are often not far behind on price. They are behind on preparation.

Have you ever missed out on a property you were seriously considering buying?
