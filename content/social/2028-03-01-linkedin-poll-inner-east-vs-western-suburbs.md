## Caption

The Brisbane western suburbs corridor, including Ashgrove, Bardon, Toowong, and Indooroopilly, has historically been Brisbane's other prestige residential market. The inner east, including Bulimba, Hawthorne, Camp Hill, and Norman Park, sits on the other side of the city with a different character and a different buyer profile.

Both corridors have seen significant price growth over the past five years. The comparison question keeps coming up: where is better value right now?

I work exclusively in the inner east and I have a clear view on where I would put my own money. But I am genuinely curious what others think.

Do you think Brisbane's inner east is better value than the western suburbs right now?
