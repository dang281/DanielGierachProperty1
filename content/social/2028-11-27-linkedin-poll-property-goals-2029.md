## Caption

With December approaching, it is a natural time to start thinking about what 2029 looks like from a property perspective. Whether that is finally pulling the trigger on a sale, making a first purchase, or reassessing an existing portfolio, the decisions people make in the next few months tend to set up the first quarter of the new year.

Brisbane's inner east heading into 2029 is in a different position to where it was three years ago. The Olympic infrastructure conversation is real and getting more concrete. The supply constraint in well-located suburbs is persistent. The underlying demand from interstate migration and natural population growth has not reversed.

What is your biggest property goal heading into 2029?
