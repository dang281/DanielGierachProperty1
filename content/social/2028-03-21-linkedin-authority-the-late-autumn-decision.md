## Caption

Sellers who are still deciding in the third week of March have a practical choice to make: move now and launch in the last three to four weeks of the autumn window, or hold until September and enter the spring market.

Both options are reasonable. The choice depends on a few specific things.

If you are ready to go, the property is prepared, and you have somewhere to move, launching now puts you in front of buyers who have been active for months and want to commit before the market cools. The autumn window does not close overnight, but the buyer urgency does soften in April and May as the year rolls toward winter.

If you are not ready, if the property needs work, if you have not sorted out finance for your next purchase, forcing a campaign into the next three weeks usually produces a worse outcome than waiting until September and doing it properly. A rushed campaign in the right season still underperforms a well-prepared one in what most people consider the wrong season.

The question I ask sellers at this point in the year is not "should we go now or wait?" It is "are you ready?" That answer tells you which choice is right.
