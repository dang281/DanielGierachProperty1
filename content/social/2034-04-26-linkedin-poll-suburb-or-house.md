## Caption

In Brisbane's inner east, the suburb and the house are both strong variables. Some buyers start with a suburb and then find the best house available within it. Others start with a floorplan or land size requirement and then look at which suburbs deliver it at the right price. The decision-making sequence shapes everything about the search. How did you, or would you, approach it?
