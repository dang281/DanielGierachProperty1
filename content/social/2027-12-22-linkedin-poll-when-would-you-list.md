## Caption

For Brisbane's inner east, there is usually a clear split among vendors about when to bring a property to market after the Christmas break. Some swear by listing in January to catch the buyers who are refreshed and ready. Others hold until late February or March when the full autumn buyer pool has formed.

Both have merit depending on the property, the suburb, and what is competing at the time.

If you were selling your home in early 2028, when would you list?

## Poll options

- January (early bird catches the buyer)
- February (market is forming, less risk)
- March or April (peak autumn window)
- I'd let the agent decide
