## Caption

The February inquiry pattern in Brisbane's inner east is one of the more reliable signals I have for what the March and April campaign season is going to look like.

When I am getting a high volume of appraisal and preparation conversations in February, the autumn listing volume tends to be strong. When February is quiet, March is often thinner than people expect, and the peak of the season shifts later into April.

Right now, the volume of incoming conversations suggests March is going to be a full market. That has implications for both buyers and vendors. More listings means more choice for buyers, but it also means more competition for vendors who have not differentiated their property through presentation or price strategy.

The window for launching a campaign before the field fills is still open, but it is narrowing.

How urgently are you thinking about a property move in the first half of 2034?
