## Caption

The pre-Christmas window in Brisbane's inner east is real, but it is shorter than most vendors realise.

To settle before Christmas 2030, a property needs to be under contract by late November at the latest, accounting for a standard 30 to 42-day settlement. That means launching a campaign in October, not December.

For vendors who want to be in the hands-on-deck category rather than listing in February, the decision point is now, not in six weeks. The buyers who need to be settled before Christmas are already in the market.

If you were selling your home this spring, when would you list?
