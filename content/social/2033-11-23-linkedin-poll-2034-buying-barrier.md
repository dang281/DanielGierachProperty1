## Caption

The inner east property market in 2034 will be shaped partly by what buyers do and do not feel they can achieve. Understanding where buyers are sitting in terms of their primary barrier is useful for both the market and for the conversations I have at open homes. Barriers change over time. In 2021 the challenge was finding anything available at all. In 2023 it was borrowing capacity under rising rates. In 2033 the answer is more varied depending on the buyer group, the price point, and where someone is in their search. First-time buyers face a different set of constraints than upsizers, and interstate arrivals are coming from different conditions again. If you are planning to buy in Brisbane's inner east in 2034, what is the single biggest thing standing between you and the right property?
