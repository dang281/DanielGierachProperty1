## Caption

LVR is the percentage of a property's value you are borrowing. Borrow $800K on a $1M property and that is 80% LVR. Go higher and you are usually paying Lenders Mortgage Insurance. Most lenders get cautious above 80%.

In the inner east, where median prices sit well above $1M for houses, the LVR calculation directly affects what buyers can access and how quickly.

What LVR are you comfortable borrowing at?

Daniel Gierach
Ray White Bulimba · Inner East Brisbane
