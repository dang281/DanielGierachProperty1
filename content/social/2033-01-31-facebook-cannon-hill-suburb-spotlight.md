## Caption

Cannon Hill has earned its place as one of the inner east's most accessible entry points for buyers who want character housing without the price tag of Hawthorne or Bulimba. The Lytton Road corridor gives the suburb strong connectivity east and west, and the proximity to Westfield Carindale keeps the suburb on family shortlists.

The first-home buyer presence in Cannon Hill is genuine and consistent. Buyers making their first move into the property market in Brisbane's inner east have found Cannon Hill to be the suburb where their budget stretches furthest while keeping them within reach of the lifestyle and infrastructure they want. That demand is structural. As long as inner east price points remain where they are, Cannon Hill will have a motivated entry-level buyer pool. ⚠️ VERIFY

The housing stock ranges from original post-war homes through to more recent infill development. The renovation market in Cannon Hill has been active, and well-executed improvements to original homes have delivered results that have steadily reset the suburb's comparable sale benchmarks.

What Cannon Hill offers vendors in 2033 is a buyer pool with genuine motivation and a market that has been tracking upward as the inner east price gradient pulls demand further from the river. If your property is original and well-maintained, there is a buyer cohort who will pay properly for the right home in this suburb.
