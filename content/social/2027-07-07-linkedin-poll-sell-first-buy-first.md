## Caption

One of the questions I get most often from people who already own a home and want to move is this: do you sell first or buy first? Both paths carry real risk. Selling first gives you certainty on funds but means you may be renting in between, or moving twice. Buying first gives you continuity but means carrying two loans until the first property settles. In a market where good properties go quickly, neither approach is perfect. What would you do?

## Poll options

- Sell first (certainty on what I have)
- Buy first (no gap, no double move)
- Both simultaneously with a long settlement
- It depends entirely on the market
