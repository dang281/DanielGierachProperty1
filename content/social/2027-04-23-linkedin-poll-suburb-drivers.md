## Caption

Buyers in Brisbane's inner east are not all looking for the same thing. For some, the school catchment is the entire brief. For others, it is the walk to the river or the 12-minute train to the CBD. And for plenty of people, the deciding factor is simply what they could actually afford and still get a house.

The inner east has become one of the most sought-after corridors in Queensland, which means the buyer pool is broad and the motivations are varied. Curious what actually drives the decision for people in this network.

What is the biggest factor that determines where you choose to buy in Brisbane's inner east?

## Poll options

- School catchment (it drives the premium and I know it)
- Proximity to the river, parks, or walkability
- Transport and commute to the CBD
- What I could actually afford in the area
