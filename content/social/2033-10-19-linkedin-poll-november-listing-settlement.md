## Caption

Settlement timing is one of those details that does not come up much in general conversation but becomes genuinely important the moment you are in a campaign. A vendor who has a clear preference on settlement date has something to offer a buyer that goes beyond the property itself. It changes the negotiation. I see very different attitudes to this when I talk to vendors planning a November launch, and I am curious whether the broader pattern holds here. If you are listing your home in November, when would you prefer to settle?

## Poll options

- Before Christmas (a December settlement)
- Early January (a clean start to the new year)
- Late January to February (the most common preference)
- Settlement date is not the priority, price is
