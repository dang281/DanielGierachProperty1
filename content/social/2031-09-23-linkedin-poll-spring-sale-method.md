## Caption

Method of sale in Brisbane's inner east spring market has historically favoured auction for properties with broad buyer appeal in the $1 million to $2 million range. The auction process suits spring conditions particularly well because buyer competition tends to be at its seasonal peak, and the transparent auction day format converts that competition into price. Private treaty remains appropriate for properties with a narrower buyer demographic or where the vendor has a specific price requirement. How would you approach it?

## Poll options

- Auction, transparent and produces competition
- Private treaty with a price guide
- Expression of interest
- I would let the agent recommend the right method
