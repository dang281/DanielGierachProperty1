## Caption

Brisbane hosted the 2032 Games in July and August. Two months on, the city has returned to its normal pace. Infrastructure is in place: the Cross River Rail corridor, upgraded pedestrian and cycling connections, improved public spaces around major venues. Whether all of that delivers lasting value to property owners in the inner east is a question the market will answer over the next few years, not a conclusion anyone can draw today. The buyers returning to the inner east now are operating on their own assessment of what the city looks like post-Games. What is your read?
