## Caption

Q1 2034 has now largely played out across the Brisbane inner east. January gave buyers time to research. February brought the first wave of committed campaigns. March delivered the listing volume and buyer activity that defines the autumn peak.

The gap between expectation and experience is one of the more revealing things to measure at this point in the season. People who came into the year expecting a soft or flat market often find it more active than they anticipated. People who came in expecting a boom sometimes find the pace more measured at street level than the headlines suggested.

From where I sit, the inner east has delivered a productive Q1. Buyer numbers at opens have been consistent, well-presented properties have moved with conviction, and vendor results have generally matched or exceeded what was agreed at campaign launch. But I am interested in whether this matches what people in and around the market have been observing.

How would you rate Brisbane's inner east property market in Q1 2034?
