## Caption

The Christmas and New Year period in Brisbane produces a specific type of property opportunity. Listings are lower, buyer competition is reduced, and some vendors who are motivated will negotiate more actively than they would in spring. At the same time, the full autumn buyer pool does not arrive until February and March.

For buyers who are in a position to purchase and are weighing up the timing, the question is a genuine one.

If you are currently in the market to buy in Brisbane's inner east, what is your approach over the summer break?

## Poll options

- Actively looking and ready to buy if the right property appears
- Taking a break and resuming in February
- Watching online but not attending inspections
- Have already purchased or paused the search
