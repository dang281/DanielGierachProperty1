## Caption

Winter in Brisbane's inner east is often described as a slower market. Transaction volumes do fall, and the number of open homes per weekend drops. But the buyers who are active in winter are typically more serious than the spring browser who is still three months from making a decision.

Whether that translates to more or less competition at the property level depends entirely on the suburb, the price point, and how many comparable listings are available.

If you are currently looking to buy in Brisbane's inner east, how would you describe buyer competition right now?
