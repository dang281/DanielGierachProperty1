## Caption

November is the last realistic window for a pre-Christmas campaign in Brisbane's inner east. Once you get to mid-November, the buyer pool starts to thin as people shift focus to the end of year. That does not mean property stops selling, but the dynamics change.

The question for vendors weighing up a November listing versus waiting is a genuine one, and the answer depends on your specific property and timeline.

If you are considering selling in the next twelve months, what is holding you back from listing before Christmas?
