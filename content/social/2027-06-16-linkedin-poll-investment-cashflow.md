## Caption

The classic property investment debate has not been resolved by rising prices or higher interest rates. Some investors are entirely focused on capital growth and are comfortable with neutral or negative cashflow in the short term. Others will not touch a property that does not cover its costs from day one. Both approaches have produced good outcomes in Brisbane's inner east over the past decade. Neither is objectively right. The answer usually depends on where you are in your investment journey and what your current income position looks like. Where do you sit?
