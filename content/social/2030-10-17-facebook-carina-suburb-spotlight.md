## Caption

Carina is often grouped with Carina Heights, but the two suburbs have a distinct character and a distinct market. Carina proper, on the flat land around the shopping centre and extending south toward Creek Road, attracts a buyer looking for value at a price point below the headline inner east suburbs, with the lifestyle access of a suburb that borders Carindale and Norman Park.

The housing mix in Carina includes significant postwar stock that has been renovated to varying degrees, alongside newer townhouse development on the main roads. The full-block character homes in the streets east of Creek Road and north toward the Carina Leagues Club have been the consistent performers in the sales data.

School catchments in Carina cover Carina State School and Mayfield State School for primary, with secondary school options including Marshall Road State School. Buyers with families run through the catchment implications carefully before making an offer in this suburb.

Carina's access to the Gateway Motorway and Old Cleveland Road makes it a practical choice for buyers with employment outside the CBD core. That commuting practicality draws buyers who would otherwise look at more expensive suburbs but find Carina's transport links work just as well for their lifestyle.

For homeowners in Carina's character home streets, spring is the season where buyer competition is most active.
