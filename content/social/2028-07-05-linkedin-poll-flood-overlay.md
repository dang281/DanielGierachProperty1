## Caption

Parts of Brisbane's inner east carry a flood overlay. Sections of Hawthorne, Bulimba, Morningside, and suburbs further upstream sit in catchments that, under a significant event, would take water. The 2022 flood is still fresh in a lot of buyers' minds.

For some people, any overlay is a hard no. For others, it depends entirely on the specific lot, the floor height, and the construction type. Queenslanders on high stumps in flood-prone streets regularly sell well, because the buyers understand what they are looking at.

Where do you land?
