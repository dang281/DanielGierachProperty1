## Caption

Winter is typically when Brisbane's property market sees lower transaction volumes, but the buyers who remain active in June and July are often the most serious in the market. Less browsing, more intent. For a buyer who is genuinely ready, winter offers a lower-competition environment than spring, particularly in the inner east where listing stock also drops. The question is whether buyers are actually using this window or waiting for spring to start looking properly. If you are currently looking to buy in Brisbane, what are you doing this winter?
