## Caption

Spring campaigns that perform well are almost always the result of preparation that started in July or August, not September. The vendors who get the best results in a spring market are the ones who spent winter addressing the things that affect presentation and buyer confidence: painting, landscaping, tradework, styling planning. By the time the market opens in September, the preparation window has typically closed. If you were listing your inner east home this spring, what would you address first?

## Poll options

- External presentation (painting, gardens, kerb appeal)
- Internal condition (repairs, fresh paint inside, cleaning)
- Decluttering and styling preparation
- Getting an appraisal first before deciding what to do
