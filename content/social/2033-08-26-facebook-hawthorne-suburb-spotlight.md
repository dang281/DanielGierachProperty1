## Caption

Hawthorne's spring buyer pool is one of the most financially committed in the inner east. The buyers who pursue Hawthorne properties in September and October have typically been active in this market for a year or more. They understand the price range, they have finance in place, and they know the streets. When a property comes to market in Hawthorne that is well presented and correctly priced, it does not sit.

Oxford Street is one of the genuine highlights of the inner east lifestyle offer. The combination of independent restaurants, specialty retail, and the weekend pedestrian energy of the strip is part of what Hawthorne buyers are purchasing when they buy in this suburb. Properties within walking distance of Oxford Street include that proximity in their buyer pitch, and the market prices it accordingly.

The river access along Hawthorne's eastern boundary, including the walking paths connecting through to the Bulimba waterfront, gives the suburb a recreational amenity that few inner east suburbs can match. For buyers who prioritise daily movement and outdoor access, that connection is a meaningful part of the Hawthorne proposition.

Spring is when the Hawthorne market produces its strongest comparable sales evidence of the year. Vendors preparing a campaign for September should be ready by the end of August.
