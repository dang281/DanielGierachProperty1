## Caption

Equity is the difference between what your property is worth and what you owe. If your home is worth $900K and your loan is $500K, you have $400K in equity. Lenders typically let you access up to 80% of property value minus what you owe. That accessible equity is how many investors fund their next purchase without saving a fresh deposit. It's a straightforward concept but the number of people who have equity sitting idle, unaware they could use it, is consistently higher than you'd expect.

Have you ever used equity to purchase another property?
