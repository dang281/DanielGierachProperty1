## Caption

Not every inner east suburb moves at the same pace. Some have already run hard over the past decade. Others are still in the earlier stages of that transition, with older housing stock gradually turning over and buyer profiles shifting. Murarrie has been on more radars since the Cross River Rail conversation picked up. Seven Hills offers some of the last relatively affordable land close to the city. Coorparoo and Camp Hill and Carina carry strong fundamentals with established demand. I am curious where the longer view sits with people. Which inner east suburb do you think has the most long-term potential?
