## Caption

The argument for listing before Christmas is a smaller but more motivated buyer pool. The argument for waiting until autumn is a larger pool and more competition between buyers. Both are valid, and the right answer depends on your property and your timeline.

For vendors who own in Brisbane's inner east and have been thinking about selling, this is the question most people are sitting with right now.

If you were selling your home, would you list before Christmas 2034 or wait for autumn 2035?
