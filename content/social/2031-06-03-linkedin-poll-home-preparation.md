## Caption

Preparation before a property campaign is where most of the result is built in the inner east. The distinction that matters is between work that protects value and work that tries to add it through renovation. Fixing what is broken, cleaning what is dirty, and removing what is distracting almost always pays back. Renovation work rarely earns its full cost within the campaign result. The order in which vendors tackle preparation also matters. Getting the sequence right means a buyer walks into a property that feels considered rather than rushed. If you were preparing your home for sale, where would you start?

## Poll options

- Declutter and clean thoroughly before anything else
- Focus on street appeal, first impressions matter most
- Address any obvious maintenance or repair issues
- Get professional styling and photography sorted immediately
