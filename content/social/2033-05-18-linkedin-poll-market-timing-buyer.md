## Caption

One of the most persistent debates among buyers in Brisbane's inner east is whether to buy now or wait for a better entry point. The honest answer is that nobody times the market correctly with any consistency, including people who do this professionally. What you can do is understand your own financial position clearly, know the specific suburbs and property types you are targeting, and be ready to act when the right property comes up at a price you can justify. Waiting for the "right time" often means missing the specific property that was right for you. That said, I'm curious how buyers are actually thinking about this right now. How are you approaching your purchase timeline?
