## Caption

The debate about when to sell in Brisbane has a different answer than the same conversation in Melbourne or Sydney. The seasonal patterns are different. The buyer behaviour is different. And the conventional advice most sellers receive is built around southern markets, not Queensland's climate and lifestyle rhythms.

I have a clear view on this. But I find the conventional wisdom is sticky enough that it is worth asking the question directly.

When would you prefer to sell your home in Brisbane?

Daniel Gierach
Ray White Bulimba · Inner East Brisbane
