## Caption

Many homeowners in Brisbane's inner east are sitting in homes that no longer fit their life. The kids have left. The stairs are getting old. The garden is more maintenance than pleasure. The question is not whether to downsize, it is when.

I talk to homeowners regularly who know they should make a move but keep finding reasons to delay. Sometimes that reason is financial. Sometimes it is emotional. And sometimes it is simply that the right place to move to has not appeared yet. Each of those is a real and legitimate reason, but they each affect the timing of a sale differently.

What is stopping you from downsizing right now?

## Poll options
