## Caption

Cannon Hill has a straightforward appeal that holds up over time. Families here are usually after two things: a character home with room to grow, and a train line they can actually use. Cannon Hill station delivers on the second point, putting the CBD and inner south within easy reach without needing a second car for every commute.

The suburb sits in the Cannon Hill Anglican College catchment, which continues to draw families relocating from across the inner east and south side. That school pull is consistent regardless of where the broader market is sitting, and it keeps a steady floor of demand under properties in the area.

Character homes on full-size blocks are the core of the market here. Renovated queenslanders along the quieter residential streets have performed well in recent years, and spring conditions in October generally bring out serious buyers who have been watching through winter. Competition for well-presented homes in this price range is genuine.

For homeowners who have been thinking about timing a sale, this part of the season tends to reward properties that come to market early rather than waiting for November.
