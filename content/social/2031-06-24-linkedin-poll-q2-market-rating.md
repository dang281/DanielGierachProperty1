## Caption

Q2 2031 ran from the post-Easter period through the full winter market. April saw the tail of the autumn buyer pool, May moved into a smaller and more targeted winter group, and June brought EOFY-motivated investors alongside owner-occupiers with genuine timelines. How the quarter felt depends significantly on whether you were buying or selling, and in which suburb and price range you were active. A well-prepared property in a tightly held street had a different Q2 than a property that was overpriced or underprepared. Looking back at Q2 2031, how would you rate it?
