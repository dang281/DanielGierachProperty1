## Caption

With spring now well underway, the question for sellers who haven't launched yet shifts from "should I list in spring" to "is there enough runway left to list before Christmas?"

The Brisbane inner east market typically softens from mid-December. A campaign that launches in late October has six to eight weeks of active buyer engagement before the holiday slowdown. That is workable.

Quick question for the Brisbane property community:

If you are considering selling, would you aim to list before Christmas this year?
