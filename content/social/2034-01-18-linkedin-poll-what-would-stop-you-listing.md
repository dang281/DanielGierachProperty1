## Caption

The decision to sell is rarely just a market timing question.

In my experience, the practical and emotional barriers to listing often carry more weight than the financial ones. Not knowing where you will buy next is a genuine constraint that no clearance rate statistic resolves. Uncertainty about what the sale will achieve makes it hard to commit to a plan. And the preparation involved in presenting a family home for sale is real work that can feel overwhelming when it is still months away.

These are not excuses for inaction. They are the actual factors that shape when people list, and they are worth naming honestly.

Curious what is most common for the people in my network who are thinking about it but have not yet made a move.

What is the single biggest thing stopping you from listing your home in Q1 2034?

## Poll options

- Not sure where I would buy next
- Concerned about what I would achieve at sale
- The preparation feels overwhelming
