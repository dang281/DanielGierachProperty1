## Caption

The first week of January is when most selling decisions for the year start to take shape, even if the listing is months away. In Brisbane's inner east, autumn (late February to May) is historically the most active selling window. But not every vendor is chasing the same window.

Some sellers prefer the pre-Easter rush. Others want the market to settle into a rhythm first. A few are deliberately waiting for post-Easter stock to thin out. All of these are rational approaches depending on the property, the suburb, and the personal circumstances behind the sale.

If you are planning to sell your inner east home in 2033, when would you aim to list?
