## Caption

Carina Heights sits in one of the quieter parts of the inner east's eastern boundary, positioned between Carina and the Mount Gravatt escarpment with a residential character that is predominantly owner-occupied and family-focused. The suburb delivers the block sizes that attract families prioritising outdoor space, with a price point that sits below the more prominent inner east suburbs while sharing the same road network and access infrastructure.

The buyer profile in Carina Heights reflects that value positioning. Buyers who have researched the surrounding area and concluded that Carindale or Carina Heights offers more land and more house for their budget than the closer-in alternatives tend to pursue the suburb with a clear sense of what they are comparing it to. They are not buying the suburb name. They are buying the fundamentals.

The proximity to Westfield Carindale, one of Brisbane's major retail anchors, gives Carina Heights the everyday convenience access that buyers with families weight heavily. Good road access to the Pacific Motorway and the Gateway further rounds out the practical picture for buyers managing city and outer suburb commutes simultaneously.

The suburb's listing pool tends to be modest at any given time, which means well-positioned properties that arrive with accurate pricing and good presentation tend to find motivated buyers relatively quickly.
