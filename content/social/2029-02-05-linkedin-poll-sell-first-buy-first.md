## Caption

The sell-first-or-buy-first question comes up in nearly every conversation I have with homeowners looking to upsize or downsize in Brisbane's inner east.

Both approaches carry real risk. Selling first gives you certainty about what you can spend but leaves you exposed to time pressure when buying. Buying first locks in your next property but creates financial risk if your sale takes longer than expected or comes in below your number.

The right answer depends heavily on the gap between your current property value, your target property value, and how much bridging capacity your lender will support.

What would you do in the current market?
