## Caption

Carindale is a suburb built for family life in a way that is rare at this distance from the CBD. The lots are larger than anything you find in the older inner east suburbs, the streets are quiet and residential, and Westfield Carindale provides the kind of day-to-day amenity that buyers with children factor into suburb decisions at a practical level. School catchments covering both Carindale State School and Citipointe Christian College give families real education options without leaving the suburb's orbit.

The buyer profile is consistently upgraders. These are households moving from smaller inner east properties into a home where the land content and the room count finally match what the family actually needs. They tend to be well-researched, have already sold or are close to selling, and they move decisively when the right property appears. Competition for large, well-presented family homes is genuine at the top of the market.

Lot sizes in Carindale regularly surpass 600 square metres, which puts the suburb in a different category to the inner east: buyers here have genuine choices about what they do with their land. That appeals to buyers who intend to build a pool, add a large shed, or simply have a yard that functions for children and outdoor living.

If you own in Carindale and are thinking about your next move, the upgrader buyer pool is motivated and active right now.
