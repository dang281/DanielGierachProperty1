## Caption

Spring campaigns in Brisbane's inner east that perform well have almost always been prepared in June and July, not in August. The difference between a property that launches with strong photography, clean presentation, and a realistic price guide versus one that is rushed to market is usually visible within the first week.

If you are thinking about listing in spring 2030, when are you planning to start the preparation conversation?
