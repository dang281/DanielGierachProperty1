## Caption

APRA's 3% serviceability buffer means lenders test your ability to repay a mortgage at your actual rate plus an additional 3 percentage points. It is the main reason many buyers find their approved amount is lower than expected.

Most people have a rough idea of what they can borrow. Significantly fewer have confirmed it with an actual lender assessment in the past three months, which is the relevant timeframe when you are actively looking.

Do you know your current borrowing capacity?
