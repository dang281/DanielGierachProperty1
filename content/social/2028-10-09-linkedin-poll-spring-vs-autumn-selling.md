## Caption

The spring versus autumn debate is one of the most consistent conversations in Brisbane real estate. Both windows have genuine merit in the inner east, and the right answer depends on the property and the seller's situation far more than the season.

Spring has more buyer volume. Autumn has less competition from other sellers. Spring has good garden presentation. Autumn has the motivation of buyers who missed out over spring.

If you were selling your home in Brisbane's inner east, which season would you choose?
