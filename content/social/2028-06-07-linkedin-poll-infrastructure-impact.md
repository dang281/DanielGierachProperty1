## Caption

Infrastructure investment shapes property values in ways that take years to fully show up in the data. Cross River Rail opened new commuter connections for inner east suburbs that previously sat just outside the sweet spot for CBD access. The Olympic 2032 preparations have changed the conversation in certain pockets entirely, with buyers factoring in what those areas will look like in five years, not just today. Cycling and pedestrian networks have quietly shifted which streets and which suburbs attract a premium. Asking which of these has had the most visible impact on where people actually want to live. Which infrastructure project has changed Brisbane's inner east most?
