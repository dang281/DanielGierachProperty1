## Caption

Listing timing is one of the most debated decisions in Brisbane property. Some agents push spring hard. Others say autumn is more consistent for the inner east. A lot of vendors end up waiting until February because it feels safer.

In the inner east specifically, October and November have a buyer pool that February does not: families with a school start deadline, buyers who have already sold, and upgraders trying to settle before Christmas.

But the numbers vary by suburb, property type, and individual situation.

I am curious what people in the Brisbane market are currently planning.
