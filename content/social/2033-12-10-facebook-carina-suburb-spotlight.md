## Caption

Carina offers something that is becoming harder to find within 10km of the Brisbane CBD: larger block sizes at a price point that families can genuinely access. The suburb has retained a community character that many of the suburbs closer to the city have gradually lost as density has increased, and that is something buyers who arrive from the inner ring notice immediately.

Westfield Carindale is one of the best-serviced shopping and lifestyle precincts in the south-east, and its proximity means Carina residents have daily convenience access that rivals or exceeds what is available in suburbs at much higher price points. For families, the practical quality of life that access provides is a genuine factor in the decision to buy in this suburb.

The Mount Gravatt State High School catchment is a consistent conversation at Carina open homes. Families who have been looking specifically for catchment access at a manageable price point often find Carina delivers exactly that combination, and they tend to have clear intent when they walk through the door.

The buyer profile I see most often is families who have researched value within 10km of the CBD and concluded that Carina delivers the most for their budget without compromising on school access, lifestyle amenity, and space. They know the suburb and are ready to move when the right property comes up.

If you own in Carina and want to know what the current market looks like for your property, I am happy to talk.
