## Caption

Most sellers start thinking about choosing an agent about two weeks before they want to list. That is a tight window for what is likely the largest financial transaction of the year.

Agents vary significantly in approach: the price guidance they give, how they run open homes, the way they handle competing offers, and how often they stay in contact through a campaign. Despite those differences, most vendors end up making their final choice based on one thing.

If you were selling tomorrow, what would matter most to you?
