## Caption

Balmoral is one of the few inner east suburbs where the combination of river proximity, elevated character housing, and a genuine village atmosphere is available within 6 kilometres of the CBD. That combination is what keeps buyers returning to the suburb through every market cycle.

The streets between Laidlaw Parade and the river produce some of the strongest results in the inner east. Properties with river views or direct access to the Balmoral foreshore attract buyers from across Brisbane and interstate who have set this suburb as their target well before they start attending opens. The competition that produces at auction is real and consistent.

Balmoral State School has one of the stronger reputations in the inner east primary school landscape, and the suburb's proximity to Lourdes Hill College gives families with girls a private secondary option within walking distance.

The Balmoral commercial strip on Riding Road and the smaller café precinct closer to the foreshore give the suburb an amenity base that is proportionate to its residential character. Not overwhelming. Not underwhelming. Right for the market that lives here.

Days on market in Balmoral trend below the broader inner east average for well-positioned character homes, reflecting a buyer pool that is prepared and decisive.
