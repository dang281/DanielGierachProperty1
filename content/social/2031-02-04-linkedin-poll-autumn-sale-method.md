## Caption

The autumn campaign season is now underway in Brisbane's inner east, and the question of sale method comes up in almost every early conversation with a seller.

Auction works well in a competitive market because it creates urgency and a hard end date. Private treaty suits sellers who want more control over price and timing. Expression of interest sits somewhere between the two. And a good agent should be able to make a case for the method based on the property, the street, and who is likely to buy it.

If you were listing your inner east home this autumn, which sale method would you choose?
