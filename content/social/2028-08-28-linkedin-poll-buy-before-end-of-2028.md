## Caption

The back half of the year is traditionally when sellers who have been thinking about listing make their final decision. The spring market opens in September, the pre-Christmas window runs through November, and then the market goes quiet until February.

If you are considering a move before the end of the year, the decision point is really now. A September or October campaign needs August preparation. A November campaign needs September preparation. The timeline runs backwards from the campaign date, and the preparation is not something you can compress without affecting the result.

For buyers, the same logic applies in reverse. Pre-approval expires and the best opportunities often surface in windows when other buyers have not yet activated.

Where are you at right now?
