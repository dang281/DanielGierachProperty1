## Caption

Winter tends to reduce the noise in the Brisbane inner east property market without reducing the underlying demand for the right property. The buyers who remain active in July and August are typically the most motivated and the most prepared. Stock is lower, competition at individual properties can still be strong when the listing is well-positioned, and the vendor pool is smaller. If you are currently looking to buy in Brisbane's inner east, how would you describe the market right now?

## Poll options

- Good conditions, less competition than spring
- Frustrating, not enough to look at
- Watching and waiting until spring stock arrives
- Actively inspecting and ready to offer on the right property
