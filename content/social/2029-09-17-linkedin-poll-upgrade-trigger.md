## Caption

A large proportion of inner east property transactions come from upsizers. People who bought well five or ten years ago, have built solid equity, and are now weighing up whether the timing is right to move. The trigger is rarely just one thing. Sometimes it is a growing family pushing the walls. Sometimes equity has reached the point where the jump is genuinely manageable. Sometimes it is simply that the right property has come up. And sometimes it comes down to confidence that the market is not about to drop the day after you sign contracts. What would most influence you to upgrade your property in the next 12 months?

## Poll options

- Change in family circumstances
- Significant equity built up
- Found the right property to move to
- Confidence that the market timing is right
