## Caption

Brisbane's inner east historically sees its strongest campaign activity in Q1 and Q2, from late January through to May. The choice of quarter is not just a calendar preference. It affects how long a vendor needs to prepare, how many competing listings will be on the market at the same time, and the profile of the buyer pool available to the campaign. Vendors who are thinking about selling in 2034 are already making a quarter decision, even if they have not named it as such. Which quarter are you targeting?

## Poll options

- Q1 (January to March): the post-summer window with motivated buyers
- Q2 (April to June): Brisbane's consistently strong autumn season
- Q3 (July to September): the winter campaign with fewer competing listings
- Q4 (October to December): spring peak or pre-Christmas push
