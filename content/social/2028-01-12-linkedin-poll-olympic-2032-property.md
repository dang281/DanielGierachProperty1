## Caption

We are now four years out from the Brisbane 2032 Olympic and Paralympic Games. The infrastructure investment across transport, precinct development, and accommodation is well underway. The inner east, including suburbs like Woolloongabba, Norman Park, and East Brisbane, sits directly in the infrastructure corridor.

The question I get asked regularly is whether the Games are already priced into Brisbane property or whether the uplift is still ahead.

I am curious what people in my network think.

Has the 2032 Olympic countdown changed how you think about Brisbane property?
