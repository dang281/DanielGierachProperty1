## Caption

Buyers in Brisbane's inner east are comparing multiple properties on most weekends right now. The autumn season compresses a lot of stock into a relatively short window, which means decisions often come down to a single factor that tips one property over another.

Sometimes it is price. Sometimes it is the feeling you get walking through the door. Sometimes it is something as specific as which school zone the property falls in. The deciding factor often reveals more about what the buyer actually needs than they have openly stated.

If you were comparing two properties this autumn, what would tip your decision?
