## Caption

Mt Gravatt East occupies a practical and underappreciated position in the inner east. It sits on the eastern slope below Griffith University, close to the major retail and medical services along Logan Road and Cavendish Road, and within reach of the Gateway Motorway for buyers who need regional access.

The streets on the northern face of the suburb, running down from the Griffith University precinct toward Cavendish Road, offer elevated outlooks and a residential character that buyers from Coorparoo and Greenslopes often find appealing at a more accessible price point. The family homes in these streets on generous blocks have consistently attracted buyers who are willing to put work into a property in exchange for the land.

Autumn campaign season suits Mt Gravatt East well. Buyers who have been searching in adjacent suburbs since January are often revisiting their search area by March, and properties here that are priced with discipline and presented properly tend to attract genuine competition.

The school catchment covers Mt Gravatt East State School and there is ready access to Seville Road State School and the independent school options further along Logan Road. Families with school-age children weigh this heavily.

If you own in Mt Gravatt East and have been considering the timing of a sale, March and early April is the window to move.
