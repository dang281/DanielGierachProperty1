## Caption

We are in the final weeks of the pre-Games period and the inner east open home circuit has a particular energy right now. Some buyers describe it as intense. Others say the competition has thinned as people step back to wait for August. The real picture, from what I am seeing on the ground, is more nuanced than either description.

Competition varies a lot by price point and property type. At certain price ranges, the pool of genuine buyers is deep and responsive. At others, it has compressed. Knowing which bucket your target property sits in matters if you are trying to read whether to act now or wait.

I am curious how buyers in the market right now are experiencing it. How would you describe buyer competition at inner east open homes at the moment?
