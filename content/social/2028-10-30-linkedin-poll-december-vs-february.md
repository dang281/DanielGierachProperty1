## Caption

There is a genuine window in late November and early December for sellers who want to be under contract before the market quietens for summer. It is not as long as the October and November window, but the buyers who are still active in December are motivated and specific.

The February window has the advantage of a fresh start to the year, more buyer volume once school begins, and the lead-up to the autumn market. It also has more competing sellers and a buyer pool that has not been warmed up by a spring of open homes.

Both can work. The question is which fits your situation better.

If you were a homeowner in Brisbane's inner east ready to sell, which timing would you choose?
