## Caption

Mt Gravatt East has a school catchment profile that drives meaningful buyer enquiry well beyond the suburb's immediate boundaries. Clairvaux MacKillop College and Seton College both pull families who want quality Catholic secondary education and proximity to established residential streets. For a significant portion of the buyer pool, the school decision comes before the suburb decision, and Mt Gravatt East is where they land.

The Logan Road dining and retail precinct is close enough that residents access it regularly, and bus connections give the suburb genuine non-car accessibility that many comparable family suburbs to the east and south cannot match. Buyers who work in the CBD and have older children in secondary school find this suburb ticks both boxes in a way that is genuinely useful day to day.

The housing stock in Mt Gravatt East is established and mixed, with opportunities for renovation sitting alongside homes that have already been updated. The upgrade buyer activity here is consistent. Families who bought in to secure the school catchment five or eight years ago are now looking at their next move, and their original purchases have performed well enough to fund meaningful upgrades.

Well-presented homes in good condition with clear school catchment access consistently attract the most motivated buyer competition at inspection. Pricing and presentation are the two variables that separate good outcomes from excellent ones.
