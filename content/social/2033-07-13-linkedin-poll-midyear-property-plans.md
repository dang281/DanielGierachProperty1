## Caption

Mid-year is a useful point to check in on where property plans actually sit versus where they were at the start of 2033. The market conditions that shaped decisions in January have shifted in some ways: the RBA has moved, listing volumes have adjusted, and the inner east has seen a busy autumn followed by the expected winter compression. Some vendors who planned to list in autumn held back. Some buyers who were active in Q1 have found their target property. Now at the midpoint: where do things stand for you?
