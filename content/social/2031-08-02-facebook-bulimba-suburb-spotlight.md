## Caption

August in Bulimba marks the turn. The mid-winter quietness of July gives way to vendors thinking seriously about spring, and buyers who have been watching for months start moving from consideration to action. If you own in Bulimba and have been weighing your timing, the next six weeks are worth planning carefully.

The fundamentals of Bulimba remain as strong as they have ever been. Oxford Street delivers the kind of daily lifestyle that draws buyers from well outside the suburb, and the ferry at the Bulimba terminal connects residents to the CBD and New Farm without needing a car. For the demographic that values that kind of urban connectivity, Bulimba sits at the top of the inner east hierarchy.

Heritage character architecture and tight supply of detached houses are the two forces that keep Bulimba values elevated. New stock rarely comes to market without significant competition, and upsizer demand from buyers within the inner east who have built equity continues to push into this suburb. Those conditions do not shift quickly.

August buyers are often the best buyers. They are motivated, they are not distracted by a market crowded with spring listings, and they have typically been looking long enough to recognise value and act. If your property is in good shape, this window has merit.
