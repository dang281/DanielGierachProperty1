## Caption

Holland Park has a character that comes from longevity. Established leafy streets, strong owner-occupier retention, and low turnover create a suburb that feels settled in a way that buyers who have been looking across the south side recognise and respond to. It is the kind of suburb where residents stay for decades rather than years, and that stability shapes the community character in a way that is hard to replicate in areas with higher churn.

Mount Gravatt Outlook provides elevated parkland and walking access within the suburb itself, which is a green space amenity that residents use rather than look at on a map.
