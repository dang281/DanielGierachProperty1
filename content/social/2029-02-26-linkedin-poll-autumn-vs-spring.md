## Caption

Brisbane's inner east has two strong selling periods: autumn (February to May) and spring (September to November). The property media tends to favour spring, but the data for Brisbane's inner east does not always back that up.

Autumn in this part of Brisbane tends to produce focused, motivated buyers. Spring brings higher volume, but more listings competing for the same pool.

If you sold your home in Brisbane's inner east, which season would you choose?
