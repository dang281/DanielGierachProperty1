## Caption

February is when sellers who have been considering a 2027 campaign need to make a decision.

Not because February is the best month to list. In the inner east, March and April typically produce the strongest results. But February is when the preparation for a March or April campaign needs to begin.

Here is the sequence. A property that lists in late March needs its photography done two weeks before. The styling and any minor repairs need to be finished before the photographer arrives. The conveyancer needs four to six weeks to prepare the contract and disclosure documents. The agent needs time to brief the registered buyer database before the property goes live publicly.

Work backwards from a late March launch and you land somewhere in early February for the initial conversation.

Sellers who come to me in the first week of February have time to do this well. Sellers who come to me in the third week of February are already in catch-up mode.

The autumn window in Brisbane's inner east is genuine and consistent. Year after year, the period from mid-March to late April produces the highest number of strong auction results in Bulimba, Hawthorne, Norman Park, Camp Hill, and the surrounding suburbs. It is driven by serious buyers who have been waiting through the post-Christmas period and are ready to act.

If you are planning to sell this year, the window you want is closing faster than you think.
