## Caption

In Brisbane's inner east, school catchment boundaries can move the price of a property by $100,000 or more compared with a comparable home on the other side of the line.

Whites Hill State College, Bulimba State School, and Coorparoo State School are among the catchments that attract a consistent premium. Buyers who have done the research know exactly where those boundaries sit. Some will only inspect inside them.

It is one of the more quantifiable premiums in the inner east market, and it affects both resale value and the buyer pool you attract when you eventually sell.

When buying in Brisbane's inner east, how much does school catchment influence what you would pay?
