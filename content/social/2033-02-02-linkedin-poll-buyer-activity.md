## Caption

From where I sit, the picture is forming. But buyer activity is also something felt from the other side of the transaction: by vendors waiting on feedback, by buyers who attended multiple open homes on the same day, and by people observing the market without yet being active in it.

What I hear from the field and what the broader community perceives can be different things. Both are useful.
