## Caption

Winter open homes tend to be quieter in attendance but not necessarily in buyer quality. The people who turn up in July are serious. They are not browsing. They are buying.

That said, most sellers still hold out for spring, which concentrates competition exactly when the market is most crowded.

Quick question for the Brisbane property community:

For sellers weighing a winter listing, which factor shapes your thinking more?
