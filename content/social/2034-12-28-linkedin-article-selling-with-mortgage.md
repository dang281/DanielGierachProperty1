## Caption

Net equity is not sale price minus loan balance. Fixed-rate break costs, discharge fees, and the overlap period between properties can each change the real number by thousands.

Fixed-rate borrowers need to request a break cost estimate from their lender before launching a campaign. This figure can be negligible or significant depending on where rates have moved since the loan was drawn.

The discharge of mortgage must be arranged by the vendor's solicitor in advance of settlement. Late discharge requests are a common cause of settlement delays.

Bridging finance can cover the gap between buying and selling. The cost of bridging finance and the carrying cost of two properties is a calculation that should be completed before any purchase is made.
