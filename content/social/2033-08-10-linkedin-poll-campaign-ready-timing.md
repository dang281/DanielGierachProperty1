## Caption

One of the most consistent underestimates I see from vendors who are planning a spring campaign is how long the preparation actually takes. Most people think in terms of a week or two for a clean and tidy. The reality for a property that is going to photograph well and present strongly at open homes is usually closer to four to six weeks when you account for trades, landscaping, any painting, and the styling or decluttering process. Understanding where sellers are with this estimate helps me understand the realistic timeline for a September or October launch. How long do you think your home would need from the decision to sell to being campaign-ready?

## Poll options

- One to two weeks (it is already in good shape)
- Three to four weeks (some work needed)
- Five to eight weeks (meaningful preparation required)
- More than eight weeks (significant work ahead)
