## Caption

Timing decisions in property are made under uncertainty and only assessed in hindsight. Most vendors I work with have a view on whether they sold at the right time, regardless of how the sale actually went. The regret question is genuinely interesting. Looking back at any property you have sold, did you feel the timing was right?
