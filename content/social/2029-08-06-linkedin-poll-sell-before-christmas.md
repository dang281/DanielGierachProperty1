## Caption

There is a window each year where sellers who move before Christmas get the full benefit of spring buyer competition, motivated purchasers working to deadlines, and a market that has not yet been saturated with new listings. That window closes faster than most people expect. But the decision to sell before Christmas is rarely just about market conditions. It usually comes down to one of a handful of triggers that make the timing feel right or necessary. I am curious what would tip the scales for people sitting on the fence right now. What would make you decide to sell your home before Christmas this year?

## Poll options

- Right offer from a known buyer
- Market feels like it has peaked
- Life circumstances requiring a move
- Finding the right property to buy next
