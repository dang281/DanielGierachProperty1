## Caption

Mt Gravatt's spring market is one that rewards vendors who understand the suburb's practical appeal. The buyers who pursue Mt Gravatt in September and October are typically buyers who have done the inner east comparison work thoroughly and reached Mt Gravatt with a clear preference: they want more house, more land, and more storage than the suburbs closer to the river allow at comparable prices. They have resolved the commute question and are ready to act.

The Garden City shopping centre provides a retail and services density that few suburban addresses in Brisbane can match. For families managing household complexity, having a major shopping centre, medical precinct, and specialty retail cluster within five minutes is a quality-of-life advantage that buyers price into their decision. The Garden City precinct is as much a reason people choose Mt Gravatt as the school catchment or the block size.

The elevation across much of Mt Gravatt produces outlooks and breezes that buyers from the lower-lying suburbs often note with genuine surprise. Properties on the elevated sections of the suburb with aspects toward the ranges or city produce a visual experience that is not what buyers expect from an address this far from the river.

Vendors preparing a spring campaign in Mt Gravatt should target a September launch. The buyer pool at this time of year is broad, well-funded, and active.
