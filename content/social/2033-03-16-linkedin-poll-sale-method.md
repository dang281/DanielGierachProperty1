## Caption

Method of sale is one of the most debated decisions in an autumn campaign. Auction creates competition and a fixed timeline. Private treaty gives buyers more time and flexibility. Expressions of interest sits somewhere between the two, collecting offers in a structured but less public way.

The right answer depends on the property, the likely buyer pool, and the current clearance environment in that specific suburb. There is no universal best method, despite what some agents will tell you. What works consistently in Hawthorne for a character Queenslander may not work as well for a townhouse in Mt Gravatt East.

Opinions vary, and that is worth understanding. If you had to choose a sale method for an autumn 2033 campaign, what would it be?
