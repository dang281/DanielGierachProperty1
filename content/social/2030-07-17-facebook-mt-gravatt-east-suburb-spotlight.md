## Caption

Mt Gravatt East sits at the intersection of two things Brisbane family buyers consistently prioritise: school access and lifestyle proximity. Clairvaux MacKillop College and Seton College are both within the suburb, giving Catholic school buyers a choice that is relatively rare without a long commute. Add to that the Logan Road retail and dining precinct running through the area and solid bus connections into the CBD, and the everyday liveability picture is strong.

The housing stock is a mix of established homes across various eras, with enough variety to attract both buyers looking for something move-in ready and those seeking renovation scope. Upgrade buyer activity has been increasing, particularly from buyers who have outgrown a smaller inner east property and want more space without moving further out.

What Mt Gravatt East offers sellers is a consistent, motivated buyer pool. The school catchments create a degree of urgency in family buyers: once they have decided on a school for their children, they need to be in the right zone, and that decision timeline is not flexible. That dynamic tends to produce purposeful buyers who are ready to commit when they find the right property.

If you own in Mt Gravatt East and are considering your options, a current appraisal is the right starting point.
