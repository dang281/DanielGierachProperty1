## Caption

Most people with a property goal for 2027 have already started thinking about it, even if they have not committed to a plan yet.

The decision to buy, sell, upsize, or downsize rarely happens in January. It builds through the second half of the previous year. The people who list in February are typically the ones who started their preparation in November or December.

If you are thinking about a property move in 2027, I am curious what your goal is.
