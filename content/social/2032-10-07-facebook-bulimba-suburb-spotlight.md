## Caption

Oxford Street is what most people picture when they think of Bulimba, and it is a fair summary. The combination of a walkable village strip, river proximity, and quality school zoning has made this suburb consistently attractive to young professional families who want inner-city convenience without sacrificing a proper backyard.

Bulimba State School zoning keeps family buyers focused here across every season. The supply of character homes is genuinely tight, and that is not a recent development. Owners who have held for a decade or more are sitting on significant land value, particularly on the blocks that run toward the river.

What makes selling in spring interesting in Bulimba is that buyer competition tends to arrive before the listings do. Purchasers who missed out through winter often come into October with clear briefs and finance in place. A well-prepared property that launches early in the spring window has less competition from other sellers and more attention from buyers who are ready to act.

If you are a Bulimba homeowner who has been thinking about the right time to have a market conversation, I am available for a straightforward appraisal discussion.
