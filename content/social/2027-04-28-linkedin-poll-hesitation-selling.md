## Caption

Most homeowners who are thinking about selling are not stopped by a disinterest in moving. They are stopped by something specific.

In my experience, the hesitation almost always comes down to one of four things: not being able to find the right next property, uncertainty about whether this is the right time in the cycle, the process feeling complicated or overwhelming, or simply not knowing what the home would actually sell for.

Each of these has a different answer. And each one is worth a proper conversation rather than a guess.

What is your biggest hesitation about selling your home?

## Poll options

- Finding the right property to buy next
- Not knowing if the timing is right
- The selling process feels complicated
- I am not sure what my home is actually worth
