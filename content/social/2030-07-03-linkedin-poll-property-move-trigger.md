## Caption

Most people who call me about selling are not acting on market conditions. They are acting on something that happened in their life. A new baby that made a two-bedroom feel like a problem. A school enrolment that required a different suburb. A relationship change. A job.

Market timing matters at the margin, but the trigger almost never comes from a headline. It comes from a life event that makes the current property the wrong fit.

I am curious where my network sits on this. If you were making a property move in the next twelve months, which of these would most likely set it in motion?

## Poll options

- A change in family size or life stage
- Finding the right property
- Market conditions or price movement
- A financial trigger (equity, rate change, EOFY)
