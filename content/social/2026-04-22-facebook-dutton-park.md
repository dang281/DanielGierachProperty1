## Caption

Dutton Park is one of the Brisbane suburbs where infrastructure investment is doing some of the heavy lifting for homeowners right now.

The suburb sits 4km south of the CBD, bordered by the University of Queensland and the PA Hospital precinct. The median house price is currently around $1,525,000, with annual growth of approximately 10.5% over the past year. But the bigger story is what's happening with Cross River Rail, which includes a station at Boggo Road, just a short walk from Dutton Park's residential streets. Direct CBD access without a car changes the buyer calculus significantly.

Dutton Park has always attracted buyers who want to be close to everything but still live on a quiet residential street. The mix of Queenslander character homes and newer builds gives buyers options at different price points, and proximity to UQ keeps rental demand consistent for investors.
