## Caption

One of the most common conversations in the weeks before a property sells is about what comes next. For some vendors, the suburb stays the same and the only question is what size or style to step into. For others, the sale is a genuine reset. The answers tend to cluster in ways that reveal a lot about where the inner east market is heading. Worth asking directly.

## Poll options

- Stay in the same suburb or immediate area
- Upgrade to a more expensive inner east suburb
- Move further out for more land or space
- Leave Brisbane entirely
