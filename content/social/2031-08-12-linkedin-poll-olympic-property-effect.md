## Caption

The Brisbane 2032 Olympics are approximately one year away. Cross River Rail has opened, stadium precincts have been upgraded, and international attention on Brisbane has grown. Whether the Games have specifically moved inner east residential property prices is a question that even professional valuers find difficult to answer cleanly, because the Olympic effect has run alongside population growth, interstate migration, and a persistent undersupply of inner east housing. What is your read on how much of the recent price movement in the inner east is attributable to the Olympics?
