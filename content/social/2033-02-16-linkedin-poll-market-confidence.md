## Caption

Twelve months ago, many people watching Brisbane's inner east property market were still calibrating their expectations around the Olympic year. The Games were still months away. Some were expecting a price surge. Some were wary of a post-event correction. Others had simply stopped trying to predict and were watching the data instead.

A year later, we are in a different place. The Olympics are done. 2033 is the first full post-Olympic year. The market has had time to settle into something closer to its underlying shape.

Whether your confidence has shifted probably depends on what you expected and what you have observed since. Both are reasonable bases for an opinion.
