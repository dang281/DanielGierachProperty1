## Caption

2032 is shaping up to be an unusual year for Brisbane property. The Olympics will bring a wave of international attention to the city that no other event could generate. The inner east market has run strongly for several years. Interest rates have been moving. And the cost of entry into the Brisbane market at most inner east price points has shifted significantly from where it was five years ago.

Against that backdrop, people are making genuinely different decisions about what they want to do with their property position in 2032. Some are buying. Some are selling into the attention. Some are paying down debt and not making any major moves. Some are waiting to see how the Olympic year actually plays out before deciding.

Worth asking directly. 2032 is the year everyone has been talking about for a decade in Brisbane property circles. Now that it is actually arriving, what are people planning to do with it?

What is your primary property goal for 2032?

## Poll options

- Buy a first home or investment property
- Upgrade or upsize from my current home
- Sell and use the equity for something else
- Pay down existing debt and build equity through the year
