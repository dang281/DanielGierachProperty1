## Caption

Fast sales get attributed to all kinds of things, and depending on who you ask the answer is usually whatever that person had most to do with. Agents credit their database. Vendors credit the styling. Buyers say they just knew as soon as they walked in. The real answer is usually a combination of factors, but I suspect one matters more than the rest. I have a clear view on this from watching hundreds of campaigns in Brisbane's inner east, and I am curious whether it matches what buyers and vendors think. What single factor do you think most influences how quickly a home sells?
