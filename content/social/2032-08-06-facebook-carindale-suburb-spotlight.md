## Caption

Carindale's defining feature is walkable access to one of South East Queensland's largest retail and dining precincts, and for households who value that kind of daily convenience, it is a feature that the inner-east suburbs to the north cannot match.

Westfield Carindale shapes daily life for residents in a way that a smaller local strip does not. Full-service retail, dining, entertainment, and services within a short walk or a two-minute drive from most of the suburb's residential streets. For families with multiple demands on their time, that is a practical asset.

The residential streets carry post-war brick and tile homes on substantial blocks. The Belmont Hills precinct in the suburb's south offers elevated terrain with views toward the ranges, and that section of Carindale draws buyers who want the space and the aspect in the same parcel.

Gateway Baptist College alongside nearby primary options gives families genuine school choice within or adjacent to the suburb. Gateway Motorway access makes Carindale practical for households with work, family, and lifestyle connections across southern Queensland.

Buyers arrive here from the inner east looking for more home and more land than the premium postcodes deliver at the same price. They find it in Carindale, and they tend to stay.

For owners in Carindale, the suburb's retail access, land sizes, and school options are a strong combined package in the current market.
