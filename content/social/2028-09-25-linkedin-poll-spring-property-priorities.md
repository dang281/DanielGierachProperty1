## Caption

Spring brings more stock to market in the inner east, which means buyers who have been watching a limited winter selection suddenly have more to look at. The question is whether the spring listings are actually better properties or just more of them.

In my experience, the best inner east properties still move quickly in spring, often in the first open home weekend. The secondary stock, the ones that needed more preparation or are priced at the top of their comparable range, tends to sit through October and negotiate in November.

If you are actively looking this spring, what is your primary filter?
