## Caption

Ask ten people what is actually driving property prices in Brisbane's inner east right now and you will get ten different answers. Some point to the sheer lack of available stock. Others cite population growth and interstate migration as the real engine. A growing number are convinced the Olympic 2032 infrastructure spend is the dominant force. And there is always a camp that says it begins and ends with interest rate direction. All of them can cite evidence. None of them fully agree. Rather than speculate, I thought I would ask directly. What do you think is the single biggest driver of inner east property prices right now?
