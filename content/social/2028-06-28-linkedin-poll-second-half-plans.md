## Caption

Mid-year is a natural checkpoint. The financial year has just turned, winter is here, and the decisions people make in the next few weeks tend to shape what they are doing by September. Spring campaigns are being planned now, not in August. Buyers who are finance-ready are deciding whether to push through the quieter winter market or hold on for more stock. Vendors are weighing whether to list while competition is lower or wait for the traditional spring lift in buyer numbers. Some people have a clear plan. Others are still doing the maths. I would like to know where this audience actually sits. What is your property plan for the second half of 2028?
