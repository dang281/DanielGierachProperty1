## Caption

Buyers weighing up two properties at similar price points almost always encounter this trade-off. Natural light is regularly described as non-negotiable. A well-considered floorplan is what makes a home function over years of daily use. In practice, most buyers find that the two do not always come together in the same property at the same price. The aspect that fills a room with morning light does not always pair with a layout that keeps bedrooms separate from living. When you cannot have both, which takes priority?

## Poll options

- Natural light (aspect, windows, openness)
- Floorplan and layout (how rooms connect and flow)
- Equally important, I would not compromise either
