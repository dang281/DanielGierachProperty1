## Caption

Pre-war Queenslanders and character homes are the dominant housing stock in Balmoral, and that is exactly what a large portion of the inner east buyer market has been searching for. The buyers who come to Balmoral open homes are typically not comparing the suburb with a list of alternatives. They have specifically targeted this precinct because the character and authenticity of the housing stock here is not something they have found at the same quality elsewhere at a comparable price point.

That specificity in the buyer profile is one of the things that makes Balmoral's market perform consistently. Buyers who have been looking for months for a character home in this precinct arrive at open homes with clear intent. They know the comparable sales evidence. They have thought about their budget and their ceiling. When the right property comes up, the hesitation is low.

Balmoral State School and Balmoral State High School catchments are a consistent draw for family buyers. Both a primary and secondary catchment sit within the suburb, so families can make a long-term decision without having to factor in a catchment change at the secondary level.

Wynnum Road forms the northern boundary of the suburb. Properties in the southern and elevated streets away from that boundary tend to attract the strongest demand and the most competitive open homes.

If you own in Balmoral and want to understand what your property is worth in the current market, I am happy to provide a detailed appraisal.
