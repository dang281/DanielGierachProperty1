## Caption

November is the decision point for inner east homeowners considering a sale. The pre-Christmas buyer pool is active right now, motivated buyers want year-end settlement, and competition among them is genuine. Autumn 2033, February to April, is Brisbane's most consistent selling window, when the heat breaks and buyers return with fresh budgets and renewed urgency. Both windows have real merit. The right answer depends on individual circumstances more than market conditions alone. What would you do?
