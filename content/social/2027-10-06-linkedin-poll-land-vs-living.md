## Caption

When buyers are weighing up houses at similar price points in Brisbane's inner east, the trade-off often comes down to land versus living space. A larger block with a modest house offers future potential but less immediate liveability. A smaller block with a well-built, larger house offers comfort now but less optionality later. Both are defensible positions, and the right answer usually depends on the buyer's circumstances, timeline, and appetite for a project.
