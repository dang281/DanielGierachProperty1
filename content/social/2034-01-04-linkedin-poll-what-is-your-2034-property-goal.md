## Caption

A new year tends to bring property intentions into focus.

In Brisbane's inner east, I see four fairly distinct groups entering January with different objectives. First-home buyers who have been saving and watching, and feel the time is close. Owner-occupiers who have outgrown their current home and are weighing up the next move. Investors looking to add to a portfolio while the numbers still work. And existing property owners who have decided to build equity first before making any move.

Each of those groups is navigating a different set of decisions, timelines, and pressures. Curious which one is most common in my network right now.

What is your primary property goal for 2034?

## Poll options

- Buy my first home
- Upgrade or upsize my current home
- Add an investment property to my portfolio
- Pay down existing debt and build equity
