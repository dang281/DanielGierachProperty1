## Caption

The Brisbane Olympics start in late July. For property owners in the inner east who are thinking about selling, that date is becoming a real planning point.

Some sellers want to be fully settled before the Games begin, preferring certainty over whatever the August market looks like. Others are holding off to see what the Games period does for demand and price attention. Neither position is obviously wrong. It depends on your circumstances, your property type, and your read on what comes next.

I'm genuinely curious where people's thinking sits right now. If the Olympics start in late July, would you prefer to have settled your property sale before or after the Games?
