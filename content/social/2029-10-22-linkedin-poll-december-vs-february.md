## Caption

One of the most common decisions vendors face in October is whether to run a pre-Christmas campaign or wait for the February autumn opening. Both have genuine merit depending on the property and the vendor's situation. A pre-Christmas campaign puts you in front of the deepest spring buyer pool, but the campaign window is short. February offers a full autumn run, but supply typically increases after the new year as other vendors who held off also come to market. Neither option is automatically better. The right answer usually depends on how prepared you are and what you're buying next. If you were selling your home, which would you choose?
