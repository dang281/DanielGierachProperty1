## Caption

Spring is the most active property season in Brisbane's inner east. Open home numbers are up, buyer enquiry is stronger, and auction clearance rates tend to track their seasonal highs through October and November.

For homeowners who have been thinking about selling, this is the window most agents will tell you to aim for. But the decision is rarely as simple as picking a season. Finance, personal timing, the state of the property, and confidence in the current market all play a role.
