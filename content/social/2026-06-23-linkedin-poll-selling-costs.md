## Caption

Selling costs are everything a seller pays to take a property to market and through to settlement: agent commission, marketing campaign, conveyancing, presentation and styling, and the smaller line items like council search fees and bank discharge costs.

For inner-east Brisbane in 2026, agent commission typically runs 1.8 to 2.5 per cent plus GST, marketing for a standard four-week campaign sits between $3,000 and $8,000, and conveyancing falls in the $1,500 to $2,500 range. Presentation costs vary widely depending on the property.

If you have sold a Brisbane home in the past few years, what did total selling costs come to as a rough percentage of the final sale price? Vote your gut answer.
