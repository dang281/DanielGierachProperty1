## Caption

Buying without attending an inspection in person has become more common in Brisbane's inner east since COVID accelerated interstate migration and normalised video inspections. Buyers from Sydney and Melbourne have made offers, signed contracts, and settled on properties they have never physically walked through, relying on video walkthroughs, building reports, and buyer's agents on the ground. Whether that approach is sensible or reckless tends to divide opinion sharply. The answer probably depends on how much groundwork the buyer has done and how well they know the market.
