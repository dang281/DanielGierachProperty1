## Caption

Vendors who are considering selling in 2033 but haven't listed yet are facing a specific decision right now. May is the last realistic window to run a full campaign before the June financial year end starts affecting buyer and vendor behaviour. After that, the July to August period is quieter but not without opportunity. Then spring resets everything. Each window has different buyer depth, different competition from other listings, and different negotiating conditions. For vendors sitting on this decision, I'm curious what is actually holding things up. What is the main reason you haven't listed yet if you've been thinking about selling?
