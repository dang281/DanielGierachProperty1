## Caption

We are now in June. The Games open in less than eight weeks. The property market in Brisbane has been running at its own pace through all of the build-up, and people are at very different stages of whatever decision they have been sitting with.

Some sellers launched campaigns in March and are already settled. Some buyers bought in April and are waiting on their settlement date. Others are still weighing it up, watching the market, watching the rates, watching the news. And some people have made a deliberate decision to wait until the Games are done.

There is no wrong answer. But I am curious where most people actually land right now.

With only weeks until the Games open, what best describes your current property situation?
