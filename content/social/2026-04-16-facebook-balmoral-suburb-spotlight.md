## Caption

Balmoral is one of those suburbs where the setting does a lot of the selling.

Nine kilometres from the CBD, on the river, with established tree cover and a quiet residential feel that buyers who have looked around the inner east specifically come looking for. It attracts a buyer who has done the research and ruled out the busier alternatives.

Property here tends to trade at a premium relative to comparable floorplans in surrounding suburbs. The river proximity, the school options, and the character streetscapes create a price floor that holds even when conditions soften elsewhere.

If you own in Balmoral and have been wondering what the current market looks like for your home, I am happy to sit down and give you a clear picture.
