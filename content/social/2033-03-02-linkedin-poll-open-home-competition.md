## Caption

The peak of the 2033 autumn campaign season has arrived. Properties that launched in late February are now through their first two or three open homes. Vendors are getting feedback. Buyers who have been attending multiple properties are forming views on where value sits.

The level of competition at open homes is one of the most reliable indicators of how the season is tracking. Multiple offers on a well-priced property signal genuine buyer competition. A single offer that takes time to arrive signals a buyer pool that is present but cautious.

The inner east covers a range of price points and property types. Experience at an open home in Cannon Hill may be quite different from one in Hawthorne. But the overall pattern is useful.

Autumn campaign season is at its peak. How would you describe competition at inner east open homes?
