## Caption

Spring is active in Brisbane's inner east. Buyers are out, open homes are full, and the question for anyone considering selling is not just whether to list but how. Method of sale shapes buyer behaviour and campaign structure before a single inspection is held. Auction creates urgency and transparency: buyers know they are competing, and a reserve sets the floor. Private treaty gives buyers the comfort of conditions and time. Expression of interest suits properties where the value range between buyers is genuinely wide. The right method depends on your property type, your timeline, and the depth of buyer demand at the time you list. Which would you choose?
