## Caption

Inner east property in Brisbane has rewarded patient holders across multiple market cycles. The costs of transacting in Queensland, including transfer duty on the way in and agent commission and selling costs on the way out, mean that shorter hold periods need meaningful capital growth just to break even. At what point a hold period justifies those entry and exit costs depends on the purchase price, growth rate, and personal circumstances. But it is worth having a view before you buy rather than after. How long would you plan to hold?
