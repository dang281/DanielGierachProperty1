## Caption

Brisbane's inner east ended 2028 with clearance rates consistently above 70% and median house prices that have moved considerably over the past two years. Heading into 2029, the question most homeowners and investors are sitting with is: where from here?

The Brisbane 2032 Olympics infrastructure pipeline is well advanced. Cross River Rail is operational. Borrowing conditions have stabilised. But affordability pressure is real, and the pool of buyers who can reach $1.5M in Hawthorne or Balmoral is not unlimited.

How are you feeling about Brisbane property as we start 2029?
