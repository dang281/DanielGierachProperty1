## Caption

Spring campaigns in Brisbane's inner east typically run from September through to November. Add 30 to 60 days for settlement after contracts exchange, and you are looking at a real money-in-the-bank date of somewhere between late November and February. That timeline matters more than most sellers realise when they first start planning. Where you want to land at the end shapes when you should be hitting the market. So if you were listing this spring, cast your vote on where you would want to end up.
