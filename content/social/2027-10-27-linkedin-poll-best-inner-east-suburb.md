## Caption

Brisbane's inner east stretches from East Brisbane along the river through Bulimba, Hawthorne, and Balmoral, and inland through Camp Hill, Coorparoo, Norman Park, Morningside, and Cannon Hill. Each suburb has its own character, price point, buyer demographic, and lifestyle appeal. Preferences are genuinely divided, and the arguments for each are real. The river suburbs appeal to one buyer. The character streets of Norman Park appeal to another. The value proposition of Morningside appeals to someone else entirely. Worth asking.

## Poll options

- Bulimba or Hawthorne (river lifestyle and Oxford Street)
- Balmoral or Norman Park (character and community)
- Camp Hill or Coorparoo (affordability and central location)
- Morningside or Cannon Hill (growth and value)
