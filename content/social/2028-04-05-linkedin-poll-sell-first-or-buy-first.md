## Caption

One of the most common decisions facing inner east homeowners considering a move is whether to sell first or buy first. Selling first gives you certainty on your budget and removes the financial risk of bridging finance. But it can mean selling without knowing where you are going next.

The advice varies depending on the market, your financial position, and how long your settlement period is. There is no single right answer. In the inner east, I am regularly asked which approach is the safer path.

If you were selling your family home in Brisbane's inner east, what would you do?
