## Caption

Late November has a distinct buyer profile and it is worth understanding if you are a vendor thinking about timing.

Buyers who are still active in late November have been searching since May or June. They have seen somewhere between 20 and 40 properties. They understand the market well enough to know a correctly-priced property when they see one. They are not here to explore. They are here because they want to be settled before December and they have been trying to make that happen for months.

That combination, deep market knowledge and genuine urgency, makes the late November buyer one of the most decisive of the year. They are not going to ask for three weeks to think about it. They are not going to submit an offer and then go quiet. They want to get a deal done.

The window is narrow. Buyers who do not find what they are looking for in November typically step back from active searching over Christmas. They re-engage in February when the autumn market opens. The late November listing captures the urgency that the February listing misses entirely.

For a vendor who is ready, well-prepared, and priced correctly, launching in late November is meeting the most motivated buyers of the year at the moment of peak readiness. That is a genuine advantage, not a consolation for missing the September or October peak. It is a different window with a different buyer, and for the right property and the right vendor, it can produce an exceptional result.
