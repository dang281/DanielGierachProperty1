## Caption

Auction is the dominant sale method for prestige and character homes in Brisbane's inner east. Through October and November, the volume of properties going to auction increases significantly as vendors and agents target the pre-Christmas buyer pool.

For buyers, auctions require unconditional readiness: finance pre-approved, building and pest complete, and a clear walk-away number set before you walk in. For vendors, a competitive auction with multiple registered bidders is one of the most transparent price discovery processes available.

The experience varies a great deal depending on the property, the crowd, and how the auctioneer runs the room.

Have you ever bought at auction?
