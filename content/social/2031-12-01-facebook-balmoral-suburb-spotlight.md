## Caption

Balmoral in December has a specific appeal that the suburb wears well. Properties with good outdoor spaces, views over the city lights, and the proximity to the river precinct at the bottom of the hill all present at their best in summer, and buyers who've been sitting on decisions through winter often commit in this period.

River Street's proximity shapes the southern part of the suburb in a way that only becomes clear when you walk the streets rather than study a map. The Bulimba river frontage is accessible from Balmoral, and buyers who want the Bulimba lifestyle with slightly more space have consistently found their answer here.

The character home stock in Balmoral includes some of the more spacious Queenslanders in the inner east. Block sizes above the Bulimba average are a recurring theme, and buyers who have been frustrated by the tighter lots on Oxford Street-adjacent streets often arrive in Balmoral with a different conversation in mind. Upsizers with equity from a previous inner east purchase are a core buyer group.

Balmoral State School is well regarded, and the catchment is a genuine factor for families making the decision between Balmoral and Bulimba. In some instances it tips the balance.

December listings are less common than November, which means supply is lower. For vendors who are ready to go in December, that reduced competition can work in their favour.
