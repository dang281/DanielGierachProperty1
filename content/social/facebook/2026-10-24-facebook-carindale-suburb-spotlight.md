## Caption

Carindale is the suburb where Brisbane's outer ring buyers graduate to, and where inner-east buyers who want more land eventually land.

Westfield Carindale anchors the suburb's commercial identity. As a retail and services hub, it provides a level of daily convenience that most inner-ring suburbs rely on their CBD proximity to substitute for. Residents in Carindale do not need to go to the city for most practical purposes, which matters for families with young children and for households with multiple work schedules to manage.

The residential land in Carindale sits predominantly in the 600 to 800 square metre range for established homes, which is genuinely generous by the standards of anything closer to the CBD. The older housing stock on those sites has attracted a wave of renovation and rebuild activity over the past decade. Buyers who are prepared to put work in find options here that simply do not exist at this land size closer to the city.

The access to the M1 motorway and the direct routes toward the CBD via Old Cleveland Road and Cavendish Road give residents practical choices about how they move.

For Carindale owners who have held for a decade or more, the growth in the suburb's standing among buyers has been consistent. A current appraisal often produces a different number than long-term owners expect.
