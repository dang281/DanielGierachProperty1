## Caption

Murarrie is a suburb that rewards buyers who look at employment proximity rather than just CBD distance. The Gateway Motorway is immediately accessible. The Airport Link tunnel and the airport precinct are within a short drive. The Murarrie industrial and commercial corridor provides direct employment access for buyers working in logistics, aviation, or the trade and services sectors that are concentrated along this corridor.

The residential streets of Murarrie sit between the commercial precinct and the river, and that position creates a suburb with practical advantages that buyers from outside the inner east sometimes discover later than they should. The Murarrie Recreation Reserve provides green space and a sporting hub that gives the suburb the community infrastructure that families look for. The access to Cannon Hill's retail and the broader inner east amenity is direct via Old Cleveland Road.

The housing stock is predominantly post-war, with solid brick and low-set construction that provides practical living spaces and good land content at price points that are competitive relative to the surrounding inner east suburbs. Buyers who are looking for a free-standing house with genuine land in the inner east and have a realistic budget often arrive at Murarrie after finding the premium suburbs beyond their reach.

Demand has been growing steadily as buyers who understand the employment proximity advantages recognise the value that Murarrie delivers at its current price point.
