## Caption

Seven Hills carries the elevation advantage that inner east buyers increasingly factor into their suburb selection. The ridge streets in Seven Hills get views and morning sun access that the flat land suburbs closer to the river do not provide at the same price point. For buyers who have been comparing Seven Hills to East Brisbane or Coorparoo on price and finding similar numbers, the elevation and the resulting streetscape character often settle the decision.

The suburb's residential character is predominantly family-scale housing on established blocks. The combination of block size and suburb location positions Seven Hills as an option for the buyer who wants a genuine backyard and a practical distance to the CBD without committing to Carindale or Carina at the outer edge of the inner east band.

Churchie access from Seven Hills is a driver that sustains a specific buyer segment through every season. Families who are making a school-driven address decision in Seven Hills are working to an enrolment timeline that does not correlate with the property market's seasonal pattern. Those buyers are present in winter and motivated.

The owner-occupier character of Seven Hills means that properties that come to market are typically well-maintained and presented. Buyer expectations reflect that, and properties that meet them sell well.
