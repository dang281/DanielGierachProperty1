## Caption

Murarrie has spent years being the suburb buyers discover after missing out on Cannon Hill or Morningside, and that pattern is now showing up in the sales data. Positioned just off the Gateway Motorway with quick access to both the CBD and the airport, it offers genuine convenience without the premium price tag of its neighbours to the west.

The suburb has a residential pocket that sits alongside its industrial precinct, and the newer housing estates that have grown up in recent years have attracted a buyer profile that values space and access over walkability scores. Families and couples priced out of Morningside have found that Murarrie delivers comparable commute times and better block sizes, at a lower entry point. [VERIFY: current median house price and comparison with Morningside before posting.]

Wynnum Road connects the suburb easily to the Cannon Hill Shopping Centre and the Clem Jones Expressway, while the Gateway Motorway makes it one of the easier inner-east suburbs to reach if you are commuting south or to the airport precinct. For buyers working at the Port of Brisbane or in the industrial corridor, proximity is a genuine daily advantage.

Stock is still relatively limited, and properties that present well are drawing interest from multiple buyer groups, including investors alongside owner-occupiers.
