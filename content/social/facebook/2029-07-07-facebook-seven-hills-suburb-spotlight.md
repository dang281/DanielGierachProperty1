## Caption

Seven Hills sits quietly between Coorparoo and Annerley, elevated enough to catch good aspect and well away from the main road noise. It is the kind of suburb that rarely announces itself, but buyers who know the inner east know exactly what it offers.

The homes here are mostly post-war and character builds on blocks that are generous by inner east standards. Streets like Balmoral Road and the quieter residential runs off Whites Road give families room that comparable suburbs closer to the river simply cannot match at the same price point. Villanova College and Our Lady of Lourdes are within easy reach, and that Catholic school corridor is a genuine driver of buyer enquiry for families arriving from outside the area.

What sellers in Seven Hills benefit from is limited supply. Listings here do not come up often, and when they do, the buyer pool is ready. Families who have been watching the suburb for twelve months move quickly because they know another comparable property is not around the corner. That dynamic consistently supports results at the top of the range.

Coorparoo Junction is minutes away for coffee and weekend markets, and the CBD is accessible without a complicated commute. For families who want space, character, and school proximity, Seven Hills checks every box.

If you own in Seven Hills and are considering selling, reach out.
