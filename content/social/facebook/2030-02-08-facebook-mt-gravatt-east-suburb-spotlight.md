## Caption

Mt Gravatt East shares the elevation and district views that define the broader Mt Gravatt ridge, and adds a specific school proximity angle that drives a portion of its buyer demand. Seton College and Padua College are both close by, and the Cavendish Road State High School catchment covers the suburb, giving families a range of secondary schooling options that's genuinely useful.

Blocks here tend to be larger than what's on offer closer to the city at comparable prices, and the renovation buyer profile has been consistently active. Buyers who want a decent-sized block with room to extend or improve, at a price that's more accessible than Camp Hill or Coorparoo, find Mt Gravatt East worth looking at seriously.

The housing stock is a mix of original post-war homes, 1960s-70s brick, and some that have been significantly renovated. The unrenovated ones on larger blocks represent the clearest opportunity for buyers prepared to do the work.

Median house prices are around $1.2M at the time of writing. Verify this via CoreLogic or PropTrack before relying on it.
