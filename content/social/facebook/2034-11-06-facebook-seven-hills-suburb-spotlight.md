## Caption

Seven Hills is one of the more underrepresented stories in Brisbane's inner east property market. The suburb's combination of larger blocks, strong elevation with views across the city and ranges, and proximity to the Carindale and Coorparoo retail catchments creates a value proposition that is not always reflected in the first-pass search results of buyers who filter by suburb name alone.

The buyer profile in Seven Hills tends to be specific: families who want land and space, buyers who have been priced out of the prestige suburbs to the north and recognise the value at this elevation, and long-term investors who understand that land content in this part of the inner south east has historically been a reliable component of growth.

Properties with genuine views in Seven Hills achieve meaningfully different prices from those without. The view premium is real and buyers with finance in place understand it. When a well-positioned home with a northerly aspect and city views comes to market in Seven Hills, the open home attendance from buyers who have been watching tends to be strong.
