## Caption

Balmoral sits at the northern end of the inner east, bounded by the river on two sides, and it has retained a distinctly residential character that larger, more commercially active suburbs have not. The streets are quiet. The blocks are generous by inner-city standards. And the housing stock includes some of the finest character homes in Brisbane.

The buyer who wants Balmoral typically knows exactly why. They have usually looked at Hawthorne and Bulimba and found what they wanted in terms of suburb feel but are drawn to Balmoral's additional privacy and lower density. The Balmoral State High catchment is one of the most consistently referenced catchment premiums in the inner east, and it draws families from well outside the area who understand what the address provides for their children's secondary schooling.

Properties in Balmoral that are well presented and honestly priced tend to attract genuine competition from a pool of buyers who have been searching specifically for what Balmoral offers. They are not buyers who ended up here by accident.

If you are a Balmoral homeowner thinking about whether now is the right time to sell, I am happy to have that conversation with you directly.
