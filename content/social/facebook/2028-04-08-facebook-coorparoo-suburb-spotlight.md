## Caption

Coorparoo keeps delivering for sellers, and the reasons are not hard to find.

At around 5 kilometres from the CBD, the suburb offers the lifestyle of the inner east without the premium attached to Balmoral or Hawthorne addresses. The Cavendish Road café strip is a genuine drawcard, with coffee, breakfast spots, and independent retailers that give the suburb a village feel without the village price tag.

The school catchment is a big part of the story for family buyers. Coorparoo State School consistently draws strong demand from families upgrading from further out, and that demand does not soften much even when broader market conditions ease.

The housing stock is varied, which means sellers across different price points all have an audience. Character homes on generous blocks appeal to renovators and owner-occupiers, while the newer townhouse developments along Old Cleveland Road attract buyers who want low maintenance and location over land size.

Median house price is tracking around $1.3 million. Units remain in demand from young couples and first-home buyers who want the Coorparoo address but are working within a tighter budget.

If your Coorparoo home has been sitting on the back burner, autumn is typically when the most committed buyers are actively looking.
