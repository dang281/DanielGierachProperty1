## Caption

Carindale is anchored by one of Queensland's largest retail centres and surrounded by some of Brisbane's most consistently in-demand family streets.

Westfield Carindale is the obvious centrepiece, covering everything from supermarkets and specialty stores to cinemas and dining. For families with children, the convenience of having that on the doorstep cannot be overstated. Add in access to multiple primary schools and easy freeway connections, and you have a suburb that checks most of the boxes on a family's list.

The housing stock in Carindale leans toward the larger end. Four-bedroom homes on decent blocks are common, and that is precisely the product that attracts families upgrading from inner-east townhouses or making the move from further south. Streets like Raven Street and the quieter cul-de-sacs off Carindale Street consistently draw competition at inspection.

Median house price is sitting around $1.1 million. Investors are also active here, drawn by rental yields that hold up well against inner-east alternatives and tenant demand that stays strong due to the suburb's family appeal.

Autumn is typically when motivated family buyers are most active, with school-year timing creating a natural push to settle before mid-year.
