## Caption

Oxford Street sets the tone for Bulimba in a way that few shopping strips do for their suburbs. The cafés, restaurants, and foot traffic on a Saturday morning give the suburb a sense of activity that buyers feel immediately. Pair that with river access, a CityCat ferry to the CBD, and a school catchment that draws families from across Brisbane, and you have a suburb that consistently commands premium prices.

The buyer profile is broad but skewed toward professionals and established families. Many have been watching Bulimba for years, waiting for the right home in the right street. When that home arrives, competition is real and swift. Days on market for well-presented properties is among the shortest in the inner east.

Median house prices are around $1.9M at the time of writing, which should be verified against current CoreLogic or PropTrack data. The upper end of the market has performed particularly well for homes with river views or within easy walking distance of Oxford Street.

Ray White Bulimba is our home base, so this is a suburb I know deeply. The results here in recent years have reflected what happens when proper preparation meets genuine buyer competition.

If you own in Bulimba and are thinking about your options, I'd be glad to walk you through what your property is worth in the current market.
