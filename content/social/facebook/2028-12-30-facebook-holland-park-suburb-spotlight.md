## Caption

Holland Park consistently outperforms its profile, which is to say it delivers inner east quality without the inner east price tag that most buyers assume goes with it.

The suburb sits roughly six kilometres south of the CBD. The ridgelines and gullies that define the streetscape create significant variation in aspect and elevation. Streets on the elevated ground with CBD views or north-facing rear yards consistently achieve the suburb's best results.

The buyer profile is predominantly the family who has been priced out of Camp Hill or Coorparoo, willing to trade a kilometre of distance for a better land holding at a lower entry price. This buyer has done their school catchment research and confirmed the busway connections.

The Holland Park West Village strip and proximity to the Tarragindi sporting precinct give the suburb practical amenity. The Cavendish Road corridor connects into the inner east proper without requiring a car for every trip.

Holland Park attracts the buyer who has found the numbers work. That buyer tends to move quickly when the right property arrives.
