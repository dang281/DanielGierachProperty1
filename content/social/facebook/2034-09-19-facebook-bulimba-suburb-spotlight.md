## Caption

Spring is when Bulimba's market tends to run hardest. Buyers who have been monitoring the suburb through winter, waiting for the right property to appear, become noticeably more active in September. The combination of limited stock and a buyer pool that has been patient since July creates the competitive conditions that produce strong results for sellers who are well-prepared.

Bulimba's appeal is easy to understand from the street: Oxford Street's restaurants and shops, the river, Bulimba State School, and a housing stock that ranges from original Queenslanders to renovated character homes. Buyers who discover the suburb rarely look elsewhere.

The streets immediately behind Oxford Street and those running toward the river are the most closely watched by active buyers. Homes with north-facing aspect, outdoor entertaining space, and a short walk to the ferry terminal consistently attract multiple groups at opens.

If you own in Bulimba and are considering a spring sale, now is the right time to get an appraisal before the September-October competition window is underway.
