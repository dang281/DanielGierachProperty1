## Caption

Hawthorne's walkability sets it apart from a lot of the inner east. The Oxford Street connection means cafés, restaurants, and daily amenity are within reach on foot, yet the residential streets themselves are quiet, tree-lined, and genuinely pleasant to live in. That combination of easy access and residential calm is difficult to manufacture and consistently drives buyer appetite here.

The dominant buyer profile is upsizers, often families moving from Bulimba who want more land, or professionals who've been renting in the area and are ready to commit. They tend to know Hawthorne well before they start looking seriously, which means when the right property appears they move with confidence.

New stock is limited. Hawthorne doesn't produce many listings per year, and that scarcity keeps competition firm. Character homes in good condition attract multiple buyers reliably, particularly those with generous land content on the better streets.

Median house prices are around $1.85M at the time of writing. Check current CoreLogic or PropTrack data to confirm this before making any decisions. Homes at the top of that range have generally been character Queenslanders with extensions done well, on blocks that give families room to operate.
