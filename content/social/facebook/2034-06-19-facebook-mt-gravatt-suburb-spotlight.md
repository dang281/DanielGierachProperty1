## Caption

Mt Gravatt's elevation is its most immediate distinguishing feature. The streets that run across the escarpment carry views toward the CBD and across the inner south that buyers who are looking at comparable suburbs at lower elevations simply cannot access. For buyers who have spent time driving the streets in both Mt Gravatt and the flatter suburbs to the east, the elevation difference settles the question of where the premium is justified.

The Griffith University presence creates a sustained residential demand profile in Mt Gravatt. The university employs a professional cohort that chooses to live close to the campus, and the student population generates consistent rental demand that keeps vacancy rates low in the surrounding streets. For investors or owner-occupiers who are considering the long-term demand fundamentals, the university precinct is a stabilising factor.

Westfield Garden City's scale means that Mt Gravatt residents can resolve the full range of their retail and services requirements within the suburb boundary. The centre's food and leisure offer extends the precinct beyond pure retail convenience into a destination that residents use regularly.

The Pacific Motorway access from Mt Gravatt is efficient for buyers who are commuting south or travelling to the Gold Coast. The on-ramp locations mean that motorway entry does not require navigating through residential streets at peak hour.
