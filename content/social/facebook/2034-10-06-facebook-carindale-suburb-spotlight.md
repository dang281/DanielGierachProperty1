## Caption

Carindale is a suburb where the fundamentals are strong and the narrative is often underestimated. Buyers who approach it primarily as a Westfield Carindale suburb are missing what the residential market actually looks like: a consistent demand for well-presented homes in the middle to upper price range, driven by families who want space, access, and good schooling without paying the premium attached to the suburbs closer to the river.

The suburb has benefited from its position between the Inner East and the Bayside corridor. Buyers who have been priced out of Camp Hill or Coorparoo find that Carindale offers genuine value on a square-metre basis for larger blocks with similar family liveability.

Rental demand in Carindale is supported by the employment clusters at Gateway Business Park and the nearby retail precinct. Vacancy rates have remained consistently low, which matters to investors assessing the holding cost of a larger property.
