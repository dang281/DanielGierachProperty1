## Caption

Bulimba is the suburb people put on their list and rarely take off. The combination of the Oxford Street dining strip, direct river access, the ferry, and a genuine village feel within seven kilometres of the CBD is difficult to replicate elsewhere in Brisbane.

The property offering is varied. Queenslanders on larger allotments sit alongside townhouses and executive homes on the riverside streets. The heritage character of the older stock is a consistent drawcard for buyers who want authenticity rather than a new build in an outer suburb.

Bulimba State School and Bulimba State High are both within the suburb. Catchment demand is a consistent driver, particularly from interstate families who research inner east schooling before inspecting.

Riverside properties on Quay Street operate in a market of their own. For the broader Bulimba market, the appeal is consistency: demand has not evaporated when sentiment has shifted elsewhere.

For homeowners considering options, Bulimba's buyer depth makes it one of the more reliable selling environments in the inner east.
