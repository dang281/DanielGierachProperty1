## Caption

Carina Heights delivers what its name suggests: an elevated position within the Carina-Carindale-Camp Hill corridor that gives the suburb a distinct residential character and, on the right streets, view lines that justify the premium over lower-lying addresses.

The suburb's position between Carindale and Camp Hill makes it a natural consideration for buyers who have researched both and are looking for the combination of Westfield Carindale's retail accessibility and the elevated, quieter residential streets that Camp Hill is known for. Carina Heights often delivers both at a price point that sits between the two, making it a suburb that buyers arrive at through process rather than serendipity.

School access is a factor in the buyer decision. Proximity to Cavendish Road State High School is relevant for families in the planning phase of their school decisions, and the Catholic and independent school options accessible from Carina Heights add to the education infrastructure that deliberate family buyers are researching.

The housing stock is predominantly low-set brick and post-war construction on established blocks. Development sites are not uncommon, which means the suburb attracts both owner-occupier buyers who intend to renovate and hold, and investors and developers who are assessing the land content. That dual buyer profile keeps demand consistent across market conditions.
