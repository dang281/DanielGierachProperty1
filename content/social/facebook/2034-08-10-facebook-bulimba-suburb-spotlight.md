## Caption

Bulimba continues to be one of Brisbane's most consistent performers in the inner east. The suburb's blend of character Queenslanders, proximity to Oxford Street, and school catchments for Bulimba State School makes it a reliable draw for families upgrading from apartments and buyers relocating from interstate.

Winter stock has been tight across the inner east, and Bulimba is no exception. When well-presented homes hit the market here, they tend to attract multiple groups at opens within the first two weekends. Buyers who've been pre-approved and waiting are active, and the hesitation that typically defines winter has not been as pronounced here as in outer suburbs.

The streets between Oxford Street and the river remain the most sought after. Properties north of Bulimba Creek also attract strong interest given their access to Hawthorne's ferry terminals and walkable retail.

If you're thinking about what your Bulimba home is worth in the current market, a no-obligation appraisal is the clearest starting point.
