## Caption

Carina Heights sits between Carina and Camp Hill, and for buyers who have done their homework, that location is the point. You get the quiet residential streets and the block sizes that Carina Heights is known for, within reach of the Camp Hill school catchments that families specifically move suburbs to access.

The housing stock here is predominantly post-war and 1970s-era homes on generous blocks, and the buyer profile reflects that. Upgraders from Carina and Mt Gravatt East come here for more land. Families already in the area stretch their budget to access Whites Hill State College or Cavendish Road State High catchments, depending on which street they land on.
