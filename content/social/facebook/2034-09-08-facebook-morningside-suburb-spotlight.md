## Caption

Morningside has firmly established itself as the suburb that offers the inner east's lifestyle at a slightly more accessible price point than Hawthorne or Bulimba. Buyers who have been outbid further north discover that the walkability, character homes, and proximity to the Morningside Plaza retail strip make it a strong alternative, and many who arrive intending to look elsewhere end up buying here.

The streets between Wynnum Road and Norman Creek carry the suburb's strongest demand, particularly those with larger allotments that allow for pool installations and extended outdoor living. Spring opens a more active buyer pool, and Morningside's price-to-lifestyle ratio means multiple-offer situations at correctly positioned spring listings are common.

The Morningside State School catchment is an additional draw for families who want a well-regarded state primary close to home. Buyer interest from both families and investors has remained consistent through the winter period.
