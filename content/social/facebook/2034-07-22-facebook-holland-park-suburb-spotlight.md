## Caption

Holland Park sits on the ridge running south of Camp Hill and has a distinct character in the inner east: wider streets, generous block sizes, post-war housing with renovation potential, and a suburban feel that attracts families who want space without sacrificing inner east location.

The suburb is well served by transport, with express bus routes to the CBD along Logan Road, and the Holland Park shopping precinct provides day-to-day convenience. The proximity to Greenslopes Private Hospital is a lifestyle factor for buyers in healthcare.

What distinguishes Holland Park in the buyer's analysis: the land size. Blocks in Holland Park often sit 50 to 100 square metres larger than equivalent properties in Camp Hill or Coorparoo at similar price points. For buyers who are weighing land versus suburb premium, Holland Park is frequently the value answer.

Family buyers, owner-occupiers, and investors who understand the suburb's fundamentals make up the consistent buyer pool here. Properties that present well and are positioned at market are absorbed quickly.
