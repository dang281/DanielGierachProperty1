## Caption

Holland Park has steadily moved up the priority list for buyers who start their search in Coorparoo or Greenslopes and discover they can get more house for their budget by looking south of Cavendish Road. The suburb's large post-war and character home stock sits on blocks that would be difficult to find at similar proximity to the CBD in most other cities.

The suburb is well-served by express bus routes into the city and has strong feeder access to the South-East Freeway for buyers with commitments on the Gold Coast corridor. Holland Park State High School draws families from surrounding suburbs, which keeps demand broad and consistent.

Spring listings in Holland Park tend to attract interstate buyers who discover the suburb during inspection trips: the price-to-space ratio surprises people who arrive expecting inner-city density. That buyer reaction is why presenting well and pricing accurately at spring launch matters here.

If you own in Holland Park and want to understand what your property would realistically achieve this spring, a current appraisal is worth having before stock levels rise in September.
