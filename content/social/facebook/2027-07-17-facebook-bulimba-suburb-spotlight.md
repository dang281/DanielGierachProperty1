## Caption

Bulimba is the suburb that sits at the centre of Ray White Bulimba's patch, and it is a market I follow closely. It has a character that other inner east suburbs do not quite replicate: the Oxford Street retail strip, the river access, the ferry connection to the CBD, and the concentration of character homes on streets that buyers return to again and again.

The demand profile in Bulimba tends to skew toward established families and professionals who want walkability alongside space. The properties that sell strongly here typically offer a generous indoor-outdoor connection, good ceiling height, and proximity to the Oxford Street precinct without being directly on it. Buyers in Bulimba are specific about what they want, and when the right property appears they tend to act decisively.

Bulimba also sits within the Bulimba State School catchment, which generates consistent buyer interest from families approaching primary school enrolment age. That catchment premium is real and has been reflected in sale outcomes for well over a decade.

If you own a property in Bulimba and you would like to know what it is worth in the current market, I am the right person to ask. I have direct visibility over what has sold, what did not, and why.
