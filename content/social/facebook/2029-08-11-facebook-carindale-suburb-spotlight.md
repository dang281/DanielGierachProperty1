## Caption

Carindale is anchored by Westfield Carindale, and for families that matters. The retail, medical, and service amenity that comes with a major centre within the suburb boundary is a practical advantage that buyers with children factor in heavily.

The residential streets here offer land sizes that the closer inner east suburbs gave up a decade ago. Large blocks, predominantly owner-occupied, and a quiet family character that has remained consistent because the suburb holds tightly. Carindale State School serves the catchment well, and several private school bus routes service the area, which broadens the school access beyond what the suburb can offer on its own doorstep.

Gateway Motorway and Beaudesert Road access gives the suburb strong connectivity for buyers who work south of the CBD or need to move around the city efficiently. Buyers arriving from Carina, Carina Heights, and Mt Gravatt East who want more space without relocating to the outer suburbs find Carindale sits at exactly the right point.

Listing frequency here is low and hold rates are strong. Families who arrive tend to stay through the school years and beyond. When a property does come to market in an established street, the enquiry is genuine and the buyer pool prepared.

If you own in Carindale and are considering selling, reach out.
