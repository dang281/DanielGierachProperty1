## Caption

Carindale is one of Brisbane's largest inner east suburbs by area, and the housing stock reflects that scale. Blocks are generously sized by inner east standards, the streets are wide, and the suburb's proximity to Westfield Carindale gives it a daily-living convenience that most inner east suburbs cannot match.

The buyer profile in Carindale trends toward families. The combination of land size, school access, and the Carindale shopping precinct attracts buyers who are prioritising functional space over proximity to the CBD. The commute is manageable by Eastern Busway or car, and the suburb's positioning between Carina and the outer ring means buyers here often have a clear sense of the value proposition.

Properties in the elevated streets near the Carindale golf course boundary and those with good natural light and larger usable rear yards tend to attract the strongest interest. Presentation matters significantly in Carindale, where buyers are comparing directly across a broad range of stock.
