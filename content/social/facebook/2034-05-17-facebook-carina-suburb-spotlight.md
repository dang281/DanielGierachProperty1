## Caption

Carina in winter sees a buyer pool that is less visible at open homes but no less motivated. Families who need to resolve a purchase before the second school term commits them to a particular address are still active in May. The buyer who has been searching since February and has not yet found the right property is still looking.

The suburb's value proposition does not change with the season. Carina offers free-standing houses on established blocks within 10 kilometres of the CBD at price points that are competitive relative to the surrounding inner east suburbs. That combination does not disappear in June. It simply attracts buyers who are more decisive rather than more numerous.

The Old Cleveland Road corridor that runs through Carina is both the suburb's main access route and its main practical amenity strip. The retail, dining, and services along the corridor are accessible without requiring a major trip, and the Westfield Carindale access from the eastern end of the suburb adds the larger-format retail that rounds out the convenience proposition.

Carina's stability as an owner-occupier suburb means that vendors are typically selling after substantial periods of ownership. The properties that are well-maintained and presented to the buyer pool rather than listed as-is consistently outperform the sub-market of unrenovated stock that also comes to market in Carina.
