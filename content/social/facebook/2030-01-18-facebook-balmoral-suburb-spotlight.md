## Caption

Balmoral sits alongside Bulimba geographically but has a quieter character. The streets are lined with timber character homes, some with river views, and the suburb has a residential density that feels less busy than its neighbour while sharing many of the same access advantages. Buyers who want the inner east without the Oxford Street foot traffic at their doorstep tend to find exactly what they're after here.

The buyer profile in Balmoral leans toward families who've done their research. They know the school catchments are strong, they've walked the streets, and they're prepared to pay for position and quality. Homes here don't come up frequently, which is part of the appeal for sellers. When a well-presented property hits the market, the pool of waiting buyers tends to respond quickly.

Median house prices are around $1.8M at the time of writing. Verify this against current CoreLogic or PropTrack data before using it in any decision. The best performers have been character homes with good land content and some aspect, particularly those with any river corridor view.

Autumn campaigns historically perform well in Balmoral. The suburb photographs beautifully in the softer March and April light, and the buyer pool tends to be active at that time of year.
