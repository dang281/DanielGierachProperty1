## Caption

Carindale's autumn market is typically driven by families who want to make a move before the middle of the school year makes disruption impractical. That timing creates a real window of buyer activity in March, and sellers who are on market at this point are meeting families who've made decisions and are ready to act.

What's been selling in Carindale and drawing genuine competition is the larger family home on a proper block in the streets closest to the school catchment areas and with easy Westfield Carindale access. The suburb doesn't produce the same kind of street-by-street variation you find in Hawthorne or Camp Hill, but position and land content still matter, and buyers here know the difference between a well-positioned Carindale home and a less well-positioned one.

Presentation matters in Carindale. The suburb attracts buyers who are comparing it against new house-and-land options in outer ring suburbs, so a well-maintained existing home that's been freshened up competes well against that alternative. Sellers who've prepared properly get results that outperform sellers who haven't.

Median house prices are around $1.2M. Verify this via CoreLogic or PropTrack before relying on it.
