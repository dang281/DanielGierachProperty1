## Caption

Seven Hills is where inner east buyers land when they start measuring by what a dollar buys. The allotment sizes and home footprints here would cost significantly more in Hawthorne or Balmoral, with the same drive time to the CBD.

The suburb has changed noticeably over the past decade. The renovation activity on older post-war stock has been consistent, and the calibre of homes coming to market has lifted. Buyers who walked through Seven Hills five years ago and were not quite ready are often surprised by what the street appeal looks like now.

Whites Hill State College draws families into the catchment area, and Seven Hills sits within reach. The Coorparoo and Cavendish Road retail corridors are accessible without a long drive.

The typical buyer has inspected Hawthorne and Norman Park, knows what they want, and needs somewhere that resolves the price equation without leaving the inner east. Seven Hills regularly does that.

Buyer interest in Seven Hills has held up well, and the fundamentals remain attractive.
