## Caption

Holland Park is the kind of suburb that experienced inner east buyers add to their shortlist once they understand the value it represents. Elevated, close to the city, with good school catchments and a consistent family buyer market, it offers the inner east formula at a price point below the premium river suburbs.

The suburb sits on the ridge south-west of Greenslopes, with the best streets offering northerly aspects and partial city views. Holland Park Road and the surrounding residential streets have a quiet, settled character that reflects a buyer base of established families who bought for the long term and stayed. Turnover is lower than comparable inner suburbs, which keeps supply constrained.

Holland Park State School and the proximity to Cavendish Road State High School are meaningful drivers for family buyers. The Cavendish Road catchment in particular generates consistent targeted demand from families who plan their purchase around secondary school access.

The Old Cleveland Road corridor gives the suburb practical convenience: multiple shopping options, dining, and transport access that shortens the effective distance to the CBD for day-to-day life.

For sellers in Holland Park, the combination of limited stock and a deep family buyer pool creates strong campaign conditions when properties are priced correctly. The suburb attracts buyers who know what they want and act decisively when the right property appears.
