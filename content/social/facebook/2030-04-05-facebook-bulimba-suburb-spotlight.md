## Caption

Bulimba has a reputation for being one of Brisbane's premium lifestyle suburbs, and that reputation is consistently supported by what happens in the sales market. It is a suburb where scarcity of supply, genuine lifestyle credentials, and a deep buyer pool combine to produce strong and relatively stable results across market conditions.

Oxford Street is the obvious draw: a strip of cafes, restaurants, and boutiques that gives the suburb a pedestrian-scale village feel that is rare in Brisbane. The river precinct, the ferry access to the CBD, and the open parkland along the waterfront all contribute to a lifestyle that is difficult to replicate elsewhere in the inner east.

The buyer profile in Bulimba skews toward professionals who have already owned in the inner east and are making a deliberate quality-of-life upgrade, often from Camp Hill, Hawthorne, or Coorparoo. These are buyers who know what they are getting and are prepared to pay for it.

What moves in Bulimba most consistently is the character home on a 600 to 800-square-metre block, typically a Queenslander or a post-war weatherboard that has been renovated with quality. The top-end properties with river views are a different conversation but attract genuinely competitive buyer interest from across Queensland.

If you are thinking about selling in Bulimba, a detailed appraisal is the best starting point.
