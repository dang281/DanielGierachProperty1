## Caption

Cannon Hill sits at an interesting point in the inner east market. It offers the infrastructure access and lifestyle amenity of suburbs like Morningside and Carina Heights without the price premium those addresses carry, and buyers who understand the area know it.

What draws buyers to Cannon Hill consistently is the combination of the Cannon Hill train station, the proximity to Westfield Carindale and the Cannon Hill Kmart precinct, and the reasonable drive or public transport access to the CBD. For families, the suburb also sits within easy reach of a number of well-regarded state and private school options.

The housing stock in Cannon Hill tends toward post-war lowsets and Queenslanders on decent block sizes, with a mix of original homes and renovated properties. Buyers in Cannon Hill often fall into two groups: families who want the space and accessibility, and investors who see the rental demand and relative affordability as a combination worth holding.
