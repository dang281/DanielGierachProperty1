## Caption

Carina is a suburb that delivers consistent long-term performance without carrying the profile premium of its closer inner east neighbours. That lower profile has historically translated into a better entry price for buyers and a patient, compounding return for owners who hold. If you have been in Carina for ten or more years, that equation is easy to see in your own position.

The suburb's access is one of its practical strengths. Gateway Motorway and South East Freeway entry points give residents flexibility for work commutes and weekend movement, and multiple bus routes service the suburb for those who prefer not to drive. Westfield Carindale is within reach for shopping and everyday retail, and the Carina Leagues Club is a genuine local institution.

Carina State School services the catchment, and the mix of post-war family homes alongside newer builds gives buyers choice between character and low-maintenance product. That range of stock keeps the buyer pool broad, with family owner-occupiers and investors both active.

Against equivalent land sizes in Morningside or Camp Hill, Carina offers value that buyers with a longer holding horizon understand well. For sellers who have held through the quieter periods, the current buyer depth in this suburb reflects that calculation being made by a new generation of buyers.

If you own in Carina and are considering selling, reach out.
