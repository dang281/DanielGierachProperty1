## Caption

Camp Hill sits on a ridge that gives it one of the best aspects in Brisbane's inner east. The views north toward the city from the upper streets are a reason people move there and stay.

The suburb has a clear character split: upper streets near Cavendish Road offer post-war homes on generous allotments, many renovated to a standard that competes with Hawthorne and Balmoral. Closer to Old Cleveland Road the offering is more mixed, with townhouses and medium-density.

School catchment is a consistent driver of demand. Whites Hill State College continues to anchor buyer appetite in Camp Hill's upper streets, and properties within that catchment regularly attract competitive interest from families who have done their research.

The suburb is ten minutes from the CBD, walkable to the Cavendish Road strip, and well-served by bus.

For homeowners in Camp Hill considering a move, the buyer pool in this suburb remains well-engaged across the price spectrum.
