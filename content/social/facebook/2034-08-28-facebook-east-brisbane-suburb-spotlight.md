## Caption

East Brisbane occupies one of the most strategically placed positions in the inner east: walking distance to Mowbray Park ferry terminal, a short drive to the CBD via the Corridor or the Story Bridge, and surrounded by the Gabba precinct amenity. That accessibility has made it consistently attractive to professionals who want the inner-city lifestyle without the South Brisbane price point.

The suburb's housing mix runs from terrace houses and low-set brick through to character Queenslanders on the hillier streets toward Morningside. The best-performing properties over recent years have been those on elevated blocks with city or river glimpses, and homes that have been updated for indoor-outdoor living.

Stock in East Brisbane tends to be limited, which creates responsive market conditions for sellers who present well and price accurately. Buyers in this suburb typically have a clear picture of what they want and move when the right property appears.

If you own in East Brisbane and want to understand the current market before spring listings arrive, a no-obligation appraisal is the clearest starting point.
