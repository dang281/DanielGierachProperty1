## Caption

Coorparoo is one of the inner east's most active property markets, and the price range reflects it: the suburb covers a wide spectrum from entry-level townhouses to character homes on large corner blocks that sit well into the upper end of the local market.

The suburb's attraction is consistent: it is genuinely close to the CBD, the retail and dining on Old Cleveland Road gives it daily-use lifestyle appeal, and the school catchments, particularly for Coorparoo Secondary College, are a material driver of family buyer demand.

What buyers in Coorparoo quickly learn is that the pricing in this suburb correlates closely with proximity to Old Cleveland Road and the schools, and the condition of presentation at open homes. Properties that are campaign-ready consistently achieve results that surprised owners who had underestimated the suburb's appeal to the current buyer pool.
