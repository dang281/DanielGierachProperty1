## Caption

Carina is a suburb that rewards buyers who look past the arterial roads. The majority of the residential stock sits on quiet streets well removed from Creek Road and Old Cleveland Road, and those streets give families exactly the block size and yard space that has become increasingly difficult to find closer to the river.

The housing mix is mostly post-war and 1960s to 1980s brick homes, with a significant proportion of properties that represent genuine renovation or development potential on blocks of 600 square metres or more. The combination of land size and relatively accessible price point compared to Camp Hill or Coorparoo has made Carina a consistent choice for buyers who want to build equity through renovation over a hold period of five to ten years.

Carindale's Westfield centre is a five-minute drive, which gives residents a self-sufficient shopping and dining option that many families find more practical than the café strips of the closer-in suburbs. The Carina area also has direct bus access to the CBD along Old Cleveland Road.

For investors, the rental demand in Carina has been reliable, supported by proximity to Princess Alexandra Hospital and the healthcare precinct along Ipswich Road, which generates consistent demand for rental accommodation at reasonable commute distances.
