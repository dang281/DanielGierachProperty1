## Caption

East Brisbane sits immediately south of the CBD in a way that almost no other suburb can claim. South Bank, the Mater Hospital, the Gabba, and the Story Bridge are all within a short commute or walk. For professionals who want to live close to their work and close to the city's best amenity, the location case here is straightforward.

The property mix is a genuine blend. Apartments dominate in volume, but the character houses that remain in East Brisbane carry significant appeal for buyers who want land and character at a price that isn't asking them to be an hour from the city. The capital growth story here has always been driven by location more than land content, and the proximity premium has held up across different market cycles.

Rental demand from professionals working at South Bank, the hospitals, or the CBD has kept vacancy rates consistently low. That makes East Brisbane reliable for investors in a way that more peripheral suburbs aren't.

Median house prices for freestanding homes are around $1.4M at the time of writing. Check current data via CoreLogic or PropTrack before relying on that figure.
