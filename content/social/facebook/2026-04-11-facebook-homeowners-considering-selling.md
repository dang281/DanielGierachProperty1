## Caption

Most sellers I meet have a number in their head before we sit down.

Sometimes the market confirms it. Sometimes it does not. The second conversation, the honest one, is the one that matters most.

Selling your home in Bulimba, Hawthorne, Norman Park or anywhere in the inner east is not just a financial decision. It involves the timing, the next chapter, and everything that goes with it. Real life, not just real estate.

What I have seen over and over again: the sellers who come out happiest are the ones who started with accurate information, even when that information was not exactly what they hoped for. They made decisions based on reality, not on an estimate someone gave them to win the listing.

My job is to give you a clear picture of what your property can genuinely achieve right now, what the process looks like, and what the market is actually doing. Not to tell you what you want to hear.

If you are thinking about selling in the inner east and you want a straight conversation rather than a pitch, I am happy to help.
