## Caption

Mt Gravatt East sits at the convergence of good access and genuine affordability within the inner southeast. Buyers who have been pushed out of Coorparoo and Holland Park by prices find that Mt Gravatt East offers comparable space and school options at a more accessible entry point, which is why the suburb has attracted consistent interest from first-home buyers and investors alike.

The suburb is well-positioned for families: Griffith University's Mt Gravatt campus is nearby, Garden City Shopping Centre is a short drive, and bus routes into the city run along Logan Road. Homes on the higher ground toward the Mt Gravatt Conservation Reserve have the added appeal of outlook and proximity to walking tracks.

As spring opens the market, Mt Gravatt East tends to attract buyers who have completed their research and are ready to act. The competition dynamic at opens shifts noticeably in September as buyers who held off through winter begin to move.
