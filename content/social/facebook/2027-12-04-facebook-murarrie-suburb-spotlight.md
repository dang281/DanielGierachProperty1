## Caption

Murarrie is a suburb that still surprises buyers who have been priced out of Morningside and Cannon Hill. The land sizes here, particularly on the southern streets closer to the river corridor, offer a footprint that is increasingly difficult to find inside the ten kilometre ring.

The suburb sits immediately east of Cannon Hill along the Gateway Motorway corridor, with direct access east toward the port and west toward the CBD via Lytton Road. For buyers who need both access and affordability, Murarrie sits in a useful position on that map.

The residential pocket south of Murarrie Road has insulated itself from the industrial footprint to the north. The character there is straightforwardly residential: post-war homes, generous blocks, renovation activity, and genuine owner-occupier demand.

Infrastructure investment around the Gateway Motorway precinct and the Port of Brisbane employment zone continues to support the suburb's fundamentals over the medium term.

For homeowners wondering what their property is worth, buyer appetite east of Cannon Hill has been more consistent than the suburb's modest profile suggests.
