## Caption

Carina occupies a practical position in Brisbane's inner southeast that buyers find increasingly appealing: well connected by bus to the CBD and PA Hospital, within easy reach of Westfield Carindale, and home to a mix of housing stock that suits a range of budgets and household types.

The suburb draws first-home buyers who want a house rather than an apartment at an entry-level price point, investors seeking rental returns from PA Hospital and Griffith University workers, and families attracted by the school catchments and the suburban pace. That diversity of demand has kept Carina's market steady across economic cycles.

Spring brings additional buyer activity to Carina as the open home season resumes. Homes presented well and priced accurately at spring launch tend to move within the first two or three weeks, before spring stock levels normalise and create more competition for buyers' attention.

If you own in Carina and want a straightforward read on what your property is worth in the current market, a current appraisal takes about an hour.
