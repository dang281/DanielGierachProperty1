## Caption

Carina sits about 8km south-east of the CBD and tends to be compared to neighbouring Carina Heights and Camp Hill, though at a meaningfully different price point.

The suburb is primarily owner-occupied, with established blocks, a mix of original post-war homes and renovated properties, and proximity to the Westfield Carindale precinct and its associated services. The Carina retail spine on Old Cleveland Road provides local amenity without the lifestyle-centre premium that drives pricing in suburbs like Hawthorne and Bulimba.

For buyers who want a practical inner-east location with school catchments and a daily commute that works, Carina delivers on the fundamentals. The fact that it sits in the shadow of Camp Hill and Carina Heights in terms of profile means it tends to attract buyers who have done a genuine comparison rather than ones chasing an address.

The market here reflects that. Buyers are motivated and reasonably informed. The comparable sales pool is broader than in some of the smaller inner-east suburbs, which makes pricing more straightforward and outcomes more predictable for sellers who price accurately from the start.

If you own in Carina and want to understand where your property sits in the current market, I am happy to give you an honest answer.
