## Caption

Bulimba is one of the inner east's most complete suburbs. Oxford Street has been Brisbane's best strip retail for more than a decade, the ferry connection to the CBD is a genuine lifestyle asset, and the housing stock runs from renovated Queenslanders on elevated blocks to contemporary townhouses within walking distance of the river.

The buyer profile in Bulimba skews toward couples and families who have been in the inner east before and are upgrading within it. These buyers know what they want. They have visited the suburb multiple times before inspecting. When they find the right property, they move with conviction.

Properties near the river precinct and those with walkability to Oxford Street consistently attract the most competition at open homes. The west end of the suburb, further from the strip, offers better value per square metre and often suits buyers who want more land and a quieter street over proximity to activity.
