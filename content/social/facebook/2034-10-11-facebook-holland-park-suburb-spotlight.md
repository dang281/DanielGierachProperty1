## Caption

Holland Park offers buyers a consistent set of fundamentals that have made it a reliable performer across multiple market cycles. Good access to the South East Freeway, established retail on Logan Road, and school catchments that attract families who are done with apartment living and ready for a house with a yard.

The suburb sits at a price point that gives buyers meaningful value for what they get. The typical Holland Park home offers more land than the suburbs closer to the river at a lower entry price per square metre. That relative value calculation is well understood by buyers who have been shopping the inner south east for six months or more.

Investor activity in Holland Park has been steady, driven by the rental yield available on larger older homes and the proximity to employment at the PA Hospital and Greenslopes Private. The investor buyer who understands the suburb tends to be patient and specific in their criteria.

For sellers, the Holland Park market rewards presentation. The buyers who are most active here have usually inspected twenty or more properties and know immediately when a home is ready to live in versus one that needs work.
