## Caption

Holland Park's autumn market in March tends to bring out a buyer who's been patient. The renovation buyer who targets this suburb has typically looked at Camp Hill and Coorparoo, understood the price difference, and made a deliberate choice to find the value in Holland Park instead. By March, those buyers have usually been looking since January and they're ready to commit.

What's been selling here and producing the strongest results is the original 1950s and 60s housing on the better blocks, particularly on the elevated streets with district views or good aspect. The gap between a basic Holland Park sale and a well-positioned, well-presented one is meaningful, and sellers who understand that distinction and prepare accordingly get materially better outcomes.

The school catchment draw from Holland Park State School and Marshall Road School adds a family buyer layer to what is already a renovation-focused market. Families who want a project home in a suburb with good schooling and a price point below Camp Hill find Holland Park a straightforward choice.

Median house prices are around $1.35M. Verify this via CoreLogic or PropTrack before using it in any decision.
