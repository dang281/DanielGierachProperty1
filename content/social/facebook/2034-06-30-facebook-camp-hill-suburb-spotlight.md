## Caption

Camp Hill's ridgeline geography distinguishes it clearly from the flat inner east suburbs at comparable price points. The elevated streets deliver morning sun, aspect, and in some cases view lines toward the CBD that buyers at the same price point in lower-lying suburbs simply cannot access. For buyers who have identified elevation as a priority, the Camp Hill ridge streets are a specific target.

The suburb's school catchment infrastructure is comprehensive. Camp Hill State Infants and Primary School draws a family buyer pool that prioritises the state school option, and proximity to a range of independent and faith-based schools in the surrounding area gives families who are weighing multiple options a workable address within the suburb boundary.

Westfield Carindale is accessible from Camp Hill without a motorway trip. For families managing a busy schedule, proximity to a major retail centre that covers weekly requirements becomes a practical advantage over time. The Carindale access combined with the quieter residential character of Camp Hill's established streets is a balance that buyers consistently respond to.

The end of the financial year is a period when some vendors decide to act before June 30. Camp Hill properties that come to market now meet buyers who are also motivated by timing.
