## Caption

Bulimba is the suburb the inner east benchmarks itself against.

Oxford Street is one of Brisbane's most active lifestyle precincts, with a concentration of restaurants, cafes, boutiques, and the local cinema that creates genuine weekend foot traffic. The river frontage, the ferry connection to New Farm, the proximity to Hawthorne and the broader eastern corridor all contribute to a suburb identity that is clear and consistent.

For sellers, that clarity is valuable. Buyers who target Bulimba have made a specific choice. They have compared it to the neighbouring options and they have decided that the combination of the Oxford Street precinct, the Bulimba State School catchment, and the character streetscape justifies the premium.

What that means in practice is that well-presented properties in Bulimba attract genuine competition. The buyer group is not passive. They have pre-approval, they know the market, and they act when the right property appears because they understand that Bulimba does not have a large supply of equivalent homes coming to market regularly.

If you own in Bulimba and have been thinking about what the right time to sell looks like, I am happy to give you a clear and honest market assessment.
