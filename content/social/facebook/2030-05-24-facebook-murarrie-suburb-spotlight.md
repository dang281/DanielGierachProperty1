## Caption

Murarrie is a suburb in the process of being rediscovered by a buyer profile that has spent the past decade watching prices in Morningside and Cannon Hill move beyond their reach. The outcome is a growing wave of buyers who understand the fundamentals of the inner east corridor and are backing Murarrie's proximity to those established suburbs as a long-term investment decision.

The Gateway Motorway access from Murarrie is one of its most practical assets. For buyers who commute to the airport, the Gold Coast, or the northern employment corridors, Murarrie reduces travel time in a way that comparable inner east suburbs cannot match. That access is valued and is reflected in the investor and professional buyer demand for the suburb.

The housing stock is varied, which is part of Murarrie's appeal for buyers at different budget levels. The older character homes on larger blocks are the premium tier. The 1970s and 1980s brick homes on standard lots are the accessible entry point. The newer townhouse developments serve the buyer who wants inner east proximity with lower maintenance.

Winter in Murarrie tends to be quieter than autumn but not absent. The buyers who are active in May and June in this suburb are typically investors with clear criteria, or families who have been watching the suburb specifically and are ready to move when the right property appears.
