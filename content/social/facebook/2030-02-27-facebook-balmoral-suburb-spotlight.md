## Caption

Balmoral's autumn campaign track record is one of the more consistent in the inner east. The suburb's character homes photograph particularly well in the softer February and March light, and the buyer pool for premium family homes in this location tends to be at its most active in the first quarter of the year. For sellers who've been considering a campaign, the timing argument for acting now is solid.

What's been selling at the upper end of Balmoral's market is the product that's hardest to replicate: homes with any river view or corridor aspect, character retained to a high standard, and land content that's meaningfully different from what you find at similar prices in comparable suburbs. Those properties have generated competitive bidding in recent campaigns.

The supply side in Balmoral remains genuinely constrained. The suburb has a settled, long-term ownership profile. Buyers who want Balmoral know they need to be ready to act when something suitable comes to market, and that readiness is reflected in how quickly well-prepared homes move.

Median house prices are around $1.8M. Check current CoreLogic or PropTrack data before relying on that figure.

If you own in Balmoral and have been watching the market for the right time, this autumn window is worth taking seriously. The link below is the right starting point.
