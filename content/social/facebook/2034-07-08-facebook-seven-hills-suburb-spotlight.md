## Caption

Seven Hills is the inner east suburb that rewards the buyer who looks past the suburb name. It sits between Mt Gravatt East and Coorparoo, with elevation, larger-than-average block sizes, and a housing stock that still offers genuine value relative to the better-known suburbs on either side of it.

The appeal is partly location: Seven Hills is close to Greenslopes Hospital, has strong access to the Pacific Motorway, and sits inside a commute to the CBD that most inner east buyers find acceptable. It also has a few streets with elevated outlooks that would command a premium if they were located in Norman Park or Camp Hill.

The buyer profile here includes families seeking more land for the price, owner-occupiers who prioritise indoor space and practical living over proximity to a café strip, and buyers priced out of adjacent suburbs who have done enough research to understand what Seven Hills actually offers.
