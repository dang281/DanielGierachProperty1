## Caption

Cannon Hill sits 9km east of the CBD and has spent the better part of a decade closing the gap with Morningside and Murarrie as the inner east's preferred family entry point.

The suburb's appeal is practical and consistent. The train line connects directly to the CBD in around 20 minutes. The local shopping and services precinct is established and convenient. The residential streets are quiet and well-maintained, with a mix of original character homes and renovated properties that reflect the broader inner-east trajectory.

What has driven Cannon Hill's growth is a combination of the relative price gap to Morningside to the north and Camp Hill to the south, and ongoing infrastructure investment in the Wynnum Road and Cannon Hill corridors. Buyers who have been priced out of adjacent suburbs have driven consistent demand, and that demand has lifted the market steadily rather than in a single spike.

For sellers in Cannon Hill, the market has matured to a point where well-presented properties command competitive outcomes. The buyer pool is broad enough to produce genuine competition when the pricing is right.
