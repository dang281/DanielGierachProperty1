## Caption

Holland Park is an elevated suburb with a consistently strong family owner-occupier base and a price point that still represents genuine value against the closer inner east suburbs. The post-war homes here have solid bones, good block sizes, and renovation headroom that buyers understand when they start looking seriously.

Logan Road gives residents reliable bus access to the CBD, and Mount Gravatt and Cavendish Road are both within easy reach for shopping, dining, and the services that families use regularly. Holland Park State School is a well-regarded local option, and the proximity to the Princess Alexandra Hospital employment corridor is a practical pull for buyers in healthcare and allied health fields.

The development potential in certain pockets of Holland Park is worth noting for owners with larger blocks. Some streets carry dual-occupancy or small-lot subdivision potential that adds a layer of buyer depth beyond the standard family buyer, and that broader buyer competition supports results at the top of the range.

For sellers, Holland Park sits at an interesting point: close enough to the inner east to draw buyers who have been looking further north, but priced at a point that keeps demand strong and consistent. The buyers here tend to be committed and ready.

If you own in Holland Park and are considering selling, reach out.
