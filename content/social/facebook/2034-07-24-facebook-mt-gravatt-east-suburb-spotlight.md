## Caption

Mt Gravatt East sits on the southern edge of Brisbane's inner east and offers buyers something that is increasingly difficult to find at its price point: land.

Block sizes here are among the largest in the inner east, the streets are established and leafy, and the suburb has a quiet residential character that contrasts with the more commercially active suburbs along the ridge. Griffith University is close, making Mt Gravatt East a consistent location for academics and professional households who want to live near the campus without paying Toowong prices.

The buyer competition in Mt Gravatt East tends toward families and long-term investors. The combination of land size, school access, and proximity to the Westfield Mt Gravatt precinct makes the suburb a practical choice for buyers who have done the research.

Properties in the elevated streets with western views and those with rear access or usable large blocks attract particular attention. Presentation matters, but the land story often carries the result.
