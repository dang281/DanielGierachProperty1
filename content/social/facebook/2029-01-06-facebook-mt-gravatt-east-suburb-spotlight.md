## Caption

Mt Gravatt East sits in a part of Brisbane's inner east that does not always get the same attention as the riverside suburbs, but the buyers who know it understand exactly what they are paying for.

The suburb is anchored by Westfield Garden City to the south and the greenery of Toohey Forest Park to the west. Families come for the school options, particularly the proximity to Padua College, Villanova College, and Seton College. Investors come for the rental demand that those schools generate year-round.

Homes in Mt Gravatt East range from original 1960s and 1970s houses on large blocks to more recent townhouse and unit developments along the main corridors. The large block holdings are what draws the most buyer competition: families who want to knock down or renovate and stay in the area for the long term.

Days on market for well-priced detached houses in Mt Gravatt East tend to track below the broader Brisbane average. Buyer enquiry is consistent because the suburb rarely produces enough quality listings to satisfy the demand that is always there.
