## Caption

Carina sits between two suburbs that get more attention, and that's part of what makes it interesting.

East of Carina Heights and west of Carindale, it occupies a position in the inner south-east that is well serviced, affordable relative to the suburbs closer to the city, and increasingly attractive to buyers who have done the calculation and found it stacks up.

The suburb is predominantly residential with a mix of post-war homes and more recent builds. Carindale shopping centre is a short drive. The Whites Hill corridor provides recreation access. The Gateway Motorway connection to the north and south makes the suburb practical for buyers who commute in different directions.

Carina has historically traded at a modest discount to Carina Heights, a pattern that has compressed as buyer demand from the inner east has pushed further out along the corridor. Median house prices have moved up around 13% over the past year, reflecting that spillover demand from buyers who started their search in Camp Hill or Carindale and found Carina is where the value remains.

For owners who purchased five or more years ago, that growth has translated to a meaningfully different equity position.
