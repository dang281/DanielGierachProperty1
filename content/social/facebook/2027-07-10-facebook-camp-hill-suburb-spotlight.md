## Caption

Camp Hill has developed a reputation in Brisbane's inner east that its median price does not fully reflect. The suburb sits on elevated terrain with some of the best views over the city corridor available in the inner east, and homeowners who have owned there for more than a few years tend to be reluctant to leave.

The character of Camp Hill is partly defined by its ridge-line streets. Properties on the high side of the suburb offer outlooks that drive genuine buyer competition, and buyers who discover those views for the first time tend to act quickly when a well-presented property becomes available. The lower-lying pockets of Camp Hill are more affordable entry points into the suburb but attract a different buyer profile.

Camp Hill State School is a consistent driver of buyer interest for families with primary school-aged children, and the suburb's proximity to Holland Park West and Coorparoo without the same pricing of those addresses makes it attractive to buyers who have done their research.

If you own in Camp Hill and you are thinking about what your property is worth in the current market, I would be happy to walk you through it. No obligation, just an honest conversation about where things sit.
