## Caption

Mt Gravatt carries a dual identity that is worth understanding if you own here. The Logan Road and Cavendish Road commercial and retail activity gives the suburb strong day-to-day amenity, while the quieter residential streets behind the main roads offer a genuinely liveable family environment that buyers discover once they look past the commercial frontage.

Westfield Garden City is within easy reach, the TAFE campus and Griffith University provide employment and education anchors, and the public transport access on the main corridors is reliable. For buyers who prioritise access over suburb prestige, Mt Gravatt consistently ranks well.

The residential streets away from the main roads carry a post-war and character home mix on blocks that are well priced relative to suburbs further north. Investors and owner-occupiers both operate in this market, which keeps the buyer pool broad and the enquiry consistent regardless of market conditions. That buyer breadth is a genuine strength for sellers.

For long-term owners in the residential pockets who have held through the commercial growth of the surrounding area, the position is a strong one. The suburb's commercial profile brings foot traffic and amenity that benefits residential values without disrupting the character of the quieter streets.

If you own in Mt Gravatt and are considering selling, reach out.
