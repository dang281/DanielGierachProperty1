## Caption

Carina is a suburb that rewards buyers who are willing to look past the surface. The blocks are larger than what you find in most inner-east suburbs, the Carina Leagues Club gives the community an established social anchor, and Cavendish Road feeds directly into the Runcorn corridor for those who commute south. It is not a suburb that shouts, but it consistently delivers for the buyers who choose it.

The housing stock is older, which is both the challenge and the draw. Many homes sit on allotments that give buyers genuine renovation scope or the option to knock down and build to a size that the inner suburbs simply cannot accommodate at the same price. Upgraders from Cannon Hill and Mt Gravatt make up a meaningful share of the buyer pool. They know the area, they know the commute routes, and they are looking for more space without moving further out.

School options in the catchment are solid, and the proximity to Garden City via Cavendish Road adds retail convenience. Buyers in Carina are generally practical. They have done the suburb comparisons and decided the value case is clear.

If you own in Carina and are considering a sale, the block size and land content story is worth understanding in the context of the current market. Get the detail at danielgierach.com/suburbs/carina
