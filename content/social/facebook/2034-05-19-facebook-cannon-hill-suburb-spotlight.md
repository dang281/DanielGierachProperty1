## Caption

Cannon Hill's rail connection is one of its most underrated assets. The Cannon Hill Station sits on the Cleveland line with direct services to Central and Roma Street. For buyers who are commuting into the CBD and want to avoid the morning traffic on Old Cleveland Road, rail access from this suburb is reliable and direct. The combination of rail connectivity and Gateway Motorway proximity gives Cannon Hill a transport flexibility that most comparable inner east suburbs at this price range cannot match.

The Cannon Hill Anglican College presence in the suburb drives a specific and consistent buyer pool. Families who are specifically choosing the college for their children's education narrow their address search to the walkable radius. That buyer pool is well-informed and motivated, and it shows in the enquiry that comes through for Cannon Hill properties that sit within practical walking distance of the school.

The Cannon Hill Shopping Centre handles the daily retail requirements without requiring residents to travel to a major shopping destination for routine purchases. The anchor tenants have stabilised the precinct and the convenience proposition is one that families with multiple practical demands appreciate in a primary school age lifestyle.

The housing stock in Cannon Hill ranges from established post-war character homes to more recent construction, with renovation activity across both the older and newer segments keeping the street presentation standard reasonably high.
