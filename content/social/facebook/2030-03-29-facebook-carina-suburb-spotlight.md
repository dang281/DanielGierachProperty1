## Caption

The Carina market at the end of March has a different texture to how it started the year. The buyers who arrived in January with general interest have either bought something or sharpened their criteria. What remains is a pool of buyers who are specific about what they want: a full block, a property with genuine renovation scope, and a price that reflects Carina's value position rather than neighbouring suburbs' prices.

Sellers who understand that buyer's mindset are the ones positioning their campaigns well right now. Carina is not a suburb where you can present a cosmetically touched-up home and expect the same result as a well-prepared original home with clear potential. The buyer here is doing their own sums, and they need to see the raw material to justify the number.

What's been selling in Carina at the top of the market this quarter is the original brick home on a proper block in the streets that buyers know as the better ones. When those homes are presented with the right approach, they generate interest from multiple buyers at once, and that's when results move above expectations.

Median house prices are around $1.1M. Verify via CoreLogic or PropTrack before relying on it.

If you own in Carina and this is the quarter you've been waiting for, the remaining weeks of the autumn window are meaningful. The link below is where to start.
