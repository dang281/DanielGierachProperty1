## Caption

Seven Hills is one of Brisbane's inner-east suburbs that still offers genuine value relative to its neighbours, which is exactly why buyer interest has remained steady through this winter period. The suburb's hillside positioning delivers elevated outlooks that buyers in Camp Hill and Coorparoo have long sought, but at a price point that often represents better buying.

Character homes here range from original Queenslanders on larger blocks to more recent renovations, and the mix attracts a broad pool: young families, downsizers looking to stay local, and investors who recognise the rental appeal of proximity to Greenslopes and the PA Hospital precinct.

Norman Park and Camp Hill State School catchments add another layer of appeal for family buyers. Homes on the ridge with views across the suburb tend to draw the strongest competition at open homes.

If you own in Seven Hills and want a clear read on where the market sits today, a straightforward appraisal conversation is the right starting point.
