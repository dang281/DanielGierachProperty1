## Caption

Carina's appeal is practical. Good access to the Gateway Motorway and the Pacific Motorway junction, proximity to Westfield Carindale, and a housing stock that offers larger allotments at prices that remain below the inner suburbs closer to the river. The buyer who values that combination is specific about what they want and tends to move purposefully when the right property appears.

The suburb has a high proportion of long-held owner-occupied homes, which means the turnover of well-presented properties is relatively low. When a quality home in a good Carina street comes to market, it typically attracts the subset of buyers who have been watching the suburb with intent.

Investor demand in Carina is supported by the PA Hospital and Greenslopes Private employment precinct to the west, and the retail and commercial employment along Old Cleveland Road. Rental vacancy in the suburb has remained consistently low, which matters to buyers who are assessing the investment case.
