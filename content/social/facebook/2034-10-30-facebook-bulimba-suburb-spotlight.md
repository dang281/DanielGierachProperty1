## Caption

Bulimba is one of the few inner east suburbs where the lifestyle case is as strong as the property fundamentals. Oxford Street, the river, the ferry connections to the CBD, and the character of the built form all combine to create a suburb that buyers actively aspire to. That aspiration is reflected in the prices and in the consistency of demand even when other inner east suburbs have softened.

The suburb's entry price point has moved significantly over the past decade, which has changed the buyer profile. The first-home buyer who was purchasing in Bulimba in the early 2010s is now more likely to be found in Morningside or Cannon Hill. The current Bulimba buyer is typically upsizing from elsewhere in the inner east, or is an interstate buyer drawn specifically to the Oxford Street lifestyle and the river access.

The character homes in the streets closest to the river and the Oxford Street café precinct attract the most competitive buyer pools. Properties with a view, a renovated interior, and access to the ferry consistently achieve at the top of the suburb's price range.
