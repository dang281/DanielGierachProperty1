## Caption

Holland Park's elevation gives it a character that the flat land suburbs between it and the river do not share. The streets that run along the ridgeline through Holland Park carry the kind of morning sun access and aspect that buyers who have done their inner east research identify as a differentiating factor. For buyers who have been looking across the inner east mid-band and have learned to distinguish between flat and elevated stock, Holland Park sits clearly in the elevated category.

The suburb's position gives practical access to both the CBD corridor via the Pacific Motorway and the Coorparoo and Camp Hill retail amenity strips without being on either of the main arterial routes. For families who want the convenience of the nearby commercial precincts without the traffic that those strips carry, Holland Park's residential streets are quieter than the address might suggest.

The Holland Park West State School catchment anchors a consistent family buyer pool that is present through every season. For families who are making an address decision based on the state school option, the catchment boundary drives their suburb consideration. That buyer type does their due diligence thoroughly and when they identify a property in the right catchment that meets their requirements, they move with conviction.

Properties in Holland Park that are renovated to current standards and presented well attract genuine competition from a motivated buyer pool in any month of the year.
