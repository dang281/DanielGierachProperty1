## Caption

Morningside is one of Brisbane's most sought-after inner-east suburbs, and the sales data keeps reinforcing why.

Wynnum Road effectively divides two price points within the suburb. North of Wynnum Road, elevated blocks with river views and larger character homes consistently attract premium results. South of Wynnum Road, the value proposition is different but the demand is just as real, with families and couples targeting the suburb for its proximity to Oxford Street Bulimba, Norman Park, and the broader inner-east lifestyle corridor.

The suburb is close to everything that makes the inner east desirable. Oxford Street in Bulimba is minutes away for dining and weekend markets. The Norman Park ferry terminal connects residents to the CBD without touching a car. The school options, both state and private, are within practical reach.

Auction is the dominant method of sale in Morningside, and clearance rates have been consistently strong. When well-presented homes come to market here, they rarely sit long. The buyer pool is deep, well-qualified, and motivated.

Median house price sits around $1.65 million, with northern premium properties regularly transacting above $2 million. The unit market is also active, drawing buyers who want the Morningside address at a more accessible entry point.
