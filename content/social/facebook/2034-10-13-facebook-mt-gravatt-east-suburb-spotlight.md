## Caption

Mt Gravatt East sits between the commercial activity of Garden City and the bushland around Mt Gravatt Conservation Reserve, which gives it a character that is noticeably different from the flat suburban streets of most of the inner south east. The topography creates elevated blocks with views across the city and the ranges, and those views are consistently reflected in the prices achieved for homes that capture them.

The suburb has attracted a mix of long-term owner-occupiers, buyers who have relocated from inner suburbs in search of more space, and investors who value the low vacancy rates supported by Griffith University and the nearby medical precinct.

Buyer demand in Mt Gravatt East has been notably steady even in periods when the broader market has slowed. The combination of the conservation reserve amenity, the school catchments, and the proximity to major employment nodes keeps the suburb on a short list for buyers who have been searching the inner south for any length of time.
