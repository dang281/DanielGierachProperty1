## Caption

Balmoral's spring market is characterised by low supply and sustained demand from buyers who have been waiting. The suburb sees relatively few listings in any given quarter, which means each well-presented property that comes to market draws a concentrated field of interested buyers. That dynamic has produced consistently strong results for Balmoral sellers across recent spring seasons.

The suburb's appeal is anchored in its physical character: large allotments, Queenslander and post-war housing stock, proximity to the river and Whites Hill Reserve, and a quiet residential atmosphere that is unusual given how close Balmoral sits to Oxford Street and the inner-city amenity of Hawthorne. Buyers who find Balmoral rarely look elsewhere.

Families are drawn to the Balmoral State School catchment and the private school proximity. Downsizers from larger properties are attracted by the lifestyle and the ability to stay within the inner east.

If you own in Balmoral and are weighing a spring sale, a current appraisal gives you the information you need to make a well-timed decision.
