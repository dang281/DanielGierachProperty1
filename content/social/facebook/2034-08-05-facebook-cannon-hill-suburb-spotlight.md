## Caption

Cannon Hill is one of the inner east's most practical suburbs, and the buyer profile reflects that directly. Proximity to the Cannon Hill Shopping Centre, strong bus and train access via the Cannon Hill railway station, and a housing stock that offers reasonable value for the inner east location combine to make this suburb a consistent performer.

The suburb sits between Murarrie and Morningside, and buyers who have been considering these adjacent suburbs often arrive at Cannon Hill after realising the value proposition is strong and the lifestyle tradeoff is minimal. Coles, a cinema, dining, and hardware in one precinct is an unusual convenience for an inner east suburb.

Properties close to the railway station attract a specific buyer: the household where at least one person commutes to the CBD by train daily. The convenience premium on these properties is real and observable in sales data. Streets in the suburb's northern residential pocket, away from the commercial activity, tend to attract families looking for quiet streets without sacrificing the suburb's practical assets.
