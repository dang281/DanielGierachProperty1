## Caption

East Brisbane is one of those suburbs where the fundamentals have been pointing in the same direction for a long time, and the market keeps reflecting that.

At around 4 kilometres from the CBD, it is close enough to walk or cycle to work for many professionals. Mowbray Park on the river gives residents a genuine green space that inner-city buyers often have to sacrifice. The mix of worker's cottages undergoing high-end renovation alongside fully transformed character homes means there is always a project for the right buyer, and always a polished product for those who want to move straight in.

The buyer profile here is mostly professionals and downsizers. Families with older children also target East Brisbane for the walkability and the short school commutes into the CBD private school belt. Demand at the premium end stays firm because supply is genuinely constrained, the suburb is not large and the period housing stock is finite.

Median house price is tracking around $1.6 million, with some well-presented character homes on good blocks pushing well beyond that. Units and townhouses offer a lower entry point for buyers who want the postcode without the outright house budget.

If you own in East Brisbane and have been sitting on the fence about the timing of a sale, autumn stock levels are telling you something worth acting on.
