## Caption

Cannon Hill is well positioned in a way that does not always get the recognition it deserves. The Gateway Motorway access point is among the most convenient of any inner east suburb, which matters to buyers who are looking at total commute time across multiple work locations rather than just CBD proximity. Brisbane Airport is accessible in under 20 minutes without navigating the inner ring. For buyers with connections to the airport or the logistics precinct at Murarrie, Cannon Hill is a specific address preference, not a default.

Cannon Hill Anglican College is the school proximity factor that drives a meaningful portion of the buyer pool to research Cannon Hill as a family address. The college catchment interaction with the broader Anglican schools corridor in the inner east makes specific streets within Cannon Hill more sought after than others, and buyers who are choosing based on school access know exactly which side of which road they want to be on.

The suburb's housing stock ranges from post-war character homes on larger blocks to more recent infill development. The established streets closest to the Cannon Hill Shopping Centre and the rail station offer the combination of walkability and land content that family buyers respond to strongly.

Demand has been consistent through this autumn across the family home category and open home attendance has been solid for well-presented properties.
