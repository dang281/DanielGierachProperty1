## Caption

Mt Gravatt is one of the larger inner east suburbs by area, and the property market here reflects the range that comes with size. From compact post-war homes on modest blocks near Westfield to larger elevated properties on the hillside, the suburb covers a broad price spectrum and attracts a diverse buyer pool.

The proximity to Westfield Mt Gravatt is the suburb's daily-living anchor. Grocery, dining, and retail in one place. The Eastern Busway connection provides CBD access without relying on a car. Griffith University is close enough to create consistent rental demand in the right property type.

The elevated streets in Mt Gravatt, particularly those running along the ridge with northerly outlooks, attract premium interest. Buyers who have been priced out of Coorparoo or Camp Hill often discover that the views and land available in Mt Gravatt's upper streets represent a genuine value proposition.
