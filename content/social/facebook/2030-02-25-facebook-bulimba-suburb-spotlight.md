## Caption

The Bulimba autumn market has a rhythm to it that sellers who've sold here before tend to recognise. Listings that go live in late February and March are meeting a buyer pool that's been building since Christmas, and the competition for genuinely good homes in this suburb is consistent year after year. The lifestyle case for Bulimba doesn't need to be made again. What matters for sellers now is execution.

What's selling at the top of the market here is clear: river-corridor properties, homes on the flat side of the suburb within walking distance of Oxford Street, and well-presented character homes on blocks that give families genuine outdoor space. The buyer who can afford Bulimba has choices, and they choose based on quality of presentation and confidence in the home's condition.

Days on market for well-prepared Bulimba homes is short. The suburb's consistent low supply of quality listings means that when something good appears, the buyer pool doesn't need to think for long. That's the dynamic that has driven strong results here year after year.

Median house prices are around $1.9M. Confirm current data via CoreLogic or PropTrack before relying on that figure.
