## Caption

Camp Hill is consistently one of the most sought-after suburbs in Brisbane's inner east, and the driver is the same each year: it is one of the few inner east suburbs where character homes, a genuine café and dining strip, and a primary school with strong community standing all sit within a ten-minute walk of each other.

Martha Street is the suburb's commercial anchor. The housing stock along the ridge and in the streets below it covers a wide range: Queenslanders, post-war brick, and more recent character-influenced builds. The school catchment, Camp Hill State Infants and Primary, contributes materially to demand from young families who treat it as a non-negotiable part of their suburb selection.

Properties in the higher streets with views and access to the walking tracks at Whites Hill or near the Martha Street strip consistently achieve strong results at auction. Days on market in Camp Hill are typically among the lowest in the inner east.
