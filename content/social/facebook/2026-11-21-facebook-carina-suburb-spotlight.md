## Caption

Carina keeps attracting buyers who thought they had priced themselves out of the inner east.

The suburb sits east of Coorparoo and south of Morningside, inside the inner ring but with entry prices that remain lower than both of its better-known neighbours. That positioning has made it the destination for buyers with a clear brief (four bedrooms, a real backyard, proximity to good schools) who have found those criteria progressively harder to satisfy in Camp Hill and Coorparoo.

The school catchment is central to the Carina story. Properties within the Citipointe Christian College zone and within range of Carina State School attract a specific buyer who has done their research and is not going to be moved on price easily. That catchment premium is measurable in how quickly well-positioned properties in this zone sell compared to the rest of the suburb.

The Carina Plaza precinct and the broader Old Cleveland Road corridor give residents solid day-to-day convenience without requiring car trips to a major shopping centre. The bus and road access to the CBD runs efficiently along the corridor.

For owners in Carina who have been watching what their neighbours have sold for in 2026, the conditions have been consistently stronger than the suburb's price point typically suggests.
