## Caption

Balmoral sits between Bulimba and Hawthorne on the river and shares their character home appeal without quite the same commercial activity on its doorstep. For buyers who want the Bulimba or Hawthorne lifestyle with more privacy and larger block sizes, Balmoral is usually the answer.

The housing stock is among the most diverse in the inner east. Pre-war timber homes on large blocks, post-war brick, renovated character homes with contemporary extensions, and newer infill. Streets close to the river and those with Wynnum Road separation tend to sit in noticeably different price bands.

Balmoral State School is a significant driver of demand, and families with primary-aged children form a consistent buyer cohort. The school's catchment boundary is something buyers in this market know before they book their first inspection.

For homeowners in Balmoral thinking about where your property sits in the current market, start at danielgierach.com/suburbs/balmoral.
