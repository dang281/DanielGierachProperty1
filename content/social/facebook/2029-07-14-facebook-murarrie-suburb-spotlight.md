## Caption

Murarrie is the inner east suburb undergoing the most visible transformation. What was largely an industrial and workers cottage mix a decade ago is now a suburb with active development applications, new residential product, and a buyer profile that has broadened considerably.

The established residential streets still carry that post-war character: solid cottages on decent land, often long held by families who arrived when the suburb was primarily a working one. Those long-hold properties are the ones attracting the most considered attention right now, because the underlying land value is shifting as rezoning opens more of the suburb to residential use.

Gateway Motorway access is a genuine selling point for buyers who work in logistics, trade, or the Port precinct. Murarrie Recreation Reserve gives the suburb a green anchor, and the proximity to Cannon Hill and Tingalpa means buyers who miss out in those suburbs often redirect their search here. That overflow demand has been consistent and is not going away.

For owners who bought here years ago and have held through the transition, the current market represents a position most long-term holders rarely find themselves in: strong land value, genuine buyer depth, and a suburb still mid-way through its change.

If you own in Murarrie and are considering selling, reach out.
