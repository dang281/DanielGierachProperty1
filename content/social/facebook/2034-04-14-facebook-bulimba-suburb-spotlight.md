## Caption

Bulimba is a suburb that buyers choose rather than compromise toward. The Oxford Street café and restaurant strip, the ferry terminal with direct river access to the CBD, and the riverside park amenity combine to create a lifestyle proposition that sustains a buyer pool willing to pay a premium over comparable inner east addresses.

The Oxford Street precinct is Bulimba's defining feature. It has the density of good food and coffee options that walkable urban living requires, the independent retail that characterises a suburb with strong community identity, and an atmosphere that draws residents from surrounding suburbs to spend time here even when they live elsewhere. Buyers who are choosing between Bulimba and a comparable property in Norman Park or Camp Hill are often making a conscious lifestyle choice to pay for the precinct access.

The Bulimba ferry terminal changes the CBD commute calculation. The CityCat and cross-river ferry services mean that residents who choose river transport can be at the cultural precinct end of the city in under 20 minutes without the stress of road traffic.

Property values in Bulimba are among the highest in the inner east. The premium is consistent and it has been maintained across market cycles because the underlying lifestyle proposition that drives it does not change with interest rates or sentiment shifts.
