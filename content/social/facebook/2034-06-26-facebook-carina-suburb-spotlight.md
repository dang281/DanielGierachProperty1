## Caption

Carina's case for vendors who are considering a winter campaign is straightforward. The suburb's demand profile is driven by buyers who are making a long-term family address decision, and those buyers do not pause their search for winter. A family that is committed to the inner east for the schools, the land content, and the CBD access is searching in June with the same motivation they brought to their February search. The number of buyers at any given open home is lower, but the proportion of those buyers who are genuinely ready to act is higher.

The value proposition that Carina offers in the inner east context remains consistent through the year. Free-standing houses on established blocks at pricing that sits below the closer-in suburbs create a demand that the inner east continues to generate regardless of interest rate movements or seasonal patterns.

The Old Cleveland Road corridor through Carina carries the daily retail and services that resident families use without requiring a trip to a larger centre. The Westfield Carindale access from the eastern edge of the suburb adds the larger-format retail for the weekly shop and the occasional leisure visit. That combination of convenience tiers is a practical arrangement that families with children specifically value.

Carina's owner-occupier character is evident in the quality of the housing stock that comes to market. Properties held by families for ten or more years before sale typically reflect that period of occupation in their presentation and maintenance.
