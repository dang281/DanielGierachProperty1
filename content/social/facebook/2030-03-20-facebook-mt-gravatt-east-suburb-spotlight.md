## Caption

By mid-March, Mt Gravatt East's autumn market is typically running well. School enrolment decisions have been made, and the families who wanted to be in the Cavendish Road State High School catchment and didn't find what they needed in January or February are still active. That ongoing buyer pressure means sellers who come to market now are not missing the window.

What's been generating competition in Mt Gravatt East is the original housing on the elevated blocks with meaningful views. Those properties, which might have been considered modest in a different time, are now attracting buyers who see the combination of land content, outlook, and school catchment as something worth paying for. The Seton College and Padua College proximity adds another dimension for families with Catholic schooling preferences.

The renovation buyer is a consistent presence in Mt Gravatt East. The suburb's price point relative to Coorparoo or Camp Hill makes it one of the more rational choices for buyers who want to buy at a discount and invest in improvement.

Median house prices are around $1.2M. Verify this via CoreLogic or PropTrack before relying on it.
