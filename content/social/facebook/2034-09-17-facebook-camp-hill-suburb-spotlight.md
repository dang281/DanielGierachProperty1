## Caption

Camp Hill has consolidated its position as one of the inner east's most consistent performers. The suburb delivers what families and professionals consistently search for: elevated streets with city glimpses, character Queenslanders with room to extend, walkability to Old Cleveland Road's cafes and shops, and well-regarded school catchments including Camp Hill State Infants and Primary and Churchie nearby.

One of Camp Hill's defining traits is the calibre of renovation work across the suburb. Buyers arriving from Sydney or Melbourne regularly note that the standard of presentation in Camp Hill compares favourably with what they are used to at similar price points. That benchmark sets expectations at open homes and sustains strong competition for well-prepared listings.

Spring is when Camp Hill tends to produce the quarter's standout results. Buyers who have been watching the suburb closely through winter are typically pre-approved and ready to move when the right property appears in September or October.
