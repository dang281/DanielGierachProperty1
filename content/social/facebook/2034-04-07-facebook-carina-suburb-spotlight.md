## Caption

Carina is a suburb that delivers strong fundamentals at a price point that remains accessible relative to its inner east neighbours. Buyers who have researched Camp Hill, Carindale, and Carina Heights before arriving at Carina have typically worked their way through the surrounding price range and found that Carina offers comparable land content and family liveability at a more considered entry point.

The suburb's practical drawcards are well established. Old Cleveland Road runs through Carina and provides direct access to the CBD corridor. Westfield Carindale is accessible without a highway trip. The school catchment includes options that attract deliberate family buyers: access to Cavendish Road State High School is a specific filter for buyers who have researched secondary education options across the inner east.

The housing stock is predominantly post-war, with established blocks and the kind of land content that is increasingly scarce in inner east suburbs where development has consumed subdivision potential. Buyers who are looking for a free-standing house on a genuine block within 10 kilometres of the CBD are finding fewer options at the price range that Carina offers.

The owner-occupier base is strong and the suburb has absorbed consistent demand from families trading up within the inner east without the price acceleration that has characterised the more premium addresses.
