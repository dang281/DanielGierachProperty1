## Caption

Cannon Hill sits at the intersection of convenience and value in the inner east. The M3 motorway is close, Cannon Hill Plaza handles the day-to-day shopping, and Morningside and Bulimba are near enough to draw on their lifestyle without paying their prices. That combination has kept buyer interest steady here for several years.

The housing stock is largely post-war homes on decent blocks, the kind of property that suits both owner-occupiers looking for a project and investors who want land content and yield at a price point that makes the numbers work. Median house prices sit around $1.15M, though this should be verified against current CoreLogic or PropTrack data before you rely on it.

The investor interest in Cannon Hill has been real and consistent. Rental demand from workers commuting via the motorway corridor, combined with proximity to the Morningside activity centre, has meant vacancy rates have generally remained low.

For families, the suburb offers something that Murarrie and Seven Hills sometimes can't quite match: a well-established street network, mature trees, and a settled residential feel that makes it easy to picture yourself there long-term.
