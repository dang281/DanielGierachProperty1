## Caption

Cannon Hill is a suburb that is often underestimated by buyers who approach it without having done their homework. The proximity to the Cannon Hill Kmart and Aldi retail precinct, the access to the Inner City Bypass and Gateway Motorway, and the Cannon Hill Palms Cinema cluster make it one of the more convenient inner east suburbs to live in on a daily basis.

The property market here attracts a practical buyer who values access and liveability over café strip proximity. Families, investors, and buyers who have been priced out of the suburbs immediately to the west all appear regularly in Cannon Hill open homes. The price point is meaningful relative to Camp Hill or Norman Park, and the gap has attracted buyers who recognise the value.

The housing stock in Cannon Hill includes a mix of pre-war character homes, 1960s to 1980s brick and tile, and more recently built infill development. The character homes in the central streets attract the renovation buyer. The established brick homes attract the set-and-forget owner-occupier and the investor.

For sellers in Cannon Hill, understanding which buyer profile your property attracts is the most useful starting point. danielgierach.com/suburbs/cannon-hill is where that conversation begins.
