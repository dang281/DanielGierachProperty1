## Caption

Hawthorne's property market is one of the most consistently performing in the inner east, and it is driven by a specific combination of factors that does not replicate easily elsewhere.

The Hawthorne ferry and the river frontage give the suburb a connection to the CBD and the South Bank that most inner east suburbs do not have. Buyers who discover the 15-minute ferry commute tend to become committed Hawthorne buyers because the alternative is a bus or a car, and for the people who value the commute quality, the river connection is worth paying for.

The streets between Hawthorne Road and the river are where the highest Hawthorne prices are found. The character homes in this precinct, many of them well-executed renovations of original Queenslanders, attract buyers at the premium end of the inner east market. These are properties that rarely discount once properly launched, because the buyer who wants them knows what they are looking for and has been watching for it.

The broader Hawthorne market, the streets toward Old Cleveland Road and the properties that fall into the middle and upper-middle price range, has a consistent buyer pool of families who want the suburb's credentials without the river-proximity premium. This segment performs solidly through most market conditions.
