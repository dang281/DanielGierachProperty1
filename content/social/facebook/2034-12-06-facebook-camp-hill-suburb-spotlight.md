## Caption

Camp Hill sits in one of the most consistent parts of Brisbane's inner east property market. The suburb has maintained a strong buyer profile across multiple market cycles, driven by a combination of character home architecture, school catchments that parents actively seek out, and a position that gives access to both the CBD corridor and the Coorparoo and Greenslopes retail strips.

The buyer for a three or four-bedroom character home in Camp Hill is one of the most consistent in the inner east. They have typically been active in the market for several months, know the comparable sales in detail, and will move quickly when the right property appears. When two of these buyers identify the same property, the competition at auction tends to reflect the genuine value both have independently concluded.

Camp Hill's price range has compressed somewhat relative to Hawthorne and Balmoral in recent years as the inner east has repriced broadly. That compression has kept buyers in Camp Hill who might previously have crossed the suburb boundary, which maintains demand.
