## Caption

Carindale is a suburb where the practical case for living there is clear. Westfield Carindale handles major retail and dining. Logan Road and Old Cleveland Road provide everyday access. The Gateway Motorway and South East Motorway put both the CBD and the Gold Coast within commuting range. Families who live here do not sacrifice convenience; they trade inner-city density for space and the infrastructure works in their favour.

The housing stock runs from post-war originals through to 1990s brick and tile family homes, and the market here rewards properties that have been maintained, updated, or extended. A renovated four-bedroom home with a pool on a proper suburban block in Carindale occupies a price point that is genuinely difficult to match in suburbs 5km closer to the CBD. That relative value proposition is what brings buyers from inner suburbs and from interstate. [VERIFY: current median house price before posting.]

The buyer profile is weighted toward families, both established local families upsizing and interstate or overseas buyers relocating and prioritising space and motorway access. Investor activity is present in the suburb but secondary to owner-occupier demand. Rental demand from workers in the eastern suburbs corridor keeps vacancy rates low for investors who do enter the market.

Properties in Carindale that are clean, well-presented, and priced within the market expectation attract buyers who have been active in the area for some time.
