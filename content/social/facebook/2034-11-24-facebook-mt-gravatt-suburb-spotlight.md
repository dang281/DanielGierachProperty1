## Caption

Mt Gravatt is a suburb that performs consistently rather than spectacularly, and that consistency is worth something in a market where some suburbs spike and correct. The buyer profile here is broad: families who want access to Garden City and Westfield, professionals who work at the Prince Charles or PA Hospital precincts, and investors who value the low vacancy supported by Griffith University student accommodation demand.

The commercial strips on Mains Road and Logan Road give Mt Gravatt walkable retail access that the purely residential streets of the outer suburbs cannot match. That access, combined with good freeway connections in multiple directions, makes the suburb functional for a wide range of household types.

Properties at the Mt Gravatt elevation with northerly or city-facing aspects consistently achieve at the top of the suburb's price range. The view premium in this suburb is meaningful and buyers who want it are willing to pay for it.
