## Caption

Seven Hills is the inner east suburb that consistently surprises buyers when they first look at it seriously.

The suburb sits between Coorparoo to the west and Morningside to the north, with the Eastern Freeway providing rapid access to the CBD and the airport corridor. The housing stock is predominantly owner-occupied, the streets are established and leafy, and the turn-over rate is low. When a Seven Hills property hits the market, it tends to attract the buyer who already knows the suburb from driving through it and has been waiting for the right listing.

The school catchment dynamic in Seven Hills is well understood by the family buyer pool. The proximity to the Coorparoo and Norman Park school precincts, combined with the access to the private school corridor on Old Cleveland Road, makes Seven Hills an efficient location for families managing school-age children across different year levels.

The land value in Seven Hills has tracked closely with Coorparoo and Camp Hill over recent years. Large blocks on the higher ground, particularly those with easterly outlook, consistently attract the strongest prices. The buyers competing for those blocks are generally doing so with a renovation or rebuild intention.
