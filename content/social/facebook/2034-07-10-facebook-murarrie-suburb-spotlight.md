## Caption

Murarrie is the inner east suburb most buyers encounter later than they should. It borders Cannon Hill and Hemmant, has direct access to the Gateway Motorway, and is one of the closest suburbs to the airport corridor without being impacted by flight paths in the same way.

The residential pockets of Murarrie are quieter than the industrial fringe suggests from the outside. Block sizes are generous, properties sit on wider streets, and the proximity to Cannon Hill Shopping Centre and the Lytton Road corridor makes daily logistics easy.

The buyer profile here tends toward value-focused owner-occupiers and investors who understand that Murarrie's undervaluation relative to Cannon Hill and Morningside is a feature, not a reflection of anything the suburb lacks. As surrounding suburbs continue to press upward in price, the attention on Murarrie follows.
