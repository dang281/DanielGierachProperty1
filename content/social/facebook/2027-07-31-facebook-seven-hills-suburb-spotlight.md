## Caption

Seven Hills is one of those inner east suburbs that tends to be discovered rather than sought out. Buyers who have focused their search on Coorparoo or Holland Park often find themselves in Seven Hills through an open home and quickly understand why residents who live there are reluctant to leave.

The suburb offers some of the best elevated positions in the inner east, with ridge-line properties enjoying views that would command a significant premium in neighbouring suburbs. The block sizes are generally generous, the streets are quiet, and the proximity to the Coorparoo Square precinct and the Eastern Busway gives residents the practical infrastructure benefits of the inner east without the concentrated density.

The buyer profile in Seven Hills leans toward families who have done their research. They know the suburb, they understand the access, and they have typically ruled out several other inner east addresses before arriving here. When they find the right property, they tend to move with conviction.

If you own a property in Seven Hills and you are curious about what it would achieve in the current market, I am happy to give you a direct assessment.
