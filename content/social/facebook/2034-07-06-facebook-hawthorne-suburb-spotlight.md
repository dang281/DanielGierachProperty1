## Caption

Hawthorne has one of the tightest property markets in Brisbane's inner east. Listings are relatively scarce, the buyer pool is well-established, and the properties that come to market attract attention quickly.

The suburb's appeal is built on a clear package: proximity to the river and the Hawthorne Garage, Hawthorne Road as a consistent cafe and retail strip, easy ferry access, and a predominantly character housing stock on reasonable to generous block sizes.

The buyers competing for Hawthorne properties are typically households with a specific reason to want this suburb over its immediate neighbours. They have usually already purchased nearby or watched the market here for some time. The number of buyers who inspect a Hawthorne listing before going to auction, or before making an offer, tends to be high relative to the inner east average.

For Hawthorne homeowners thinking about the current market and what your property could achieve, the conversation is always worth having. danielgierach.com/suburbs/hawthorne.
