## Caption

Hawthorne consistently performs at the upper end of Brisbane's inner east market for reasons that are well understood by anyone who has spent time in the suburb. The combination of the Oxford Street lifestyle precinct, the river, the ferry connections to the CBD, the character architecture, and the school catchments creates a demand environment where the buyer pool is active and well-funded.

The suburb's price ceiling has continued to move in recent years, driven partly by interstate buyers who have completed the Sydney-Brisbane value comparison and identified Hawthorne as an equivalent lifestyle suburb at a meaningfully different price point. That buyer type does not require the same depth of local market knowledge as a long-term Brisbane buyer, and they tend to move faster when they find the right property.

Properties in Hawthorne that capture the full combination of the suburb's appeal, river views or proximity, quality interior renovation, and functional outdoor space, consistently attract the most competitive buyer pools in any season.

For owners in Hawthorne who are thinking about a sale, understanding the specific attributes of your property relative to recent comparable sales is the most useful starting point. danielgierach.com/suburbs/hawthorne is where that conversation begins.
