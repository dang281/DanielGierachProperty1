## Caption

Hawthorne earns its reputation from the details: ferry access at two terminals, Oxford Street within walking distance, and some of the best-preserved Queenslander streetscapes in Brisbane's inner east. Buyers targeting this suburb tend to be decisive, because they know supply here is genuinely limited.

The character homes along Hawthorne Road and the quieter streets running down toward the river represent a lifestyle that is difficult to replicate in other suburbs. School catchments for Lourdes Hill, Churchie, and Cannon Hill State School also make Hawthorne a strong choice for families who want private school access without long commutes.

Midwinter stock levels have been modest, which has worked in sellers' favour when well-presented homes have been correctly positioned. Buyers who missed out earlier in the year remain active and motivated.

If you've been thinking about what a sale would realistically achieve in this market, the best first step is a current appraisal.
