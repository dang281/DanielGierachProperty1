## Caption

Carina Heights sits in the quieter eastern edge of the inner east, a suburb that rewards buyers who prioritise space and value over proximity to the CBD café strip. The blocks are generally larger than the inner suburbs closer to the river, the streets are quieter, and the demographic has historically skewed toward owner-occupiers who have been in their homes for a decade or more.

That profile creates opportunities for sellers. A long-held home in Carina Heights often comes to market with unrealised potential: functional but dated presentation that a buyer with renovation appetite will pay for if the block and position are right. The renovation buyer is a consistent presence in Carina Heights open homes.

The suburb's access to Westfield Carindale and the Princess Alexandra Hospital employment cluster are two factors that keep buyer demand stable outside of peak spring periods. Families who work at PA or Greenslopes often start their property search in Carina Heights because the commute works without relying on the motorway.
