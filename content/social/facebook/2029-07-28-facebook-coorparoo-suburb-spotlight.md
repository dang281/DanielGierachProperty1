## Caption

Coorparoo is the most accessible suburb in the inner east. Multiple bus routes run directly to the CBD, Coorparoo Junction pulls the suburb together with shops, markets, and daily amenity, and the school options here are among the strongest in the area.

Coorparoo State School and Coorparoo Secondary College sit within the suburb, and private school options at Villanova, Padua, and St Laurence's are all within a short commute. That school depth is a direct driver of family buyer demand, and it shows in how the family home market performs when well-positioned properties come to market.

The land here is relatively flat compared to the hilly pockets further into the inner east, and that flatness opens up genuine dual-occupancy and development potential on the right blocks. Sellers with larger land holdings in established streets often find buyers who are looking at both the immediate lifestyle value and the longer-term optionality.

Families priced out of Hawthorne and Norman Park have consistently redirected their search to Coorparoo. The suburb sits at the southern edge of where the inner east premium is most strongly felt, and the transition has been gradual but consistent. For owners considering timing, that trajectory is worth factoring in.
