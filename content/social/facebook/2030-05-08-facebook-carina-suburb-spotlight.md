## Caption

Carina is one of the inner east suburbs where buyers increasingly arrive not as a compromise but as a deliberate choice. The combination of block sizes, access to the Gateway Motorway corridor, and relative price point compared to Morningside and Cannon Hill has attracted a buyer profile that is actively choosing Carina rather than settling for it.

The suburb's practical infrastructure is strong. The Westfield Carindale proximity means that day-to-day retail and dining access is genuinely convenient. The bus network to the CBD is serviceable, and the drive time via the internal suburban roads keeps Carina within the 15 to 20-minute commute envelope for most inner-city employment destinations.

Carina State School has a solid reputation that keeps the family buyer segment anchored in the suburb, and the secondary school catchment network in the inner east southeast means that parents thinking about the full schooling journey from primary through to Year 12 can plan without significant commuting complexity.

The housing stock includes a good proportion of older character and brick homes on 600-square-metre-plus blocks, alongside a growing number of townhouse developments in the flatter sections. The house-on-land component continues to attract the most competitive buying activity.

For sellers in Carina, the winter market tends to see a slight cooling in open home volume but not in buyer qualification. The buyers who are through the door in May and June are serious.
