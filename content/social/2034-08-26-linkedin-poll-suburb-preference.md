## Caption

Brisbane's inner east covers a range of suburb profiles. Riverside character homes in Bulimba and Hawthorne. The ridge suburbs with larger blocks in Camp Hill, Carina Heights, and Carindale. The established family pockets in Norman Park and Morningside.

Each attracts a different buyer for different reasons. For those who know the area well, the preference usually comes down to lifestyle priorities.

Quick question:

If you were buying in Brisbane's inner east, which factor would most influence your suburb choice?

## Poll options

- Proximity to the river and café strip
- School catchment
- Block size and privacy
- Commute distance and infrastructure
