## Caption

The inner east is not a single market. Bulimba, Hawthorne, Norman Park, Morningside, Cannon Hill and the surrounding suburbs all have distinct buyer pools, different price points, and different exposure to the Olympic year dynamics playing out in 2032.

Some buyers are focused on riverside lifestyle and prestige. Others are tracking school catchments and character homes at more accessible price points. Others are watching for value relative to infrastructure access heading into and beyond the Games.

With the Brisbane Olympic year now open, curious where people think the strongest positioning sits.

## Poll options

- Bulimba or Hawthorne (river, lifestyle)
- Norman Park or Camp Hill (schools, character)
- Morningside or Cannon Hill (value, access)
