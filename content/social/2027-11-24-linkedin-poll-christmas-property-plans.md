## Caption

Every year, the question comes up at this time: do you push to sell before Christmas or plan for the new year?

For some vendors, the Christmas deadline creates useful focus. They have a reason to move quickly, prepare the property properly, and make a decision that has been pending for months. For others, the end of year feels like the wrong time to be dealing with open homes and contract negotiations, and they would rather have a clean run at the market from late January or February.

Both positions are reasonable. What I find interesting is how many people's answer changes depending on what kind of market it is.

Does the Christmas and new year period affect your property plans?
