## Caption

Most sellers picture settlement day as a high-pressure event where something can go wrong at the last minute.

In practice, for the vast majority of vendors in Queensland, settlement happens quietly and entirely without their direct involvement. The PEXA digital settlement platform processes the transfer electronically. The bank discharges the mortgage, the buyer's lender releases funds, and title transfers. Most sellers find out it is done via a text or email from their solicitor.

What that means is: the preparation phase is where settlement is actually won or lost. Clearing the property, confirming the inclusions match the contract, making sure nothing has been damaged between signing and handover, and keeping the utilities running until the settlement date. Those are the things that create last-minute problems. Not the settlement itself.

I have put together a full breakdown of what happens in the weeks before, on, and immediately after settlement, written specifically for Queensland sellers.
