## Caption

Q1 2032 closes today. It has been a quarter with an unusual backdrop: the Olympic year context, a buyer pool that entered January with more urgency than usual, and a supply side that has been measured and deliberate rather than flooded.

The results I have seen in the inner east suggest a market that has behaved with more discipline than some of the commentary predicted. But the view from inside a campaign is always different from the view from the outside, and I am genuinely interested in how buyers, vendors, and observers have read the quarter.

Whether you have been actively searching, selling, or just watching the market from the sidelines, your read on Q1 is worth capturing before the second quarter begins.

Looking back at Q1 2032, how would you rate Brisbane's inner east property market?
