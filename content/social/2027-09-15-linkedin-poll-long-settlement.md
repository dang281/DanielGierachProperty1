**Caption:**

Settlement periods in Queensland contracts are typically 30 to 45 days, but they are negotiable. Some buyers need more time, particularly if they are selling first. Some sellers prefer a longer settlement to align with their own purchase. Whether a longer settlement is worth accepting often depends on what else is on the table.
