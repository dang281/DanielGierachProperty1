## Caption

Spring campaign preparation involves a sequence of decisions rather than one single choice. What vendors do first often determines what they have time and budget for afterwards. Starting with decluttering creates space to properly assess what needs fixing. Starting with styling before defects are addressed can produce photographs that create expectations the property then fails to meet at inspection. Getting the sequence right matters as much as doing each individual task well. What would you tackle first?

## Poll options

- Declutter and deep clean before anything else
- Address maintenance issues and defects
- Focus on street appeal and the front of the property
- Get a stylist involved early and work backwards from the photography
