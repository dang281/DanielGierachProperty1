## Caption

Brisbane's inner east sees its first meaningful wave of new listings in late January and February. Sellers who list early in that window tend to face less competition. Those who wait until March or April enter a more crowded field as the spring conversation shifts to autumn.

If you are planning to sell in 2027, your preparation timeline starts now, regardless of when you plan to go live. Styling, photography, marketing copy, and pricing strategy all take time to get right. The sellers who list in late January with a strong campaign are not the ones who decided to sell in January. They are the ones who made that decision in November or December.

Curious where you sit right now.
