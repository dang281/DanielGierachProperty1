## Caption

Auctions create transparency on the day but require unconditional readiness. Private treaty gives you conditions and time but less clarity on where other buyers are sitting.

Both methods are common in Brisbane's inner east. Auctions are more prevalent in suburbs like Bulimba, Hawthorne, and Norman Park where buyer depth is strong and properties are genuinely hard to value. Private treaty suits properties where the buyer pool is narrower or where conditions are needed.

For a buyer, the experience of each is very different. An auction requires you to have your finance sorted, your building and pest done, and your decision made before you walk in the room. Private treaty gives you a window to work through those steps after you have made an offer.

Which approach do you prefer as a buyer?
