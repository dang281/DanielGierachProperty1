## Caption

Creek Road gives Mt Gravatt East something that many inner-east suburbs at its price point do not have: a strong retail and service corridor without the noise and density of a major shopping centre. Buyers who know the suburb have usually walked Creek Road and understood what it offers before they have made their decision.

The school story runs in two directions. Mt Gravatt East State School serves the primary catchment through most of the suburb. For the southern sections, access to Mt Gravatt State High School for secondary years is a draw for families who want a clear pathway from primary through to senior without changing areas.

The suburb's southern portions along the ridge separating Mt Gravatt East from Carindale carry elevation and views that are not obvious from the main roads. Buyers who have done a thorough street-by-street search of this part of the suburb know that certain streets offer aspects that the entry price into Mt Gravatt East does not advertise. That is a feature for buyers willing to do the research.

The value proposition in Mt Gravatt East is straightforward: inner-east access at a price point below the premium suburbs, with land sizes that the premium suburbs stopped offering as their prices climbed. Patient buyers who moved past the more recognised names in the inner east have been finding it here for years.
