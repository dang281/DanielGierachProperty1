## Caption

Not every buyer or seller will transact before the Games close. Plenty of people are deliberately planning to settle in August or later, for their own good reasons. But settling into the post-Olympic period comes with its own set of questions.

Will the rental market shift? Will short-term let properties flood back into long-term supply and affect values? Will the city feel different once the Games infrastructure winds down? Will interest rate decisions after the Games change borrowing conditions? These are all reasonable things to be thinking about.

I am curious which of these concerns is carrying the most weight for people who are looking at settling after the Games.

If your settlement is planned for after the Olympics close, what is your biggest concern?
