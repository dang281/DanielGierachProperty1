## Caption

Coorparoo is the kind of suburb that surprises people who have not paid close attention.

Four kilometres from the CBD. Bus and train access. A real dining and cafe scene. A mix of original Queenslanders, renovated character homes, and newer apartments. And over the past year, unit prices up around 11 per cent and houses up around 13 per cent.

The buyers who look at Coorparoo are often comparing it to suburbs further in at twice the price and deciding the numbers make more sense here. That is healthy competition for sellers.

If you own in Coorparoo and you have been thinking about your options, I am happy to sit down and give you a clear read on the current market.
