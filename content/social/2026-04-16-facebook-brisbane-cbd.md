## Caption

Brisbane's CBD apartment market has had a significant 12 months, and it's worth paying attention to if you own property in the inner city.

The median unit value in Brisbane CBD is currently around $844,000, up approximately 18% over the past year, according to Cotality (CoreLogic) data from early 2026. That pace of growth in the apartment segment is notable, driven by a combination of interstate migration, tight rental vacancy, and returning confidence from buyers who sat on the sidelines through the uncertainty of recent years.

For inner-city apartment owners, the dynamics are different from houses. Strata levies, building quality, floor plan size, and proximity to the river corridor all affect buyer appetite significantly. Not all CBD apartments are equal, and that gap between well-positioned stock and less attractive offerings has widened.

If you own an apartment in or around the CBD and you're considering your options, it's worth getting a current read on where your specific property sits in the market.
