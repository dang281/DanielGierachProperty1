## Caption

Spring in Brisbane's inner east runs from late August through November, but the character of the market shifts considerably across that window. August and September see buyers who have been active through winter and are ready to transact. October and November see more buyer volume but also more competing listings. Vendors who launch earlier in spring often face a buyer pool that is motivated and a listing environment that is not yet crowded. The question of timing is one of the most common conversations I have with vendors right now.
