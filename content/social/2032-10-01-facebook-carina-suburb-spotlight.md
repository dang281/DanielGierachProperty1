## Caption

Carina is one of those suburbs that keeps delivering for families who do their homework. The streets around Carina State School fill up quickly with buyers who have come across from Camp Hill and found they can get substantially more land for the same money. That is a pattern that has held for years and it shows no sign of slowing.

The creek-side reserves along Bulimba Creek give the suburb a genuine sense of space that is hard to find this close to the city. Families with kids in primary school tend to stay for a long time once they arrive, which keeps turnover low and buyer competition strong when properties do come to market.

Spring in the inner east brings out a wave of buyers who have been waiting since winter. Open homes fill up, pre-approvals are active, and the families who have been watching comparable sales for months are ready to move. If you are sitting on a property in Carina that you have been thinking about selling, now is the time the market is working in your favour.

The proximity to the QE2 Hospital precinct also draws buyers who want a short commute without paying inner-city prices. Good access, established streetscapes, and a suburb with real community roots. That combination does not go unnoticed.
