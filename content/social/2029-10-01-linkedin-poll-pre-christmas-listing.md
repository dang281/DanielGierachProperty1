## Caption

October is the month when vendors move from "thinking about it" to actually deciding. In Brisbane's inner east, spring campaigns that launch in the next four to six weeks catch the deepest buyer pool of the year. Leave it past mid-November and the December calendar starts cutting into your campaign before it has room to run. If you're weighing up whether to list before Christmas or wait until autumn, you're not alone. Most of the conversations I'm having right now are about exactly that. Where are you sitting with it?
