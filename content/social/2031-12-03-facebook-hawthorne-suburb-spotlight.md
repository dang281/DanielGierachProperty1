## Caption

A Hawthorne property coming to market is a significant moment for buyers who've been waiting. The suburb's turnover rate is low enough that the gap between listings can stretch to weeks or longer in some pockets. When a well-positioned home comes up, the buyer pool that has been watching assembles quickly.

December is not the quietest month for Hawthorne. The suburb's prestige character means it draws buyers who are not overly constrained by school term calendars or the conventional sense of when the selling season is. Serious buyers at the top end of the market tend to be available year-round, and December listings in Hawthorne have historically performed well.

The Hawthorne Ferry connection to the CBD is one of those lifestyle elements that sounds good in theory and delivers in practice. Residents who use it describe it as one of the more enjoyable daily commutes in Brisbane. For buyers who place a premium on not sitting in traffic, it changes the calculation.

Hawthorne State School is a solid local primary, and it helps ensure family buyer demand alongside the downsizer and professional buyer groups that make up the broader market here.

River frontage in this suburb is at a completely different price level from the rest of the stock. When a riverfront or river-view property comes to market in Hawthorne, the buyer profile changes entirely.

Hawthorne continues to post some of the strongest medians in the inner east. ⚠️ VERIFY current median and days on market before posting.
