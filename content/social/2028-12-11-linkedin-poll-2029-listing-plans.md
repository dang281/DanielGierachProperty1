## Caption

If you are thinking about selling in 2029, the decision about timing is worth making now, before the year starts, rather than in February when the market has already opened and your preparation window has compressed.

The inner east autumn window runs from late February through to May. To launch well in late February, preparation typically needs to start in January or earlier. Styling, photography, minor repairs, and the disclosure paperwork all require lead time.

January is a good month to plan. It is not a good month to rush through preparation.

If you are planning to sell in Brisbane in 2029, when do you intend to go to market?
