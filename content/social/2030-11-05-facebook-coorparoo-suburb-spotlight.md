## Caption

Coorparoo is one of the inner east's most active markets. It sits at a price point that attracts first-home buyers stretching into the suburb, families upgrading from units, and investors who can see the suburb's rental appeal from its transport links and amenity density.

The commercial strip along Old Cleveland Road anchors the suburb's lifestyle. The proximity to Stones Corner and Greenslopes adds café and retail access that Coorparoo residents use as much as their own strip. The suburb functions as part of a broader inner east amenity network.

School catchments in Coorparoo include Coorparoo State School for primary, which has maintained a strong reputation. Secondary school access via the broader network includes Cavendish Road State High, which serves some Coorparoo streets depending on the specific boundary.

The topography of Coorparoo works in favour of some street positions more than others. The elevated streets toward the Seven Hills boundary and the blocks above Old Cleveland Road on the eastern sections deliver city views that buyers specifically target. Those positions outperform the flat, road-facing stock on the main thoroughfares consistently.

For Coorparoo homeowners on the better-positioned streets, November is a productive month to be on the market.
