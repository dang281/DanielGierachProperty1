## Caption

Mt Gravatt East carries a practical kind of appeal that holds up under scrutiny. Greenslopes Private Hospital to the north brings a steady stream of healthcare professionals to the area, and Griffith University's Nathan campus means the suburb sits in a corridor that draws both families and investors for different but overlapping reasons.

The elevated blocks here offer views that surprise buyers who have not visited before. There is a mix of post-war homes and later infill development, and that variety gives the market genuine depth: downsizers, growing families, and investors often compete across the same street. That breadth of demand is what keeps the suburb resilient.

December is when some of the most prepared buyers in this market make their move. Healthcare workers have year-end certainty about their rosters; university staff and affiliated buyers know their schedule for 2033. Owners who have been watching and waiting sometimes find the gap between now and February longer than expected, and the buyers who were ready in December have moved on by the time the campaign launches in March.

If you own in Mt Gravatt East, a summer appraisal is a low-commitment way to understand where you sit before the new year.
