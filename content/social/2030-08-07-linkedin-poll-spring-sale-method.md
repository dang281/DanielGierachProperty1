## Caption

The method of sale shapes the entire campaign, and the right choice depends on the property more than the market. In Brisbane's inner east, auction has historically performed well for homes with a broad buyer pool: four-bedroom family homes in Bulimba, Hawthorne, and Norman Park where multiple buyers are likely to compete. Expression of interest suits a unique property where the buyer range is genuinely wide and a fixed auction date would disadvantage buyers who need time to do due diligence. Private treaty works when flexibility and conditions matter more than competitive tension. The question below is about your instinct, not the right answer.

If selling your inner east home this spring, which method would you choose?
