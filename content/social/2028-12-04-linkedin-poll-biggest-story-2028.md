## Caption

2028 has been a full year in Brisbane property. The Olympic 2032 conversation has moved from infrastructure announcements to visible construction timelines. The inner east land market has continued to reflect that demand. And the broader affordability conversation has not resolved itself.

Looking back at the year, some themes have been more significant than others. What stands out depends on whether you are an owner, an investor, a buyer still trying to get in, or a seller who moved this year.

In your view, what was the most significant Brisbane property story of 2028?
