## Caption

Carina Heights tends to attract buyers who have done their research. The suburb sits in a quieter position than its neighbours, with larger blocks and some elevated streets that give a genuine sense of space. For families, that combination of room to move and proximity to Cannon Hill Anglican College is a strong draw.

The access to Westfield Carindale is a practical advantage that residents do not take for granted. Having one of Queensland's largest retail centres within easy reach adds a layer of convenience that families value, especially with young children. At the same time, Carina Heights retains a residential feel that Carindale itself, sitting closer to the retail precinct, sometimes lacks.

Block sizes here run larger than the inner-east average, and that land content supports long-term value. Buyers who missed out on homes in Cannon Hill or Carindale often find Carina Heights offers comparable lifestyle at a more accessible price point, and that flow-on demand keeps the suburb active even in quieter seasonal periods.

If you own in Carina Heights and have not had a market appraisal recently, winter is a good time to get the numbers straight before spring brings more vendor competition.
