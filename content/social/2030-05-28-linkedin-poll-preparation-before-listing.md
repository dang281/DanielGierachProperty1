## Caption

One of the most consistent patterns I see in property campaigns is the gap between when a vendor decides to sell and when they are actually ready to launch. For some homeowners, the preparation takes a weekend. For others, it is a two-month project.

The homes that achieve the best results are almost always the ones where the preparation was done without rushing. Decluttered, repaired, photographed well, and launched with a price guide that reflects both the market and the seller's genuine expectation.

How long would you realistically need to have your home ready to sell?

## Poll options

- Two weeks or less (I keep it pretty tidy)
- Four to six weeks (some work needed)
- Two to three months (significant prep required)
- I have not thought about it yet
