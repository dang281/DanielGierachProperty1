## Caption

Buyers in Brisbane's inner east increasingly make the decision whether to attend an open home based on listing photography before they are anywhere near the property. Vendors who invest in professional photography and styling see this reflected in their open home attendance figures. The gap between a well-photographed listing and a poorly photographed one on the same street, at the same price point, is measurable in how many groups come through. But photography is not the only factor. What actually tips you into attending?

## Poll options

- The photography in the listing
- The price guide relative to my budget
- The suburb and street location
- A recommendation from someone who has already seen it
