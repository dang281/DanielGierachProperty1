## Caption

Flood overlay properties in Brisbane's inner east are not rare. Parts of East Brisbane, Morningside, Norman Park, and other suburbs sit within overlay areas. The overlay covers a range of risk levels, from properties that inundated in 2011 or 2022 to properties with a theoretical risk based on modelling but no actual flood history. Buyers who understand the distinction between overlay categories and actual flood history make better decisions than those who treat overlay as a simple pass or fail signal. Where do you sit on this?
