## Caption

The summary page of a building and pest report is the wrong place to start. Most buyers read it first and make the wrong call.

Active timber pest activity and general maintenance items require completely different responses from a buyer. Confusing them is one of the most common causes of buyers walking away from a property they should have purchased.

A report with many line items is not necessarily a bad report. Older inner east properties will always have maintenance items. The relevant question is whether those items are cosmetic or structural.

The inspector can walk you through the report by phone. That conversation is worth requesting before you make any decision about proceeding.
