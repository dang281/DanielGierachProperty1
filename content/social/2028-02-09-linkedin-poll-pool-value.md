## Caption

In Brisbane's inner east, a pool is a genuine lifestyle feature for roughly 8 months of the year. It is also a maintenance commitment, a safety obligation if you have young children, and a cost that varies significantly depending on the condition of the equipment.

Whether buyers value pools depends almost entirely on their life stage and how they plan to use the property.

Genuinely curious where people in my network land on this.

How important is a pool when buying in Brisbane's inner east?
