## Caption

Q2, the April to June quarter, is Brisbane's most consistently productive selling period. The autumn listings that launched in February and March are resolving. The buyers who have been searching since January are making decisions. And a second wave of vendors who wanted to see how Q1 played out before committing are now starting their preparation conversations.

For buyers, Q2 typically brings good choice in the first half and a thinning field in the second half as the listings that launched in March clear and winter approaches. The buyers who move in April and May generally have better selection than those who wait until June.

For vendors, Q2 is the window where preparation that started in January pays its full dividend. A campaign that launches in the first two weeks of April is competing in a market that still has strong buyer numbers and limited winter hesitancy. A campaign that slips to late May is heading into a shorter season.

The intention data I collect through polls like this one shapes how I read what is coming in April and beyond. The ratio between active preparers and watchers tells me something about the supply side of the Q2 market.

Heading into Q2 2034, what best describes your property plans?
