## Caption

Cannon Hill in the mid-autumn campaign window is a suburb where the entry-level and mid-market buyers are at their most active. The peak of the season brings motivated buyers who have been searching since late January and are now at the point of deciding. For a well-prepared Cannon Hill vendor, these are the buyers you want walking through your door.

The Lytton Road corridor gives the suburb east-west access that connects it efficiently to both the inner east and the port corridor. For buyers who need flexibility in where they work, Cannon Hill's transport connectivity is a practical plus that does not always appear in listing descriptions but that buyers notice. ⚠️ VERIFY

The renovation activity in Cannon Hill has steadily raised the suburb's presentation standard. Streets that had a row of original homes a few years ago now have several well-executed renovations that have reset the comparable sale floor. This is relevant for vendors of original homes: the market is comparing your property against the renovated version on the same street. Pricing needs to account for that gap honestly.

For first-home buyers in Brisbane's inner east, Cannon Hill continues to be the suburb where the numbers most often align with realistic budgets. That demand is not going away. It is structural and it is supported by the ongoing price gradient of the suburbs to the west.

March is the peak. This is the moment to be on market.
