## Caption

A buyer's agent works exclusively for the purchaser. Their job is to find properties, assess value, and negotiate on your behalf.

Fees typically run from $10,000 to $25,000 or more depending on the service level. In Brisbane's inner east, where good properties sell fast and competition is real, a skilled buyer's agent can sometimes save more than their fee through better negotiation or earlier access to off-market stock.

The counter-argument: if you know the market well, do the research yourself, and are comfortable negotiating, you may not need one.

What's your take?
