## Caption

Brisbane's inner east winter market has historically delivered a smaller buyer pool alongside noticeably less listing competition. A property launching in June or July typically faces fewer comparable listings on the street than the same property launched in October. The buyers who remain active in winter are usually not browsing. They have missed out on something in the autumn campaign and are ready to move when the right property appears. Whether winter suits you depends largely on which side of the transaction you are on. What do you expect from the inner east market this winter?
