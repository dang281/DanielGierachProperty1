## Caption

Carindale is where the inner east corridor delivers its largest blocks. Families who have been in Carina or Carina Heights for five years and want more space, a bigger yard, a second living area, move to Carindale. It is a logical progression along the corridor and the buyer profile reflects it: upgraders who know exactly what they want because they have already been living nearby.

Westfield Carindale is the suburb's commercial anchor and it is a comprehensive one. The Gateway Motorway access to the east and south gives Carindale a practical travel advantage for buyers who use that corridor regularly. School catchments here connect into the inner east network and the suburb's proximity to Chandler and Belmont adds sporting and recreational options that families with active children find useful.

The blocks in Carindale are the product. Buyers come here for land. A four-bedroom home on a 700 or 800 square metre block in Carindale represents a category of property that the suburbs closer to the CBD largely stopped offering a decade ago. That combination still carries genuine buyer demand from the upgrader segment and from buyers who have moved out from the inner city and want to reset their expectations about what a backyard looks like.

For Carindale owners thinking about the 2031 market, the upgrade buyer pool is active. They are ready.
