## Caption

The inner east upgrade buyer is one of the most active segments in the market right now, and often one of the most stuck. Many homeowners in suburbs like Bulimba, Hawthorne, and Norman Park have built substantial equity from years of price growth. The challenge is that they are upgrading within the same market, which means the gain on sale is partially offset by what the next home costs. That calculus is manageable, but it takes planning. The bigger question is usually which barrier sits between you and the next step.

What is your biggest barrier to upgrading your home right now?

## Poll options

- Deposit or equity gap
- Uncertainty about whether to sell first or buy first
- The right property has not come up yet
- Cost of selling (agent commission, stamp duty, moving)
