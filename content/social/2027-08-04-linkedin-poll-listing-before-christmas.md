## Caption

Brisbane's inner east typically runs a strong sequence of campaigns from September through to late November, before the pre-Christmas slowdown brings activity to a near standstill. For vendors, the window is real and the timing decision matters. Launch in September or October and you have a full campaign run. Push to November and the window is tighter. Wait for the new year and you are starting fresh in a different market. Many sellers are weighing this up right now. Where do you sit?
