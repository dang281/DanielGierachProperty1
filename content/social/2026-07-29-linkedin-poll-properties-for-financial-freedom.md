## Caption

The answer depends on the properties, the debt levels, the rental income, and what "financial freedom" actually means to you.

Some investors argue that two or three high-quality assets in strong suburbs, held long enough, are sufficient. Others chase a portfolio of ten or more properties, relying on volume and spread to generate the passive income they need.

In Brisbane's inner east, the argument for quality over quantity is compelling. One well-located house that doubles in value over ten years does more work than five mediocre properties in thin rental markets.

But it's a genuinely open question. Where do you land?
