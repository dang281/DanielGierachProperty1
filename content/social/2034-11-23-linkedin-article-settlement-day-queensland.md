## Caption

For most sellers in Queensland, settlement day is quiet and entirely digital. The keys change hands after funds clear, not before.

Queensland uses PEXA for electronic settlement. The vendor's solicitor and the buyer's solicitor complete the transaction digitally, and funds are typically clear within minutes of the scheduled settlement time.

Settlement does not always occur at the exact scheduled time. Delays of one to four hours are common and usually relate to the buyer's bank confirming the discharge of the vendor's loan.

The vendor does not need to be present at settlement. The agent releases the keys once settlement is confirmed by the vendor's solicitor.
