## Caption

Professional styling before a sale is one of those decisions that splits vendors right down the middle. Some see it as an obvious investment. Others look at their home and genuinely can't see why it would be necessary.

The honest answer is that it varies. A well-presented property in a strong price bracket may benefit significantly from staging. A tidy, well-maintained home at a particular price point may not need it at all. What matters is how the property photographs, how it reads at an open, and what the competing listings look like.

Curious what your instinct is.
