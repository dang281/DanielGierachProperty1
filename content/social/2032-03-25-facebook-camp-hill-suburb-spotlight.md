## Caption

Camp Hill sits at the intersection of everything the inner east buyer values most. The elevated streets along the main ridge of the suburb, from Old Cleveland Road climbing toward the plateau above Coorparoo, offer a combination of character housing, treetop outlooks, and a walkable daily environment that makes the suburb consistently competitive for buyers with strong purchasing capacity.

The Camp Hill Marketplace on Old Cleveland Road has become a genuine daily-use precinct, with the coffee and food options there cited by buyers and residents alike as part of what makes the suburb feel like a complete place to live rather than just a convenient location. That distinction matters to a specific and valuable buyer cohort.

The school zone is another consistent talking point. Camp Hill State Infants and Primary School is well regarded, and the proximity to Loreto College, Villanova College, and Padua College makes the suburb a practical choice for families with secondary school-age children.

March campaigns in Camp Hill perform at the top of the inner east range. The buyer pool here is experienced, financially prepared, and conducting genuine comparisons. Properties that are presented honestly and priced with reference to what has actually sold in the past three months, not the aspirational end of the range, are the ones that perform without extension or price adjustment.

If you own in Camp Hill and are thinking about selling this autumn, the window before Easter is your best available opportunity.
