## Caption

Vendors spend tens of thousands renovating kitchens and bathrooms. Buyers often decide how they feel about a property in the first thirty seconds of walking through the door, before they have even reached the kitchen. That first impression sets an emotional frame that colours every room that follows. The features that shape it are not always the ones vendors spend the most money on. Worth asking honestly: what actually hits first?

## Poll options

- Natural light and the feel of the space
- Layout and flow between rooms
- The condition and quality of finishes
- Street appeal before I even get inside
