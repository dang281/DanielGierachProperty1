## Caption

If you are planning to sell your home in the first half of 2035, the preparation that determines your result has probably not started yet. This is worth understanding before the Christmas break.

The sellers who achieve the strongest results in the February to May window are not the ones who start preparing in January. They are the ones who started the conversation in November or December, completed any minor repairs or cosmetic work before Christmas, had photography and styling scheduled for early January, and launched their campaign in late January or February into a buyer pool that had been watching all through summer.

The preparation phase for an inner east property typically takes four to six weeks. That includes any repair work, decluttering, professional styling and photography, the agent sign-up process, and the listing preparation itself. Compressing that into three weeks after New Year produces a campaign that shows the shortcuts.

December is actually one of the better months to have the initial conversation with an agent about a 2035 sale. The market is quieter, there is no pressure to decide immediately, and it gives you the full Christmas break to think through your situation, your timeline, and your goals.

If 2035 is when you are planning to move, starting that conversation now costs you nothing and changes the quality of your preparation.
