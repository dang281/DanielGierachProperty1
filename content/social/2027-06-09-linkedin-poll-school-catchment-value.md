## Caption

In Brisbane's inner east, the premium for a property on the right side of a school catchment boundary can be significant. Two houses on the same street, similar size, similar condition, the one that falls inside the Whites Hill State College or Balmoral State High catchment can attract more offers and achieve a higher price. The question is whether that premium is real or perception. From what I see in actual sales data, it is real. But how much it matters depends entirely on where the buyer is in their life. Curious where people stand on this.
