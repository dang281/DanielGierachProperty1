## Caption

Most of the owners I talk to who are thinking about selling have a reason they have not yet taken the step. That reason is worth understanding because it often has a practical answer that changes the timeline. If you own a property you have considered selling but have not yet listed, what is the primary reason for the pause?

## Poll options

- Waiting for the right market conditions
- Need to find the next property first
- The preparation feels overwhelming
- Price uncertainty, not sure what to expect
