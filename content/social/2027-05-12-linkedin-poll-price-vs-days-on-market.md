## Caption

There is often a real trade-off between holding out for the highest price and selling within a reasonable campaign window. Some vendors prioritise a fast result, because the certainty matters more than squeezing the last dollar. Others will extend a campaign by several weeks to extract a better number, because the price difference is meaningful to their next move.

Neither position is wrong. The right answer depends entirely on your circumstances, your timeline, and what you are doing next. But most sellers I talk to have a clear instinct one way or the other, even before they work through the numbers.

When selling your home, which matters more to you?
