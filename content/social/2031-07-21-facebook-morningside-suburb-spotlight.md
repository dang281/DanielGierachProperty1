## Caption

Morningside occupies a sweet spot in the inner east. It delivers much of what buyers love about Hawthorne and Balmoral at a price point that opens the door to a broader group of buyers. Character homes along Apollo Road and surrounding streets attract owner-occupiers who want something with soul, not a new build that looks like every other new build.

The Cross River Rail has changed the conversation for this suburb. Morningside Station and Cannon Hill Station both serve the precinct, giving residents genuine train connectivity to the CBD and inner city that was less compelling before the infrastructure came online. Buyers factor that in, and it is reflected in how people now describe Morningside relative to the suburbs immediately around it.

Morningside State School adds to the family appeal, but the suburb draws a wider demographic than school-zone buyers alone. Young professionals, couples upgrading from apartments, and downsizers from larger properties all find Morningside working for their particular needs.

July volumes are lower, which is normal for winter. But the buyers operating right now are motivated, and a well-presented Morningside home in this environment does not sit around. If you have been thinking about your options, this is a good time to have that conversation.
