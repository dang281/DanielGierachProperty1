## Caption

The last few weeks of the autumn selling season are here. For vendors who have not yet listed, the decision about when to go to market is becoming more urgent. Easter is approaching. The peak window, which runs from mid-March to mid-April in Brisbane's inner east, is narrowing.

What makes the timing decision hard is that the factors pulling at a vendor are not all equal. Market conditions matter, but so does life. Agent advice is useful, but so is personal certainty. And fear of missing the season is real, even if it can lead to rushed decisions.

What single factor would most affect your decision on when to list your property this autumn?
