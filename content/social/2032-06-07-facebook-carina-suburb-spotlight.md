## Caption

Carina is a suburb where the land sizes tell most of the story. Blocks that run to 600 or 700 square metres on streets like Creek Road, Grays Road, and Old Cleveland Road attract buyers who want room to work with, whether that means building a pool and entertaining space or planning a longer-term renovation. That combination of land and proximity to the inner east is what keeps Carina on buyer shortlists alongside its more decorated neighbours.

The suburb sits within a practical distance of Westfield Carindale, which functions as a genuine daily convenience hub for residents rather than a destination. Carina has a mix of post-war homes and more recent infill housing, and the character homes on the eastern ridge streets tend to generate stronger buyer depth than the flat-block properties closer to Creek Road.

Gateway Baptist College and Carina State School both operate in the catchment, and families with school-age children factor both options into their suburb search. Bus links along Old Cleveland Road provide a direct corridor to the CBD, and easy access to the Gateway Motorway makes Carina a practical choice for buyers who travel south or to the airport regularly.
