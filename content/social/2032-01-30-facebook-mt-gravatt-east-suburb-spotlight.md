## Caption

Mt Gravatt East is a suburb that rewards patient buyers and consistent vendors. It sits on the southern edge of the inner east corridor, bordered by the Kessels Road and Creek Road arterials, and it has built a reputation as a suburb where buyers can still find genuine value compared to closer-in alternatives.

Griffith University's Nathan campus is within a short drive, which gives the suburb a consistent tenant and buyer base connected to the education precinct. The proximity to the Princess Alexandra Hospital and the broader Woolloongabba and Buranda health and services corridor adds another dimension to the buyer profile. This is a suburb with multiple demand sources that do not all move together.

The housing mix covers post-war brick and weatherboard homes on generous blocks, alongside a growing number of dual occupancy and secondary dwelling configurations where the land size supports it. Buyers looking for rental yield alongside owner-occupier credentials find Mt Gravatt East a practical option. The renovation market is active.

For families, Mt Gravatt East State School and the access to Seton College and other secondary options in the southern suburbs make the suburb a considered choice for parents planning their children's schooling trajectory.

In 2032, the suburb's value position relative to suburbs north of it, combined with improving infrastructure access toward the inner city, is drawing buyers who are thinking five to ten years ahead rather than just at current comparables.
