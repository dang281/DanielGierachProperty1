## Caption

Every spring, there are vendors who plan to list and don't. Not because conditions aren't right, and not because the property isn't ready. Usually it comes down to something specific that hasn't been resolved. Some of those things are practical, some are emotional, and some are just the mental weight of starting a process that feels large. Understanding what actually stalls people is useful, especially if you're one of them. What is most likely to stop a homeowner from listing this October or November?

## Poll options

- Not sure where they'll buy next
- Worried about not achieving asking price
- The preparation feels overwhelming
- Waiting for the right moment that never comes
