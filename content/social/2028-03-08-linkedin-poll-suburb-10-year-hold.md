## Caption

Forced choice questions tend to reveal what people actually think rather than what they say when every option is available.

I work in Brisbane's inner east every day and I have a clear view on which suburbs I would back over the next decade based on infrastructure investment, buyer demographics, and what the comparable sales data actually shows.

But I am curious what people in my network think. Sometimes the market consensus is more interesting than my own analysis.

If you could only buy in one suburb in Brisbane's inner east and had to hold it for 10 years, which would you choose?

Daniel Gierach
