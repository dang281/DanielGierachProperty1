## Caption

One of the most reliable market signals is what you observe at open homes, not reported sales data. By the time a result appears in CoreLogic, the market has already moved.

I track open home attendance numbers, how many groups are registering, how many are returning for second inspections, and whether competing properties are drawing multiple offers. Those indicators tell you more about where the market is heading than a median price update.

This autumn, I am interested in what buyers and vendors are actually experiencing on the ground, not just what the data says in aggregate.

How would you describe buyer competition at open homes in the inner east this autumn?

## Poll options
