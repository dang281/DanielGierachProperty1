## Caption

One of the most common conversations I have in April is the autumn-versus-spring timing question. Both windows have genuine strengths. Autumn has less competition from other sellers. Spring brings more buyer volume. The right answer depends on the property and the vendor's circumstances. If you own in Brisbane's inner east and are thinking about selling, where do you sit on this?
