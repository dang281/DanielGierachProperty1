## Caption

The idea that winter is the wrong time to sell a Brisbane home persists despite evidence that well-prepared inner east properties in June and July regularly produce competitive results. The buyer pool is smaller, but the buyers who are active are not browsing casually. They have typically missed out on something in the autumn campaign and are ready to act when the right property appears. Less listing competition on a given street can offset the narrower pool. Brisbane's mild winters also mean presentation and street appeal are rarely disadvantaged. Would you be comfortable selling your inner east home during winter?
