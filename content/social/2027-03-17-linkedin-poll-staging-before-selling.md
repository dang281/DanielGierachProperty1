## Caption

Property styling, or staging, means bringing in a professional to furnish and present a home for sale. The cost in Brisbane typically ranges from $2,500 for a partial styling of key rooms to $6,000 or more for a full staging of a larger home.

The argument for styling is straightforward: better photography. Styled homes photograph better. Better photography generates more enquiry. More enquiry drives more people through open homes. More open home attendance creates competition. Competition drives price.

The argument against: the cost comes out of net proceeds, and for a well-maintained, attractively furnished home it may not add enough to justify the spend.

In practice, the homes that benefit most from professional styling are not the ones where the owner has poor taste. They are the ones where the current furniture is fine for living in but does not present well at the scale and angle of a real estate photograph. Empty homes benefit even more. An empty room is harder to understand than a furnished one.

Would you invest in professional styling before selling your home?
