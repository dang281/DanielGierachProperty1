## Caption

We close out the first half of 2032 today. The Games open in less than four weeks. The Brisbane property market has been through a full half-year of Olympic-year conditions, compressed timelines, interstate interest, and a rate environment that has kept everyone calibrating.

The second half of the year is a different question. The Games period itself, then September when the city returns to something like normal, then the traditional spring selling season. It is genuinely hard to read, and anyone who tells you with confidence exactly what Brisbane property does in Q3 and Q4 of an Olympic year is making it up.

What I am more interested in is what people think. Sentiment is part of the market, and how buyers and sellers feel about the second half shapes how they act.

Halfway through 2032: what is your property outlook for the rest of the year?
