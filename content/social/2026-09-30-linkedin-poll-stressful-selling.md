## Caption

Selling is a complex process, financially and emotionally. What stresses people most varies more than you'd expect. Some vendors lie awake over whether the price is right. Others find the waiting, the open homes, the silent phone on Sunday afternoons, to be the hardest part. Others again find the preparation phase overwhelming, or worry about what happens when settlement actually arrives. There's no wrong answer here. Curious where most people find the weight sits.

What's the most stressful part of selling a property?
