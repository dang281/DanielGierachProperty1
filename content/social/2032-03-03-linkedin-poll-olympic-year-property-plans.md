## Caption

Brisbane 2032 is now four months away. March is traditionally the highest-quality buyer month of the year in the inner east, and this autumn there is an additional layer of decision-making: people are actively asking whether the Olympic calendar should influence when they buy, sell, or hold.

Some of the reasoning I hear is rational. Some of it is based on assumptions that do not hold up when you look at comparable Olympic host cities. But the sentiment is real and it is affecting how people are approaching their property decisions right now.

What best describes your property plans for 2032?
