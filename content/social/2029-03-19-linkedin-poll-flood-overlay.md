## Caption

Flood overlays affect a significant number of properties in Brisbane's inner east and have been a factor in buyer decisions since the 2011 and 2022 flood events. How buyers respond to a flood overlay varies enormously: some treat it as an automatic dealbreaker, others price it in and proceed, and some do not check until they are already emotionally committed to a property.

Brisbane City Council's flood awareness maps are publicly accessible and worth checking before any inner east property purchase. The overlay affects insurance, lending, and future resale in ways that are worth understanding upfront.

When you are looking at a property in Brisbane's inner east that has a flood overlay: what is your position?
