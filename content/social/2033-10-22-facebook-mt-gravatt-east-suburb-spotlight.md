## Caption

Mt Gravatt East offers a combination of elevation, block size, and school catchment access that is genuinely difficult to find at comparable price points in the suburbs closer to the city.

The elevated position gives many streets city views. That is not a minor feature in Brisbane's inner east fringe, where the flat topography of most suburbs means outlook is a premium that typically comes at a premium price. In Mt Gravatt East, that outlook is available at price points that buyers who have been looking more broadly find genuinely attractive once they make the comparison carefully.

The block sizes here are larger than what is available in inner east suburbs at comparable values. For families who want space to extend, add a pool, or have a useable yard, the land component in Mt Gravatt East stacks up well against what the equivalent budget buys elsewhere.

The Mount Gravatt State High School catchment gives families the secondary school access that many inner east buyers are specifically seeking, and it does so without requiring the Camp Hill or Coorparoo price premium to get there. Westfield Garden City is accessible by bus, which handles the daily retail and convenience picture.

For families doing the maths on where to buy with school, space, and value all in the brief at the same time, Mt Gravatt East comes up as a genuine answer.
