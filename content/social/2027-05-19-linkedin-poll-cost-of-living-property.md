## Caption

The cost of living conversation has been running in Australia for two years now, and it has shifted household budgets in a way that shows up clearly in property decisions. Groceries, insurance, and energy costs have all moved, and for many households the monthly number looks quite different to what it did in 2024.

Whether you are looking to buy, sell, or invest, the calculation has changed for most people. Some have pulled back on their plans. Others have actually accelerated them, reasoning that property is a better long-term position than sitting on cash in a high-cost environment. Neither response is irrational. It depends entirely on your situation.

I am curious where people in my network actually land on this.

Has the cost of living affected your property plans in the past 12 months?
