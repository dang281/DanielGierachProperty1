## Caption

Easter creates a break in the autumn campaign window that vendors planning to list in March or April need to factor in. Properties that launch the week before Easter often lose momentum over the long weekend, when buyers are travelling and attention shifts away from real estate.

The vendors who manage this window best either launch early enough to run two or three full open home weekends before Easter, or they launch the week after Easter when buyers return with renewed focus.

If you are planning to list your home this autumn, when would you go to market relative to Easter?

## Poll options

- Before Easter (enough time to build momentum)
- The week after Easter (fresh start)
- Whenever the property is ready
- Not selling this autumn
