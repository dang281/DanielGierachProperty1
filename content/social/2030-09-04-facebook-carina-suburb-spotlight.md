## Caption

Carina offers something that has become genuinely scarce in the inner east: established homes on blocks large enough to matter, at prices that still make sense for families who have been priced out of Camp Hill. The suburb sits neatly between Carindale's amenity and Camp Hill's character, and it draws buyers who want the best of both without paying a premium for either postcode.

Blocks in Carina regularly run wider and deeper than comparable inner east addresses, which gives buyers real options, whether that means extending, landscaping, or simply having a backyard that functions. That appeal is not lost on the renovation market: a steady flow of buyers is purchasing to improve, and those improved homes are in turn setting new benchmarks for the suburb.

School access is a genuine driver here. Citipointe Christian College and Seton College are both well within reach, and families with children at those schools are active in the market. Quiet residential streets give Carina a neighbourhood feel that its proximity to Old Cleveland Road and the Mount Gravatt corridor makes surprisingly easy to access.

If you own in Carina and have been thinking about your options, the buyer pool is active and motivated. An accurate appraisal is a good place to start.
