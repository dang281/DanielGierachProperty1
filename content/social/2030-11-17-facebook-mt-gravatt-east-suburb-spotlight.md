## Caption

Mt Gravatt East has a buyer profile that is distinctive in the inner east: a high proportion of buyers who know exactly what they want and have narrowed their search deliberately to this suburb, rather than buyers who arrived here after being priced out of somewhere else.

That deliberate buyer reflects the suburb's own clarity of offer. The elevated streets deliver views. The blocks deliver space. The Mansfield State High catchment delivers the secondary school option that families have been researching since their children were in primary school. The combination of those three things in one suburb, at a price point below the riverfront postcodes, has produced consistent buyer demand through the property cycle.

The streets around Gowan Road and the elevated section east of Cavendish Road are where the clearest view positions are found. Vendors with properties in these pockets have historically found that good campaign positioning produces competitive open home attendance and strong auction results.

For investors, Mt Gravatt East has offered reasonable rental yields through 2030, supported by demand from professionals and families seeking the suburb's lifestyle offer without the ownership commitment.

The November market is active for well-positioned properties in Mt Gravatt East.
