## Caption

Brisbane buyers have more ways to find a property than ever. The portals, social media, buyer's agents, and agent databases all play a role. But what actually works? In the inner east, a meaningful proportion of transactions involve a buyer who knew the agent or was in a database before the property went public. Agents with serious buyer networks earn their commission partly through this access. Curious what worked for people in practice.

How did you find your most recent property purchase?
