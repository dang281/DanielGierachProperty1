## Caption

The winter market in the inner east is quieter than autumn and spring. Buyer numbers are lower, open home traffic is more selective, and the pressure that competition creates is harder to manufacture from a smaller buyer pool. For vendors who are in the market right now or planning a campaign soon, I am curious what you are prioritising. In a quieter market, what matters most to you as a seller?

## Poll options

- Getting the right price, even if it takes longer
- Speed of sale, even if it means adjusting the price
- Finding the right buyer, not just the first one
- Minimising the disruption of a campaign
