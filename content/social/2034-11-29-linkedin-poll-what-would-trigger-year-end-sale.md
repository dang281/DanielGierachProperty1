## Caption

A small but consistent number of properties sell in December and early January in Brisbane's inner east. The vendors who list in this window typically have a specific reason for the timing, and the buyers who purchase in this window are often more motivated than the average spring buyer.

For those who are still sitting on a listing decision, the question is often what would need to be in place to make the move.

If you are a homeowner who has been considering selling, what would be the deciding factor that gets you to market before year end?

## Poll options

- The right price expectation confirmed by an appraisal
- Knowing the right buyer is out there now
- Having my next property sorted first
