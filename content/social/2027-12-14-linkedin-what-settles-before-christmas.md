## Caption

A question I am fielding regularly this week: can we still settle before Christmas?

The honest answer depends on where in the process you are.

If you are already under contract with a standard 30 to 42-day settlement and contracts were exchanged in mid to late November, you are tracking to settle before Christmas. PEXA processes most residential settlements electronically, so the December 24 window is achievable for contracts that are clean and fully executed.

If you are still at the open-home stage in the second week of December, the maths are difficult. Even a quick four-week campaign puts you at contract exchange around Christmas, which means a settlement in late January to February.

The practical implication: the last realistic opportunity to launch and settle before Christmas has passed for most properties. That is not a failure of timing, it is just a calendar reality.

What this week is actually useful for, if you are a vendor planning for early 2028, is preparation. Properties that use the quieter December period to complete repairs, style the home, engage a solicitor, and get marketing photography done are the ones that launch in January or February with momentum behind them rather than starting from zero.

The vendors who hit January ready tend to find the early-autumn buyer pool before the competition catches up with them.
