## Caption

Preparing a home for sale involves real decisions about where to spend time and money. The answer is not the same for every property or every market condition, and what feels important to the seller is not always what moves the needle for buyers.

Styling lifts photography. Deferred maintenance protects existing value from being discounted at the inspection stage. Street appeal drives the decision to walk through the door. And sometimes the most valuable thing is getting professional advice before spending a dollar on any of it.

If preparing your home for sale this autumn, what would you tackle first?

## Poll options

- Presentation and styling (it is a photography investment)
- Fixing deferred maintenance (protect existing value)
- Landscaping and street appeal (first impressions drive enquiry)
- Hire an agent first and follow their advice
