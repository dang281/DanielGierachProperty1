## Caption

2032 is genuinely hard to read for Brisbane's inner east and I think it is worth being honest about that rather than projecting false certainty.

The Olympic attention is real and will be at its peak intensity in the first half of the year. The inner east market has run strongly for several years and values have moved a long way. Affordability at current price points is a genuine constraint on the buyer pool. Rate decisions in the first half of 2032 will shape borrowing capacity in a way that is not yet clear. And the question of what comes after the Olympics, whether attention sustains or recedes once the event is over, is genuinely open.

Any honest assessment of 2032 holds all of those variables at once. People who are active in the market will have a view based on their position and their observations. Worth asking.

What do you expect from Brisbane's inner east property market in the first half of 2032?

## Poll options

- Strong continued growth as Olympic attention peaks
- Steady, moderate growth in line with the recent run
- Flat, as affordability puts a ceiling on further gains
- Hard to predict, too many variables in an Olympic year
