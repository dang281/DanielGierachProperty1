## Caption

Open homes in Brisbane's inner east can move quickly. In a competitive autumn market, a well-priced property in Balmoral, Camp Hill, or Carina Heights can receive offers within 48 hours of the first open. Other properties sit for six or seven weekends waiting for the right buyer.

The difference is often price positioning, but buyer decision speed plays a role too. Some buyers know within five minutes of walking through a property whether it works for them. Others need to visit two or three times before they are ready to commit.

How quickly do you typically decide whether to make an offer after inspecting a property?
