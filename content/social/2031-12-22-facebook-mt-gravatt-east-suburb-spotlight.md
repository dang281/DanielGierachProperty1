## Caption

Just before Christmas, the buyers who remain active in Mt Gravatt East are among the most committed you'll see across the whole year. They're not browsing. They've been through the spring market, they know what they want, and they're prepared to act before the break if the right property surfaces.

Mt Gravatt East's location between Holland Park and Carindale gives it an interesting position in the inner south ladder. Buyers who started their search in Camp Hill or Holland Park and kept reaching slightly beyond their ceiling often discover Mt Gravatt East as the place where the block sizes are practical, the garden character is intact, and the price point still makes sense.

Greenslopes Private Hospital's proximity to the north remains one of the suburb's most consistent buyer draws. Healthcare workers who want a short commute are a recurring presence in the buyer pool here and they tend to be realistic, decision-focused buyers who don't require extensive convincing on the suburb's merits.

The Mt Gravatt-Capalaba Road and Newnham Road corridor handles most of the traffic in the suburb, and the streets off those main roads are genuinely quiet. That quietness is what families who move here mention most often when describing what they like about the decision.
