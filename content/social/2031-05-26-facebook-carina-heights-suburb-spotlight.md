## Caption

Carina Heights is a suburb that families tend to discover when they have outgrown their previous home and are searching specifically for more space without making the longer commute that space normally costs in South East Queensland. The lot sizes in Carina Heights allow for pools, substantial alfresco areas, and side access for a boat or trailer, and that combination is meaningful for families at the stage where those things have moved from optional to necessary.

The suburb's elevation, in the sections that sit above the flatter ground toward Carindale, delivers an aspect and a sense of space that the block boundaries alone do not convey. Buyers who visit properties on those elevated sections and look north toward the city skyline tend to understand immediately why the price differential between elevated Carina Heights and the surrounding flat suburbs exists.

The proximity to Westfield Carindale provides Carina Heights residents with a retail and services offer that significantly larger suburbs would be satisfied with. Medical, dental, specialist retail, major supermarkets, and cinema are all available without requiring a trip to a retail strip that does not have parking. For families managing schedules, that matters in a practical sense every week.

Listing volumes in Carina Heights are modest through winter. Vendors who are prepared and committed to a May or June listing are typically dealing with a less competitive vendor environment than the autumn peak provided.
