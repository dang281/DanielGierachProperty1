## Caption

November in Mt Gravatt is when the pre-Christmas selling window opens properly, and the window is shorter than most sellers realise.

Buyers who want to be settled before Christmas are already in the market. They have pre-approval in hand, they have been watching the suburb through spring, and they are ready to commit on a property that presents well. The Upper Mt Gravatt shopping precinct and Westfield Garden City give this suburb a practical liveability story that buyers from further out recognise, but it is the family buyer who already knows the suburb who tends to act fastest in November.

The 1970s and 1980s brick homes that make up much of the residential stock here are not overlooked product. Buyers understand that a well-positioned brick home on a generous suburban block holds land value in a way that newer and smaller formats do not. The block sizes across Mt Gravatt, many of them substantially larger than what equivalent money buys in the inner east, remain a consistent drawcard for families making a long-term purchase decision.

Mt Gravatt State High School is the secondary school anchor that locks in demand at the family home price point. Buyers with children in late primary school who are buying ahead of the secondary catchment are among the most motivated purchasers in the November market.

If you have been waiting for the right time, the pre-Christmas buyer is active now and numbers drop sharply in late December.
