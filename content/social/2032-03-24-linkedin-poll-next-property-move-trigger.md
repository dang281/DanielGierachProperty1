## Caption

Peak autumn campaign season produces more buyer decisions than any other time of year. Some of those decisions are triggered by finding the right property. Others are triggered by something entirely unrelated to market conditions.

In my experience, the factors that actually move people are rarely "the market is right" in isolation. Most property moves happen because something else shifted first: a job, a family change, a financial position reaching the threshold where the move is possible. The market timing is secondary to the personal trigger.

That said, when market conditions and personal circumstances align, the decisions tend to happen quickly. Autumn 2032 in the inner east is producing exactly that kind of alignment for a specific cohort of buyers and vendors.

With autumn campaign season at its peak, what is most likely to trigger your next property move?

## Poll options

- A property coming up that genuinely fits what I am looking for
- Reaching the right equity or financial position
- A change in personal circumstances like family, job, or lifestyle
- Getting a clear view on where the market is heading
