## Caption

With the autumn selling season about four to six weeks away, sellers who are preparing now tend to get better results than those who start when the campaign is already live.

Most homeowners know they need to do something before going to market, but they do not always know where to start. Some pull every room apart and then run out of time for photography. Others spend on renovations that buyers do not price in. Getting the order right matters as much as the work itself.

If you were preparing your inner east home for sale right now, which step would you prioritise first?

## Poll options

- Declutter and deep clean
- Minor repairs and fresh paintwork
- Professional styling and photography
