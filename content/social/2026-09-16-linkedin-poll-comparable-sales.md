## Caption

When an agent gives a price guide, they lean on comparable sales, recent sales of similar properties nearby. But "comparable" is subjective. A sale from six months ago in a rising market can already be well below current value. And cherry-picking the comps that support a preferred narrative is entirely possible without the client knowing. How much weight do you put on them?

When assessing property value, how much do you trust comparable sales?

## Poll Options

- A lot (it's the most objective data we have)
