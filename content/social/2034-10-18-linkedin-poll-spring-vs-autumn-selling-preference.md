## Caption

Brisbane's inner east has two clear selling seasons: spring, which peaks in October and November, and autumn, which tends to peak in March and April. Both produce strong results for the right property. The question is which one works better for your specific situation.

The standard advice is to sell in spring. But spring means competing with more listings, and autumn often delivers a buyer pool that has been watching all through summer and is ready to act.

If you were selling your home in Brisbane's inner east, which season would you choose?
