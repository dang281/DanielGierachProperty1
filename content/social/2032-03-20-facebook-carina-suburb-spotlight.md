## Caption

Carina is a suburb that rewards buyers who look past the main arterial roads and explore the residential streets. The pocket south of Old Cleveland Road, running toward the Wakerley boundary, offers generous blocks with a residential scale that is increasingly hard to find at the price points Carina currently represents.

The suburb's access to public transport via the Carindale bus corridor and the proximity to the Westfield Carindale precinct make it practical as a daily base. Buyers who are commuting to the CBD or to the south-side employment corridor along the Pacific Motorway frequently include Carina in their search area.

The school catchment, covering Carina State School and Citipointe Christian College among the nearby options, is a consistent topic at family open homes. Buyers with children typically have this research done before they step through the door.

March is an active month in Carina. The suburb attracts a cross-section of the buyer market, including upgraders from units and townhouses, downsizers from nearby larger homes, and investors who understand the rental fundamentals in the area. That breadth of buyer interest means that well-presented properties tend to draw genuine competition rather than a single offer.
