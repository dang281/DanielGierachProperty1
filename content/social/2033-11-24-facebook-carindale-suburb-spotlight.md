## Caption

Carindale's most immediate selling point is practical: Westfield Carindale provides grocery, dining, entertainment, and services at a scale that removes most daily errands from the to-do list. Residents are not driving across town to fill gaps that their suburb cannot provide. That convenience is embedded in the address, and buyers who have lived near a major retail and dining precinct before tend to factor it in heavily when comparing their options.

The housing stock in Carindale is characterised by older-style homes on generous blocks, and that combination is specifically what attracts buyers who are looking to add value. A buyer who wants to renovate, extend, or redevelop has land to work with, and the older builds mean there is scope to improve rather than starting from a finished baseline that leaves little upside. ⚠️ VERIFY typical block sizes. For buyers with a longer-term ownership horizon and some appetite for renovation, Carindale offers an opportunity that is harder to find at comparable price points in suburbs closer to the CBD.

Owner-occupier holding rates in Carindale are strong. ⚠️ VERIFY. That low turnover creates a community character that is felt in the suburb's streets and that resonates with families looking for a settled, established neighbourhood rather than one with high transient population. Lower turnover also means that when properties do come to market, the buyer pool takes notice.
