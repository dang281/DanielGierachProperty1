## Caption

Anyone planning to list their home in spring 2033 is already in the preparation window, even if it doesn't feel like it yet. A property that hits the spring market in September needs photography done in August, styling arranged in July, and any pre-campaign repairs or compliance work completed by late June at the latest. That means decisions being made right now. The vendors who treat September as the start date almost always arrive underprepared. The ones who treat May as the planning phase arrive with an advantage over the competition they'll face in spring. If you are thinking about a spring listing, where are you in the process?
