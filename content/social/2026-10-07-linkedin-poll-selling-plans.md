## Caption

October is when vendors who want to sell in the first quarter of next year should be starting to plan. Preparation typically takes six to eight weeks done properly. To list in January or February for a strong autumn campaign, the decisions about styling, repairs, legal preparation, and agent appointment need to happen now. Leaving it until December means either rushing the preparation or pushing the launch to March, which is still a good window but costs you the early-autumn buyer pool. Where are you at with your plans?

Are you planning to sell a property in the next 12 months?
