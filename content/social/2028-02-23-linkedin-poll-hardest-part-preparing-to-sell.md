## Caption

Most sellers know in theory what needs to happen before a property goes to market. In practice, the preparation phase is where the decisions get complicated.

What work needs to be done versus what can be left for the buyer? How much is too much to spend? What does the property actually look like through a buyer's eyes rather than someone who has lived in it for 10 years?

I work through this conversation with sellers regularly and the challenges tend to cluster in a few areas.

What is the hardest part of preparing your home for sale?

Daniel Gierach
Ray White Bulimba · Inner East Brisbane
