## Caption

The RBA's rate decisions through 2025 and into 2026 reshaped borrowing capacity across the market. Whether the subsequent cuts have changed your approach to property depends on where you sit in the cycle: first home buyer, existing owner, investor, or vendor. The effect on actual behaviour is worth asking directly, because the answer varies considerably depending on who you ask.

## Poll options

- Yes, the cuts have made me more likely to act
- No, my plans have not changed
- I was already committed before the rate movements
- Waiting to see if rates move further before deciding
