## Caption

Murarrie is the inner east suburb that most consistently surprises buyers who arrive without preconceptions. The proximity to the Gateway Motorway, the light industrial and commercial strip along Murarrie Road, and the industrial precinct to the north give parts of the suburb a character that buyers from other postcodes sometimes overlook. The residential streets on the high ground tell a different story.

The elevated sections of Murarrie, particularly on Kianawah Road and the streets approaching Tingalpa Creek, deliver the block sizes, the natural setting, and the quietness that buyers looking east from Cannon Hill and Morningside find compelling. Some positions on the ridge have views that reach to Moreton Bay on clear days.

Gateway access is one of the suburb's practical strengths. Buyers who commute to the airport precinct, the Gold Coast corridor, or the southern manufacturing and logistics belt find Murarrie's position highly functional. That buyer profile drives consistent investor and owner-occupier demand alongside the family buyer who values the space.

The suburb's school catchment brings Murarrie students into the Cannon Hill catchment for state primary and into Murarrie State School, with secondary options via the broader inner east network.

For homeowners in Murarrie's elevated streets, the November buyer pool is typically active and informed.
