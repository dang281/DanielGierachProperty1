## Caption

Spring officially starts next week and Brisbane's inner east is returning to a normal market rhythm after several months of Olympic-adjacent distraction.

The question now is how buyers feel about it.

The post-Games period was always going to be a reset point. Buyers who had been waiting have had time to recalibrate. Interest rate conditions, borrowing capacity, and the overall property landscape have not changed dramatically through the Games period, but sentiment is its own factor and it shifts independently of fundamentals.

Listing volumes across the inner east are starting to build again. Open home attendance from the past two weekends is tracking closer to what I would expect for a late August market than what we saw in July.

As spring approaches and the city returns to normal post-Olympics, how would you describe your property confidence in Brisbane's inner east?
