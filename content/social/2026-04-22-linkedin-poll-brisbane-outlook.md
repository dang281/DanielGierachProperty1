## Caption

KPMG forecasts close to 11% house price growth for Brisbane in 2026. Westpac has flagged the city is entering territory where affordability starts to become a genuine constraint.

Strong demand. Limited stock. The cash rate is now at 4.10% after two consecutive rises from the RBA in February and March. At some point, something adjusts.

Where do you see Brisbane property prices heading over the rest of 2026?
