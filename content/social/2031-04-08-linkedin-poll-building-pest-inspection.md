## Caption

A building and pest inspection in Queensland typically costs between $400 and $700 and takes three to five business days. It is standard in most QLD contracts. In a competitive situation, some buyers waive it to make their offer more attractive. The risk is real, particularly in older inner east character homes where concealed defects, termite activity, or structural movement are genuinely possible. The cost of an inspection is modest compared to what can be missed without one. The question buyers face is how much risk they are willing to carry. Would you ever waive the building and pest inspection?
