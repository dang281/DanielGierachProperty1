## Caption

Ask most vendors when they want to sell and the answer is spring. It is deeply embedded in Australian property culture. The gardens look their best, the weather is good for open homes, and the conventional wisdom says spring is when buyers are most active.

In Brisbane's inner east, the evidence is more nuanced. Autumn, from late February to May, consistently produces strong results. Buyer competition tends to be higher relative to available stock, days on market are often shorter, and vendors who list in March or April frequently find less competition from other sellers than they would have in October.

Summer and winter both have their advocates too.

If you were listing your home, which season would you choose?
