## Caption

The sale method is not a neutral administrative choice. It is the single biggest lever a vendor has over how buyer competition develops.

Auction creates a deadline that forces buyers to commit with finance and building and pest complete before the fall of the hammer. Private treaty allows buyers to remain conditional for longer.

Expression of interest suits properties where the vendor wants to receive multiple offers simultaneously but maintain control over which terms they accept.

The right method depends on the nature of the property, the size of the buyer pool, and whether competition between buyers is likely to be immediate or slow to develop.
