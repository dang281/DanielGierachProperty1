## Caption

Carina is a suburb that has absorbed consistent buyer demand from families who are priced out of the inner-east suburbs they originally targeted and arrive in Carina having made a deliberate calculation rather than a disappointed compromise. The proximity to Cannon Hill and Carindale gives Carina residents access to two separate retail and transport hubs, which is a practical advantage that the suburb does not always get credit for in casual market commentary.

The Carina State School draws families who are thinking carefully about the primary years, and the suburb's position relative to the Gateway Motorway makes it workable for households with one partner commuting to locations that are not on a train line. That dual accessibility, by road for one partner and by bus corridor for another, is a real feature of how Carina functions for the families who live here.

The housing stock mixes character homes with newer construction at a ratio that reflects the suburb's development history. The character homes on the older streets in Carina have a price premium over equivalent newer stock, but that premium is more modest than it would be in a suburb closer to the river. For buyers who want a character home without paying the Morningside or Hawthorne premium for one, Carina represents a considered option.

As Brisbane moves into the winter months, the listing volumes in Carina tend to thin slightly. For vendors who are prepared and can list in May, the competition from other vendors is often lower than it was in the autumn peak.
