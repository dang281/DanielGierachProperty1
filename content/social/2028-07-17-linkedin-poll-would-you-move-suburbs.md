## Caption

Brisbane's inner east is a collection of suburbs with genuinely different characters. The choice between Camp Hill and Hawthorne is not just a price difference, it is a lifestyle difference: the ridge versus the river, the school catchment, the commute.

I get asked regularly whether buyers are willing to move suburbs when their first preference becomes unaffordable. The honest answer is: some are and some are not. The ones who are tend to end up happier than they expected, because they were buying lifestyle anyway and just did not know it.

If you had to move somewhere in Brisbane's inner east today, where would you go?

## Poll options

- Hawthorne or Balmoral (river suburbs)
- Camp Hill or Norman Park (ridge suburbs)
- Bulimba or Morningside (village feel)
- Coorparoo or Carina (entry point value)
