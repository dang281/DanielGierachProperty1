## Caption

Carina Heights sits in an interesting position in the inner east buyer's mind. It offers the space and street scale of Carindale with a commute time that puts it within easy reach of the CBD, and buyers who have been priced out of Camp Hill or Coorparoo are increasingly landing here with strong budgets and a clear brief.

The elevated streets on the western side of the suburb, particularly those running off Creek Road toward the ridge, attract the most consistent interest from families upgrading from smaller properties. Blocks here tend to be generous, and the combination of quiet streets and access to the Westfield Carindale precinct makes the suburb genuinely practical to live in day to day.

The autumn campaign season is the strongest selling window for Carina Heights. March open homes draw buyers who have been searching since January and are ready to make decisions. The school catchment question, specifically Gateway College and the independent school options nearby, is one of the first things buyers with children raise at inspections.

Properties that present well and are priced with reference to genuine comparable sales, rather than aspirational figures from adjacent suburbs, are moving cleanly right now.

If you own in Carina Heights and want to understand what your home is worth in the current market, an appraisal takes an hour.
