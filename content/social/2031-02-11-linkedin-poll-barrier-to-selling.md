## Caption

There are more homeowners in Brisbane's inner east who are thinking about selling than are actually listing. The gap between the two is usually explained by one specific thing holding them back.

Sometimes it is uncertainty about price. Sometimes it is not knowing where they will live next. Sometimes the preparation feels too big to start. And sometimes people are simply waiting for a market signal that feels certain enough to act on. Knowing which barrier is most common helps frame the conversation better.

What is the biggest thing stopping homeowners from listing right now?

## Poll options

- Not sure what the property is worth
- Have not found where to buy next
- Preparation feels too overwhelming to start
- Waiting for market conditions to improve
