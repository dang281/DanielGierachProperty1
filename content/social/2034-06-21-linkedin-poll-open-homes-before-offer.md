## Caption

One of the things I track at open homes is how many properties buyers have seen before they attend. Some buyers are decisive early. Others attend 30 or 40 open homes over several months before making an offer. The range is wide and it tells me something about where buyers are in their process and how motivated they are. Thinking back to the last property you purchased, how many open homes did you attend before making an offer on the property you bought?
