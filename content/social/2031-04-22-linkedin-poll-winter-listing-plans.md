## Caption

The conventional advice is to wait for spring. In Brisbane's inner east, the argument for winter is different from the Melbourne or Sydney seasonal case. Mild weather means presentation is rarely a problem. A smaller buyer pool sounds like a disadvantage until you consider that it also means fewer comparable listings in your street competing for the same buyers. The decisive winter buyer is typically not shopping speculatively. There is a genuine question here about whether the timing advantage of less competition outweighs the narrower overall pool. If you were considering selling, when would you list?
