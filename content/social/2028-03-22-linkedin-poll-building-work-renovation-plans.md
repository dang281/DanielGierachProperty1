## Caption

Building and renovation costs in Brisbane have remained significantly above pre-2020 levels, driven by the sustained labour and materials pressure that followed the pandemic construction surge. The Olympics infrastructure pipeline has added further demand to an already constrained trade labour market.

For homeowners thinking about renovating before selling or improving their property, the cost-versus-return calculation has changed. A $60,000 kitchen renovation that might have earned back its cost five years ago is a harder argument to make when trade quotes come in at $80,000 to $100,000 for the same scope.

I am curious how this is landing in practice for people in my network.

Has the current cost of building work changed your renovation plans?
