## Caption

Spring is the most competitive selling season in Brisbane's inner east, which makes the choice of sale method more consequential than at other times of year. Auction works well when buyer depth is strong and you want competition to set the price. Private treaty suits sellers who want more control over the process and timeline. EOI gives you a broad campaign with a single decision point, which some sellers find easier to manage. And plenty of vendors simply back their agent's judgement on what the market calls for. If you were selling your home this spring, which sale method would you prefer?
