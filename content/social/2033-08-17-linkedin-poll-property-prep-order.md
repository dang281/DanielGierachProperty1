## Caption

The order in which sellers prepare a property for campaign matters more than most vendors initially realise. Doing things in the wrong sequence wastes money, creates rework, and results in photography that captures the work in progress rather than the finished result. In Brisbane's inner east, where the buyer pool includes experienced buyers who scrutinise presentation closely, the sequence of preparation is part of the strategy. If you were preparing your home for sale right now, which category would you address first?

## Poll options

- Condition and repairs (roof, plumbing, structural items)
- Visual presentation (paint, gardens, floors)
- Styling and decluttering (furniture, clean lines for photos)
- I would get an agent's advice before doing anything
