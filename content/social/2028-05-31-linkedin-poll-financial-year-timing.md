## Caption

30 June is a real deadline for property investors. Settlement timing determines which financial year a capital gain lands in, and for some vendors the difference between settling before or after that date is a significant tax conversation. Some campaigns are deliberately structured so vendors can control exactly when settlement occurs. Buyers with investment properties are often reviewing their position each July, which shapes when they are ready to transact again. It is a factor that experienced investors build into their decisions almost instinctively. For others it has never come up. Curious how many people in this audience actively factor it in. Does the end of financial year influence your property decisions?
