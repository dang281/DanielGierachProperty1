## Caption

The spring market in Brisbane's inner east is now open. After the expected winter compression of stock and buyer numbers, the question is what the buyer pool looks like as it re-enters the market. Strong spring markets in the inner east typically show motivated buyers who were patient through winter and are now ready to move decisively on the right property. Softer springs show buyers who are more cautious, more price-sensitive, and willing to wait for more stock before committing. If you are currently looking to buy in Brisbane's inner east, how would you describe conditions so far this spring?
