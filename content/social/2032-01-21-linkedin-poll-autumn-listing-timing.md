## Caption

Brisbane's inner east has a consistent autumn campaign window that outperforms spring in most years. For vendors planning to sell in 2032, the first half of the year is the stronger half, and the Olympic Games running from late July means the autumn window has additional buyer demand from people who want to be settled before July.

The practical question is where within the autumn season you aim to list. February campaigns avoid the Easter break but launch into a market where vendor preparation is often rushed. March and April are the peak weeks in most years. May is still workable but begins to compete with the approach of the June-July dip.

If you were selling your inner east home this autumn, when would you aim to have it on the market?

## Poll options
