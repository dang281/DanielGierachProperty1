## Caption

The decision to call an agent for a property appraisal is almost always driven by something specific. It might be a sale in the street that looked surprisingly strong. A life event that has changed the calculus around where you are living. A growing sense that the timing is close, without a clear trigger yet. Or simply wanting an updated number to inform a decision that is not yet made. Understanding what prompts that first conversation helps me understand who is close to making a decision and who needs more information before they get there. If you were considering selling your Brisbane inner east property, what would most likely prompt you to pick up the phone?

## Poll options

- A strong sale nearby made me curious about my own value
- A life change (job, family, downsizing) has created the timing
- I want a number before I decide anything else
- I am essentially ready, just finalising the decision
