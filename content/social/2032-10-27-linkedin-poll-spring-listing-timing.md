## Caption

Spring in Brisbane's inner east runs through October and November. October delivers the largest motivated buyer pool, with buyers who want to settle before Christmas working to a late-November exchange deadline. November brings that pre-Christmas urgency to a point: buyers who have not found anything are ready to move fast. December is quieter, but the buyers still active in that month tend to be decisive and are often less concerned about conditions. Each window has a different buyer profile. If you are planning a spring campaign, the timing shapes who you are selling to and how that campaign is structured. Where do you sit?
