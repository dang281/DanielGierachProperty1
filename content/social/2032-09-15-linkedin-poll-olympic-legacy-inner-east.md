## Caption

The 2032 Brisbane Olympics closed five weeks ago. The infrastructure, the venues, and the transport upgrades that came with the Games are now a permanent part of the city.

For Brisbane's inner east, the question that matters more than what happened during the Games period is what the lasting legacy looks like over the next decade. Several things have changed that were not in place five years ago: Cross River Rail is open, the Gabba has been redeveloped, the city's profile has shifted internationally.

Which of these do you think will have the most durable positive impact on inner east property?

## Poll options

- Transport upgrades (Cross River Rail, improved bus corridors)
- Sporting and community venue upgrades (Gabba, Chandler)
- Improved city reputation and interstate migration
- The infrastructure investment into the CBD and surrounds
