## Caption

The Olympics brings a lot of short-term noise to the rental market: properties let on short-term platforms during the Games period, longer-term leases paused or ended to capture that, and tenants displaced or choosing to move ahead of the disruption. What happens after is less talked about.

Past host cities have seen very different outcomes. Some saw rents push higher in the months after the Games as the city settled back into normal demand patterns. Others saw little movement. Brisbane's inner east has its own supply and demand dynamics that may or may not follow those patterns.

I am curious what people actually think will happen here. What is your expectation for inner east rents in the six months after the Games close?
