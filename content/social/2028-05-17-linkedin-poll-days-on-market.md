## Caption

Days on market is one of the most honest indicators of real market conditions. When properties sell quickly, buyers are competing. When they sit, something has shifted, whether that is price, presentation, or buyer depth. It is a metric that cuts through the noise of headline commentary. The gap between perception and reality on this number tends to be significant. People who are not actively shopping often assume conditions are either faster or slower than they actually are. I am curious where perceptions sit right now. How long do you think homes are taking to sell in Brisbane's inner east right now?
