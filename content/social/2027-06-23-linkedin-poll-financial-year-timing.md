## Caption

The Australian financial year ends on 30 June, and for property investors that date is a real consideration. Whether to settle before or after 30 June can shift how capital gains are treated, which depreciation schedules apply, and in some cases whether a loss falls into the current or the next year's return. Not every investor thinks this through before they list. Some discover the implications after contracts are already signed, which is too late to do much about it. Curious how many people have actually factored this into their timing.
