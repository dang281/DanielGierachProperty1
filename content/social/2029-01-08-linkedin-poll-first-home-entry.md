## Caption

Brisbane's entry-level market has shifted significantly over the past three years. The gap between renting and buying has changed for different buyer profiles, government support schemes have been adjusted, and lender servicing calculations have moved in both directions.

For first home buyers looking at Brisbane's inner east suburbs, the decision about when and how to enter is more layered than it was a few years ago.

If you are a first home buyer, or know one currently trying to enter the market: what is the single biggest barrier right now?

## Poll options

- Deposit gap while rents keep rising
- Borrowing capacity at current rates
- Finding the right property within budget
