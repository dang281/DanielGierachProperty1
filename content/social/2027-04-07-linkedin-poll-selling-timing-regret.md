## Caption

Timing a property sale is one of the hardest calls to get right. You can have the right property in the right suburb and still wonder afterwards whether you listed a month too early or waited six months too long.

The autumn window is well underway in Brisbane. Buyer demand is strong, stock is reasonably tight, and the window typically starts to narrow from June. But the right time to sell is different for every vendor, and the honest answer often has less to do with market conditions than with personal circumstances.

Have you ever sold a property and wished you had listed at a different time?
