## Caption

Vendors preparing for spring campaigns often focus on what's visible. What buyers react to is sometimes different. At a spring inspection, first impressions form within about ninety seconds of walking through the door. Smell, light, space, and price all register quickly, and any one of them can end a buyer's interest before they've seen the backyard. If you're thinking about listing in the next few weeks, knowing what most often switches buyers off is useful intel. What is it for you?

## Poll options

- Smell or general presentation
- Layout or floorplan issues
- Price feels off the market
- Lack of natural light
