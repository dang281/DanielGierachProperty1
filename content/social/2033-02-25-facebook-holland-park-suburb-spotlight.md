## Caption

Holland Park in the autumn campaign window is a different suburb from the one buyers overlook in quieter months. The stock that comes to market here between February and April tends to draw a buyer pool that is both broader and more committed than the suburb's mid-tier positioning might suggest.

The post-war timber homes that define Holland Park's character are reaching a critical point of renovation cycle. Many of the original homes on the street are now two or three renovations behind the leading examples in the suburb. That gap between the best-presented and the least-presented creates a pricing range that offers genuine opportunity at both ends: for vendors who have invested, for buyers willing to do the work. ⚠️ VERIFY

Wellers Hill State School continues to draw school-zone buyers who make a deliberate calculation about value relative to the Camp Hill and Coorparoo alternatives. The catchment demand here is real and consistent, and agents who sell in Holland Park without actively marketing the school zone are leaving a buyer cohort on the table.

The Logan Road corridor offers the suburb's daily service infrastructure, and the frequency of buses along that corridor provides transit options that reduce car dependency for commuting households.

For vendors in late February 2033, the timing is right and the buyer pool is active. A well-prepared Holland Park home, correctly priced for the current comparable sales, is in a good position heading into the peak autumn weeks.
