## Caption

Since 2020, the dedicated home office has moved from a nice-to-have to something that significantly influences buyer decisions in the inner east. Properties with a genuinely usable fourth room or separate study regularly attract a different buyer profile from those without.

The question is how much it moves the price. In some segments, particularly families buying in Camp Hill and Carina who need a room that functions as a home office and a guest room, the difference is measurable. In others, the layout premium is modest.

Would you pay more for a property with a dedicated home office?
