## Caption

People make property decisions over the summer holidays more than at any other time of year. Long weekends, family conversations, and time away from work routine tend to produce clarity on property goals that October and November do not.

For the inner east, the February autumn market is when those summer decisions land in the form of appraisal requests, buyer registrations, and new listings. The pipeline for a February campaign is built in December.

Thinking about 2031, what is your primary property plan?
