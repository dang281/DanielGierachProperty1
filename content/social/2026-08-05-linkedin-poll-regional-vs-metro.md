## Caption

Regional markets got a serious boost post-COVID. Some have since pulled back; others held ground. Metro offers liquidity and long-term demand. Regional offers yield and lower entry, but higher vacancy risk. The answer isn't obvious, and it shifts depending on your stage, your equity position, and how closely you can manage the asset. Where would you place your next dollar?

For your next property purchase, which would you choose?
