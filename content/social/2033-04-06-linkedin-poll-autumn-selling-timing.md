## Caption

Most vendors in Brisbane's inner east assume spring is the only serious selling window. Autumn tells a different story. Buyer motivation tends to be higher in April and May because many of these buyers have been searching since January and are ready to act. Listing competition is also lower, which changes the dynamic at open homes and at negotiating tables. I'm curious what drives timing decisions for people who have thought about selling. When would you most likely list your home?
