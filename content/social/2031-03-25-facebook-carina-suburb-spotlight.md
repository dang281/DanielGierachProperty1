## Caption

A significant portion of Carina's buyer pool arrives here after running out of reach in Camp Hill or Norman Park. They arrive knowing exactly what they want. They have seen it in the suburbs they could not quite afford. Carina gives it to them at a price that works.

The suburb offers block sizes that its more prominent neighbours no longer reliably provide. Creek frontage sections along the waterways that thread through the suburb's lower streets give buyers a land premium that inner east suburbs closer to the river cannot replicate at comparable prices. The adjacency to the Belmont Hills corridor provides the same green buffer that makes Carina Heights appealing on the slope above. Carindale Shopping Centre sits at the suburb's eastern edge, handling the day-to-day retail requirements without the suburb itself having to absorb the traffic.

The character housing stock in Carina runs from well-maintained post-war homes to larger family blocks that have been updated over the decades. For the buyer who has been circling Camp Hill and found every open home either overpriced or underwhelming, Carina regularly provides the property they were actually looking for, on a block they did not expect, at a number that allows them to breathe.

In late March, those buyers are active and ready to make decisions before Easter ends the momentum of the autumn market.
