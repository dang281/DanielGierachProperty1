## Caption

Timing a property sale is one of those decisions that only looks obvious in hindsight.

Plenty of homeowners look back and feel they sold at the wrong point in the cycle. Either the market ran hard after they settled, or they held on too long into a softening period. A small number feel they got it right. And a lot of people in the inner east are still waiting for their first sale. The honest answer to this question tends to surface some useful conversations about what drives the decision to sell and when.

Have you ever sold a property and felt the timing was wrong?
