## Caption

We are three months into 2033. The first full post-Olympic quarter is in its final weeks. January set the scene, February opened the autumn season, and March has brought the peak campaign activity that the inner east is known for.

Whether 2033 has delivered on expectations depends enormously on what those expectations were coming in. Vendors who prepared well and priced honestly have seen a consistent market. Buyers who understood that inner east supply is genuinely limited have found properties to buy. Those who came in expecting either a market correction or a continued surge have had a more complicated experience.

Looking at Q1 2033, has Brisbane's inner east delivered on what you expected?
