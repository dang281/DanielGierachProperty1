## Caption

Murarrie offers the most affordable detached housing price point in Brisbane's inner east, combined with block sizes that are larger than you would find in comparable-priced suburbs closer to the city. For buyers who want land, a genuine detached home, and access to the inner east amenity corridor, Murarrie delivers a combination that is increasingly hard to find at this price level.

The Gateway Motorway proximity is a practical convenience for residents who need road access to the airport, the Gold Coast corridor, or the broader Brisbane network. Combined with the established retail, dining, and services amenity of Morningside and Cannon Hill within easy reach, the daily-life quality of Murarrie is well above what its price point would suggest from the outside.

What I observe in Murarrie is a suburb that is further along its residential transition than the current price point reflects. The demographic change that has already happened in Morningside and Cannon Hill over the past decade is a process Murarrie is in the earlier stages of, and buyers who understand that pattern are factoring it into their purchase decisions.

The buyer profile is typically first-time buyers and investors who have researched the relative value position carefully. They have made a considered assessment of where genuine value sits in the inner east and identified Murarrie as the answer before the broader market prices it in.
