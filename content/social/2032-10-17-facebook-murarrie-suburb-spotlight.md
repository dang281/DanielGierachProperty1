## Caption

Murarrie does not get the same level of attention as its inner east neighbours, but the buyers who know it tend to know it well. The suburb offers inner east proximity at a price point that still makes sense for families who want more for their money, and that value argument has been drawing a steady stream of buyers across the past several years.

Gateway Motorway access is a practical advantage that matters to households with work commitments in multiple directions. The connectivity is straightforward and there is no navigating through suburban streets to reach it. Murarrie State School serves the local families, and the suburb has been gentrifying at a measured pace, with owner-occupiers gradually taking over from the investor mix that dominated a decade ago.

The shift in that owner-occupier composition tends to lift presentation standards over time, which in turn brings more family buyers into the search. October is a reasonable window to bring a well-maintained Murarrie property to market, particularly as buyers priced out of Cannon Hill and Coorparoo widen their search radius.

If you own in Murarrie and have been thinking about what your property would realistically achieve in this market, I am happy to provide an honest assessment.
