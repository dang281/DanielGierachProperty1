## Caption

Oxford Street is one of the best-performing café strips in Brisbane, and it sits at the centre of a suburb that has one of the most consistent buyer demand profiles in the inner east. Bulimba draws families and professionals in roughly equal measure, and both cohorts are active year-round. The result is a market where low days on market is the norm, not the exception.

The housing mix runs from character Queenslanders on elevated blocks to post-war homes close to the river, with Ferry Road and the Bulimba Ferry adding a commuting option that genuinely changes how buyers think about distance to the CBD. Bulimba State School is the catchment anchor for families, and the walkability of the suburb means that once people arrive, they rarely want to leave.

Homes close to Stratton Street and the river corridor attract the strongest competition. Well-presented character homes with northerly aspects and outdoor living spaces are what buyers in this suburb will pay a genuine premium for. Post-war homes on larger blocks attract a parallel buyer pool with renovation intent and the budget to follow through.

If you own in Bulimba and have been thinking about selling, the depth of buyer interest here means you have real options. Start with an accurate appraisal.
