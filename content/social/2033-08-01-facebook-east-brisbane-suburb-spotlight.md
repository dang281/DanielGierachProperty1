## Caption

East Brisbane is one of the inner city's quietly consistent performers. Positioned between Woolloongabba and Kangaroo Point on the inner southern bank, the suburb is two kilometres from the CBD, walkable to the Gabba precinct, and served by excellent public transport including direct bus routes and proximity to the South Bank and Woolloongabba rail connections.

The housing stock in East Brisbane is a mix that reflects the suburb's compressed geography and inner-city location: character cottages and Queenslanders on modest lots, contemporary townhouses and infills, and a proportion of apartment product that provides entry-level and investor-grade options. That diversity makes East Brisbane accessible to a wider buyer profile than many inner east suburbs.

The precinct transformation around Woolloongabba and the ongoing development of the Boggo Road urban renewal site has increased the amenity surrounding East Brisbane significantly. Buyers who purchase in East Brisbane today have access to a services and lifestyle layer that is considerably richer than it was a decade ago.

The proximity to the inner city without the inner city price tag has made East Brisbane a consistent focus for buyers who are making a deliberate trade-off: a smaller footprint in exchange for a location that delivers more of what they want from daily urban living.
