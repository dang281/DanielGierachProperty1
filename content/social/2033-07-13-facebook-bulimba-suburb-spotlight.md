## Caption

Bulimba is the inner east suburb where the lifestyle offering, the river frontage, the Oxford Street dining precinct, the park runs and ferry connections, is matched by a property market that reflects that desirability consistently. It is not a suburb that surprises buyers with its price level. Buyers who choose Bulimba have done the research and made the decision that the combination of lifestyle, location, and long-term capital growth justifies the entry price.

The Oxford Street and Riding Road area represents one of the best urban restaurant and lifestyle precincts in Brisbane. Buyers are not just purchasing a home in Bulimba. They are purchasing proximity to a neighbourhood that has invested in itself and shows it. The coffee, the dinner tables, the river access, the weekend farmers' markets nearby: these are real and daily.

The housing stock in Bulimba ranges from pre-war character cottages and Queenslanders on smaller lots to contemporary architect-designed homes on the larger flood-free elevated blocks. The school catchment to Bulimba State School is a significant demand driver for families, and the catchment boundary is well known and respected in the price evidence.

Bulimba's consistent position in the upper tier of the inner east market reflects a buyer base that understands long-term value in property and has chosen this suburb deliberately.
