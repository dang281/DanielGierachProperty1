## Caption

The Brisbane 2032 Olympics has been a fixture in property conversations for nearly a decade. Early buyers in the inner east cited it as a factor. Developers referenced it in feasibility studies. Media covered it in wave after wave of analysis.

Now with the Games less than 12 months away, the question is no longer speculative. It is a real one. Has the inner east market actually repriced because of the Olympics, or was the premium absorbed years ago when the announcement was fresh?

At inspections and in appraisal conversations, I hear different views on this. Some people are convinced the Olympics has added real value. Others think it was a story that the market absorbed in 2025 and has since moved on. Worth asking directly.

How much do you think the Brisbane 2032 Games have added to inner east property values?

## Poll options

- A lot, more than 10% above where prices would otherwise be
- Some, roughly 5 to 10% above where they would otherwise be
- Marginal, the Olympics effect was priced in years ago
- Honestly hard to separate from the broader market
