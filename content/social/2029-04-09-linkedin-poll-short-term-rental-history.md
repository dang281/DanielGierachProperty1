## Caption

Short-term rental platforms have made it common for inner east properties to operate as holiday lets for years before coming to market as a private sale. The wear and tear pattern on a property used as a short-term rental is different from a long-term tenancy or owner-occupied home.

If you were buying in Brisbane's inner east and the property had been running as a short-term rental for the past two to three years, how would that affect your decision?

## Poll options

- No issue, I would price in the wear

- I would want a price reduction
