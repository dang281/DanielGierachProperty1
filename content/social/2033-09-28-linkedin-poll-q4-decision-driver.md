## Caption

Q4 in Brisbane's inner east runs from the spring peak through October and into the pre-Christmas selling window, before the traditional December slowdown. The decisions made in September and October about whether to buy, sell, or hold are shaped by a different combination of factors than the decisions made at the start of the year. Interest rates, life circumstances, stock availability, and the time pressure of wanting to be settled before the end of the year all influence the calculation differently at this point in the cycle. What is driving your property thinking right now?

## Poll options

- Life change that requires a property decision
- Wanting to be settled before end of year
