## Caption

We are officially in the Brisbane Olympic year. The Games run from 23 July to 8 August 2032, and the inner east suburbs have been part of the buyer conversation for years in anticipation of this.

For some people, the Olympic context has actively shaped decisions: timing a purchase to be settled before the Games, or timing a sale to capture elevated buyer interest in the first half of the year. For others, personal circumstances and long-held plans take precedence regardless of the calendar. Both positions are entirely rational.

Curious where people actually land on this.
