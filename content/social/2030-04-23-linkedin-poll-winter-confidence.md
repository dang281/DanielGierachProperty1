## Caption

As autumn wraps up and winter approaches, buyer and seller behaviour in Brisbane's inner east shifts. Some sellers pull back and wait for spring. Some buyers, now less distracted by open home volume, can actually move faster in a quieter market.

The conventional wisdom is that winter is a slow period for property. The data in Brisbane, particularly in the inner east, is more nuanced than that.

How are you feeling about the Brisbane inner east property market heading into winter?
