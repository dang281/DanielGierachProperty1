## Caption

Most vendors pick the wrong order when they start preparing to sell, and it costs them time and sometimes money. Styling before fixing maintenance issues means the camera captures problems in high resolution alongside your beautiful cushions. Starting with comparable sales research before understanding the property's actual condition leads to a pricing conversation that has to be revisited later. The sequence matters. Curious what most people's instinct is here.

When preparing to sell your home this spring, which would you prioritise first?

## Poll options

- Presentation: styling, declutter, clean
- Maintenance: fixing anything that needs work
- Research: comparable sales, pricing strategy
- Choosing the agent
