## Caption

Winter in Brisbane's property market carries a reputation for being slow, and in some segments it is. But the inner east tends to behave differently from the city-wide trend. The pool of active buyers shrinks, but the quality of the buyers who remain is often high. Lower listing volumes mean less competition for vendors willing to go to market. The open homes are smaller but the conversations are more serious. Whether you are buying or selling, how you read the winter market determines your strategy. What is your view of the Brisbane property market heading into winter?
