## Caption

Norman Park does not shout about itself. It does not need to.

Four kilometres from the CBD, river views from the high streets, catchment for Norman Park State School, and the kind of leafy quiet that people move to the inner east specifically to find. The suburb ticks the boxes that matter most to the families who end up here, and once they arrive, they tend to stay.

When a home does come to market, that loyalty translates into genuine competition among buyers who know exactly what they are looking for.

If you have been sitting on a decision about whether to sell, the honest answer is: the buyers are there. The question is whether the timing works for you.

Happy to chat if you are curious about what your Norman Park home is worth right now.
