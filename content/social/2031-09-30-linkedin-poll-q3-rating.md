## Caption

Q3 2031 covered the winter market through July and August and the opening of spring in September. The quarter began with a small but motivated buyer pool, moved through the pre-spring August period where preparation-ready vendors often achieved strong results, and opened into the spring campaign window with buyer demand building from late August. How the quarter actually felt depended significantly on what you were doing in the market and in which suburb. Looking back, how would you rate it?
