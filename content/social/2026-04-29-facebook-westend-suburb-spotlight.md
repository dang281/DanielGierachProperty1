## Caption

West End is one of those suburbs that has been changing steadily for years, and the buyers have noticed.

The character of the area — the markets on Boundary Street, the independent cafes and restaurants, the proximity to the South Bank precinct and the river, the university crowd that gives the place its energy — attracts a buyer who is not looking for a quiet cul-de-sac. They want to be in it.

Character homes and renovated cottages here are seeing genuine buyer interest, and the gentrification of the broader precinct continues to lift the suburb's long-term appeal.

If you own in West End and want to know what your property is worth in the current market, I am happy to have that conversation.
