## Caption

Q1 2033 ends this week. Three months of post-Olympic market activity have played out across the inner east: the autumn season launched, peak campaign weeks came and went, and we are now in the narrowing window before Easter changes the rhythm.

For most people in the property market, the end of Q1 is a moment to take stock. Did the year go to plan? Did the property decision you intended to make happen, or is it still pending? And if it is still pending, when does it actually happen?

The Brisbane inner east market does not stop in Q2, but the character of it changes after Easter. Winter brings lighter stock, different buyer behaviour, and different decision timelines. Understanding what you are planning for the next three months matters now.
