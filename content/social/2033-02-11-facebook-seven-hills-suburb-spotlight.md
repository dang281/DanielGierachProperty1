## Caption

Seven Hills comes up less often in property conversations than its positioning deserves. The suburb sits east of Eight Mile Plains with a bush character that feels a long way from the city even though the distance is modest. Mt Gravatt-Capalaba Road provides the practical connection, and the combination of reserve access and larger land parcels makes this a suburb that rewards buyers who do their own research rather than following the crowd.

Acreage and near-acreage properties in Seven Hills are genuinely rare at the inner east edge. The block sizes here are not a marketing description. They are a structural feature of how the suburb developed, and the bush setting around Whites Hill reinforces the sense of space in a way that neighbouring suburbs cannot replicate. ⚠️ VERIFY

The Whites Hill State College catchment is a pull factor for family buyers who have researched their school options and made a deliberate choice. The school draws families who value a community-oriented, slightly less urban school environment, and the catchment zone has become a genuine motivation for buyers looking in the area.

February is the point in the year when families who want to be in a new home before the middle of the school year are starting to act with urgency. If you own acreage or a larger-format property in Seven Hills, right now is when the buyer who needs to move this year is looking.
