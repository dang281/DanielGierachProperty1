## Caption

Every year, sellers who intended to list in the spring window arrive at late October or November and find a reason to delay. Sometimes the reason is legitimate. Often it is one of a handful of recurring hesitations that I hear consistently.

The cost of waiting is real, even if it is invisible until you are on the other side of the decision. A January launch after a spring delay means competing with every other seller who made the same choice, and without the buyer pool that carried over from spring.

If a homeowner in Brisbane's inner east was ready to sell but decided to wait, what would most likely be the reason?

## Poll options

- Waiting to see if prices move higher
- Not ready to decide where to buy next
- Home still needs preparation work
- Timing around work, family, or school
