## Caption

The Olympics closed three weeks ago and September has arrived. The traditional spring selling season in Brisbane is opening.

Listing volumes tend to increase through September and October. Buyers who sat out the Games period are re-entering the market. The question is how many sellers are ready and what buyer depth looks like after a compressed midyear period.

Spring in Brisbane's inner east looks different to the Sydney and Melbourne spring advice you see in national media. The window here is real but the dynamic is local.

What are your property plans for the rest of the year?
