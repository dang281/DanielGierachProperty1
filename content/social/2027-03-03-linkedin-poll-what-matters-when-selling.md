## Caption

When a homeowner makes the decision to sell, there are usually a handful of things they are optimising for. The sale price is obvious. But in practice, how that price is achieved, the certainty of the deal, and the experience along the way all matter too.

Sellers who have been through a campaign before often tell me the process was more stressful than they expected. The ones who tell me it was fine are almost always the ones who had clear expectations set at the start: what the property would achieve, how long the campaign would run, and what would happen at each step.

The goal is not just a good number. It is a good outcome, which includes the number, the terms, and the experience of getting there.

When selling a property, what would you say matters most to you?

## Poll options

- Achieving the highest possible price
- A fast, clean sale with no complications
- Certainty (unconditional offer, no fallthrough risk)
- A smooth process from listing to settlement
