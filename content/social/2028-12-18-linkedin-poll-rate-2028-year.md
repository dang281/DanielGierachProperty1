## Caption

2028 has been a year where Brisbane property delivered different experiences depending on where you stood. Sellers of well-located inner east properties have generally had a functional market. Buyers have navigated a market where the best properties still attract competition. Investors have dealt with a more complex cost environment.

Whether 2028 was a good year for you in property depends almost entirely on what you were trying to do and where you were doing it.

Looking back at 2028, how would you rate it as a year for Brisbane property overall?

## Poll options

- Strong: better than I expected
- Solid: in line with expectations
- Mixed: depended heavily on location and timing
- Disappointing: the market did not deliver what I hoped
