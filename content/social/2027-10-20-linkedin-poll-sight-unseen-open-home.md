## Caption

Virtual inspections, 3D walkthroughs, and video tours have made it possible to assess a property in detail without attending in person. In practice, most buyers in Brisbane still want to walk through before making an offer. But the technology has changed how much information buyers gather before their first physical visit, and for some buyers, particularly investors or interstate purchasers, a physical inspection is not always possible. Worth asking how people draw the line.
