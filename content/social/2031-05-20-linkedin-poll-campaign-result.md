## Caption

Campaign results in Brisbane's inner east vary more than vendors typically anticipate before they go through the process. The same property, launched in different seasons, with different presentation, marketing quality, and method of sale, can produce meaningfully different outcomes. The gap between what a vendor expects going in and what the campaign actually delivers is one of the more useful conversations to have before committing to a strategy. Understanding what drives that gap, on either side of the expectation, is relevant for anyone considering a sale in the next year or two. Have you ever sold and been surprised by the result?
