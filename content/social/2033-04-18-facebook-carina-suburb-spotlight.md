## Caption

Carina is a suburb that delivers on the fundamentals: good land sizes, solid housing stock, practical access to services and a price point that remains accessible relative to what is on offer closer to the city. At around nine kilometres from the CBD, it sits in the middle ring that continues to attract families who have done the sums and concluded that the trade-off in distance is worth it for the space and value they receive.

Westfield Carindale, just across the suburb boundary, is a genuine asset for everyday convenience. Shopping, dining and services are all within a short drive or walk, which reduces the friction of suburban living and makes Carina an easier sell to buyers used to inner-city convenience. Families also note the school options in the area as a positive factor in their decision-making.

The housing stock in Carina includes original 1960s and 1970s homes on good blocks alongside more recently renovated properties. That mix creates buying opportunities at different price points, from the entry-level home needing work through to the finished family home ready to move into. Current medians in Carina reflect a market where quality and presentation still matter and where unrenovated homes with development potential attract investor interest alongside owner-occupiers.

Bus services connect Carina to the CBD and surrounding centres, and road access via major arterials is straightforward.
