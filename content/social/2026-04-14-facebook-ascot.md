## Caption

Ascot is one of Brisbane's most consistently sought-after addresses, and the numbers reflect it.

The median house price in Ascot currently sits at approximately $2,550,000, with annual capital growth of around 13% over the past 12 months. That kind of sustained growth in a prestige market is not common, and it tells you something about how buyers view this suburb's long-term position.

What drives it? The school catchment for Ascot State School remains one of the most sought-after in Brisbane. The proximity to Hamilton's dining and retail precinct, the heritage streetscapes, and genuinely limited supply of quality homes all contribute. Buyers in this price bracket are not in a hurry, but when the right property comes up, competition is real.
