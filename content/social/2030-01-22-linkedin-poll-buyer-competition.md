## Caption

The best way to read a market is to be inside it, attending inspections and tracking what is happening from offer through to contract. Data tells you what happened; the open home tells you what is about to happen.

January's buyer pool in Brisbane's inner east is smaller than March's, but the buyers who are active now tend to be serious. They didn't take a break from searching. They came back from the holiday period with a clear idea of what they want and what they are prepared to pay.

By mid-February, that pool deepens significantly. Understanding the conditions now gives sellers useful intelligence for launch timing and pricing conversations.

As a buyer in Brisbane's inner east right now, how would you describe competition at inspections?
