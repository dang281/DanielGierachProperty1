## Caption

The autumn selling window in Brisbane's inner east has opened.

Late February through to early May is the most consistent period of the year for well-presented properties in this part of the city. The heat has broken enough to make open homes comfortable, serious buyers are back from the Christmas period with finance sorted, and listing volumes have not yet surged to the point where competition between sellers becomes a factor.

The properties I see sell for the strongest results in autumn are not always the ones with the biggest marketing budgets. They are the ones where the preparation was done in January and the launch was clean.

If you have been considering listing in 2028 and have been waiting for the right moment, this is the window. The buyers who are active right now are not browsing. They have usually been looking for several months and are ready to act when the right property appears.

The conversation about whether autumn makes sense for your property is worth having now, not in March when the window is already running.
