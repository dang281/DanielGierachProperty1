## Caption

Carindale is a suburb built for families who want space, amenity, and good schools without the price pressure of the closer-in inner east. The homes here tend to be larger than you find in Coorparoo or Camp Hill, the blocks are more generous, and the Westfield Carindale shopping centre provides a level of day-to-day convenience that many comparable suburbs simply do not have on their doorstep.

The buyer profile in Carindale is strongly family-oriented. School catchments for Carindale State School and Citipointe Christian College both drive motivated enquiry, and upgrader buyers arriving from smaller homes elsewhere in the east represent a consistent thread through the market. These are buyers making a considered long-term decision, not reactive purchases.

Larger family homes in good condition on well-sized blocks attract the strongest competition at inspection. Buyers who have been looking at Carindale for a period move quickly when the right property comes to market because they understand that well-presented stock at fair prices does not stay available for long. Properties that are priced and presented correctly tend to generate multiple offers.

Owners in Carindale who purchased when the suburb was underpriced relative to its amenity have accumulated equity that deserves a serious look. The upgrader cycle here is active and the market rewards preparation.

If you own in Carindale and want to understand what selling looks like in the current environment, the conversation starts here.
