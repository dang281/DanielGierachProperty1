## Caption

Autumn is Brisbane's most consistent selling window, and the inner east is where that pattern shows up most clearly.

Buyers in this precinct are typically active from mid-January, and by February the competitive conditions that define a good campaign are already in place. Most sellers who outperform their expectations list before Easter, not because of luck but because the buyer pool is at its deepest and most motivated before the mid-year slowdown sets in.

The sellers who get to March having done the preparation work, pricing discussions, building reports, styling, and photography, consistently outperform those who list quickly without that groundwork.

If you are thinking about selling in 2030, when are you planning to go to market?
