## Caption

Brisbane's property market has changed significantly over the past three years. Rate movements, population growth, infrastructure investment, and affordability pressures have reshaped what is achievable for different buyer groups.

The conversation I have with a first home buyer who has been saving for three years is very different to the one I have with an upsizer in Hawthorne who is selling to buy in Camp Hill. Both are making important decisions. Both are watching the same market and drawing different conclusions.

Worth asking directly: what is actually driving your property thinking right now?

What is your primary property goal over the next two years?
