## Caption

The decision of which agent to appoint is the single most consequential pre-campaign choice a vendor makes. Most vendors meet with two or three agents before deciding. The criteria they use vary considerably. Some focus on fee. Some look at recent results in the suburb. Some decide based on the conversation and whether the agent's read of the market matches their own experience. Understanding which factor actually correlates with the best outcome is a question worth asking honestly.

## Poll options

- Their knowledge of comparable sales in my suburb
- Their track record of recent results in my area
- Their fee and commission structure
- How confident I feel they can manage the negotiation
