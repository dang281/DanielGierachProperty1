## Caption

The way people use their homes has changed significantly over the past five years. Flexible and hybrid work arrangements have made a dedicated study or home office a practical requirement for a significant portion of buyers, not just a nice-to-have.

In the inner east, I see the demand for a usable fourth room or a separate study reflected in how buyers engage with properties that have a good workspace versus ones that don't.

The question is whether it changes what people will actually pay.

Would you pay more for an inner-east property with a dedicated home office or study?

Daniel Gierach
Ray White Bulimba · Inner East Brisbane
