## Caption

Carina Heights occupies an elevated position overlooking the surrounding inner east with a quiet residential character that draws owner-occupier families who want suburban amenity and the stability of a well-established neighbourhood. It is not a suburb that generates a lot of noise, but it consistently retains its residents, and the families who move there tend to stay.

The proximity to Westfield Carindale is one of the suburb's practical advantages. Grocery, dining, entertainment, and daily services are covered in a single nearby destination without the need to drive into a more congested commercial strip. For families managing school runs, sport schedules, and daily logistics, that convenience is a real factor in suburb selection. ⚠️ VERIFY.

Mount Gravatt State High School serves Carina Heights as part of its catchment, and that school's reputation draws families who have been comparing secondary school options across the south side and inner east. ⚠️ VERIFY catchment boundaries. For buyers with children in the ten to thirteen age range, the school question is often the first filter they apply to their suburb search.

The buyer profile in Carina Heights tends to be families who have found that Camp Hill and Coorparoo are beyond their current budget and recognised that Carina Heights delivers a very similar school catchment outcome and lifestyle quality at a more accessible price point.
