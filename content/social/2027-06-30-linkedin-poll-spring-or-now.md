## Caption

Spring is the most popular time to sell in Australia because that is when everyone sells in Australia. The competition that comes with spring works both ways. More buyers in the market, but more properties to compete with. For inner east Brisbane, the calculus is different from Melbourne or Sydney: the autumn and early winter windows are often less crowded and just as effective. The right time to list depends more on your property's specific strengths than on the calendar. That said, most people have a season they trust. Which one is yours?

## Poll options

- Spring (September to November)
- Autumn (March to May)
- Winter (June to August)
- Whenever the property is ready, regardless of season
