## Caption

Carina Heights delivers the elevated land, the full blocks, and the quietness that buyers from the noisier inner east suburbs move here to find. The suburb sits on ground that rises above the Carina and Carindale retail corridors, giving the residential streets a separation from commercial activity that is a genuine selling point for families and downsizers alike.

The housing stock in Carina Heights skews toward freestanding homes on 600-plus square metre blocks. Development pressure has been relatively limited compared to adjacent suburbs, preserving the low-density character that consistent buyers here value. That scarcity of new stock on existing land supports resale pricing when well-positioned homes come to market.

School catchments in Carina Heights are a meaningful driver. Whites Hill State College covers parts of the suburb for secondary, and the catchment boundary question is one of the first things buyers with secondary school-aged children ask about. Carina Heights State School services primary age children in the suburb.

The practical logistics of Carina Heights are straightforward: the Carindale shopping precinct is minutes away, the Gateway Motorway and Old Cleveland Road provide easy access north and east, and the suburb's relative quietness makes it a practical choice for buyers who want inner east proximity without inner east density.

For homeowners in Carina Heights, November is within the active spring buyer period.
