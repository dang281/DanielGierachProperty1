## Caption

Cannon Hill Anglican College draws families to this suburb from across Brisbane. For parents who want a co-educational Anglican school with strong results and a local address, Cannon Hill is the answer. That school draw is consistent demand, and it doesn't disappear when the broader market softens.

Cannon Hill sits between Morningside and Murarrie, with the river accessible via the Cannon Hill Rowing Club. Cannon Hill Shopping Centre handles the daily needs. The suburb has a genuine character home base, and the streets close to the Anglican College and near the rowing club have historically been the most competitive.

The buyer profile here splits into two distinct groups. Families coming for the school who want space, a garden, and a home they can grow into. And investors attracted by the suburb's proximity to the Gateway Motorway and steady rental demand from the Trade Coast employment corridor nearby. Both buyer types compete for the same stock, which keeps the market reasonably active across cycles.

Unrenovated Queenslanders and post-war homes in good structural condition sell well here, especially in spring when family buyers are most active. Properties needing full renovation trade at a wider discount than you'd see in Camp Hill, reflecting the price difference between the two suburbs.
