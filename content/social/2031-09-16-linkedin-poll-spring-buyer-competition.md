## Caption

Spring 2031 in Brisbane's inner east is producing the buyer competition pattern that typically emerges when a winter of motivated buyers meets a new wave of spring listings. How competitive the market actually feels varies by suburb, price range, and how well the property is presented and priced. A useful reality check is to ask your agent how many groups attended the last comparable sale in your street or suburb and what the campaign produced. What is your read on how competitive open homes feel this spring?

## Poll options

- Very competitive, multiple offers on most properties
- Moderate competition, good properties attracting multiple buyers
- Selective, only well-priced properties are drawing real interest
- Quiet, there is more stock than motivated buyers
