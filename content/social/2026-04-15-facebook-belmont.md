## Caption

Belmont doesn't always make the headlines, but the data tells a compelling story.

Sitting around 12km east of the Brisbane CBD, Belmont has recorded median house price growth of approximately 19.5% over the past 12 months, with a current median of around $1,450,000. Homes here are spending an average of just 16 days on market, which signals genuine buyer competition.

The suburb has a mix of established character homes and newer builds on the hillier terrain, with some properties offering elevated outlooks. Families are drawn by proximity to Westfield Carindale, good local schooling, and easy access to the Gateway Motorway for those commuting south or to the airport precinct.
