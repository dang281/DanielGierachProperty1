## Caption

Coorparoo is one of the anchoring suburbs of Brisbane's inner east, positioned four kilometres from the CBD with a character housing stock, a functional retail and dining precinct along Old Cleveland Road, and a buyer profile that reflects the suburb's consistent appeal to families and professionals who want inner east proximity without the premium of the river-adjacent suburbs.

The school catchment question in Coorparoo involves a number of options that give the suburb a broader family appeal than more catchment-specific suburbs. Coorparoo State School and the proximity to Brisbane Boys' College, Villanova College, and several other well-regarded private schools in the eastern corridor create demand from a buyer base that is balancing state and independent schooling options. That breadth of schooling access is reflected in the suburb's market performance.

The housing stock ranges widely: smaller cottages and units for first buyers and investors on the lower end, through to fully renovated character homes and contemporary infills that attract the upgrader market. The Old Cleveland Road retail and dining strip and the proximity to the Coorparoo Square development have brought services density to the suburb that has changed the daily experience of living there.
