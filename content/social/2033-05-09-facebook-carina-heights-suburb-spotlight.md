## Caption

Carina Heights is a quiet residential suburb about nine kilometres from the CBD that appeals most strongly to families who prioritise a settled, low-traffic environment over café-strip proximity or inner-suburb prestige. The suburb has a genuine neighbourhood character, with well-kept streets and a resident base that skews toward long-term owner-occupiers who are invested in the area.

Access to Westfield Carindale is one of Carina Heights' practical advantages. Major retail, dining and services are close without the suburb being positioned on a busy arterial itself. Families find that the combination of residential quiet and nearby convenience fits a life stage where school runs, weekend sport and household errands define the weekly rhythm more than restaurant access or nightlife.

The housing stock in Carina Heights leans toward solid brick homes built between the 1980s and early 2000s. These properties typically offer good internal space, established gardens and practical layouts that suit families. Renovation potential varies; some have already been updated while others present opportunity for buyers willing to invest in modernising the interior. Current medians in Carina Heights reflect a market that rewards presentation and condition, with the well-maintained property attracting the most competitive interest.

Bus services provide connection to the CBD and to neighbouring centres, and road access to major arterials is straightforward.
