## Caption

Seven Hills sits between Coorparoo and Camp Hill in a position that gives it the inner east quality of life while sitting at a price point that many buyers find more accessible than its immediate neighbours. For buyers who know the area well, that is an attractive combination.

The elevated position means many streets in Seven Hills carry city views, which is a feature that buyers who have been looking across the inner east notice when they compare their options. Quiet, leafy, residential streets with tree canopy and established gardens are the consistent character of the suburb, and that atmosphere is something buyers are looking for when they have decided they want the inner east but want to step back from the pace of higher-density areas closer to the river.

The proximity to both Camp Hill Village and the Old Cleveland Road retail strip means Seven Hills residents are a short drive from two of the inner east's best suburban commercial precincts.

Mount Gravatt State High School serves Seven Hills as part of its catchment. ⚠️ VERIFY. That places the suburb within reach of a school that draws families from several surrounding suburbs, and it is a relevant factor for buyers with secondary-school-age children or approaching that stage.

The buyer profile in Seven Hills tends to be families who have done the inner east research, want the lifestyle and school access, and are looking for a suburb that delivers it at a price between Camp Hill and the outer suburbs.
