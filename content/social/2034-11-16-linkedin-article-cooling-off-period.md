## Caption

Queensland's cooling-off period is five business days. It is not free, and it does not apply at auction.

The buyer who exercises their cooling-off right pays 0.25 per cent of the purchase price to the vendor. On a $1 million property, that is $2,500. It is a meaningful penalty but not a deterrent for a buyer who has made the wrong decision.

The cooling-off period runs from the time the buyer receives a copy of the signed contract. Ensuring the buyer receives the contract promptly matters for the vendor's timeline.

Properties sold at auction have no cooling-off period. Buyers who purchase under the hammer are immediately unconditional and legally bound to complete.
