## Caption

Rentvesting means renting where you want to live and buying an investment property where you can afford.

It has grown in popularity as inner-city prices have moved beyond reach for many owner-occupier buyers. You stay in the suburb you want. You build an investment asset in a suburb where the numbers work. You access negative gearing and potential capital growth.

The catch is psychological. A lot of people find it genuinely hard not to own the roof over their head, regardless of what the numbers say.

What is your take on rentvesting?
