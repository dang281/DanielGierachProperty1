## Caption

Cannon Hill's train station on the Cleveland line changes the commute calculation for buyers who need to get to the CBD regularly without driving. Combine that with Cannon Hill Plaza's daily retail, Gateway Motorway access for the airport and motorway corridors, and you have a suburb that covers multiple household requirements from a compact geographic footprint.

The buyers who choose Cannon Hill are making a practical calculation rather than a lifestyle one, and that is a stable foundation for a property market. Buyers motivated by specific infrastructure rather than suburb prestige tend to be decisive once they have identified what they need.

Post-war brick homes on generous blocks anchor the established residential streets. More recent townhouse development serves the buyer who wants the access story without the maintenance commitment of a larger lot. Cannon Hill Anglicans holds a meaningful community presence in the suburb, and that network draws a consistent owner-occupier segment. Cannon Hill State School serves the primary catchment for families.

Well-priced homes in Cannon Hill have moved with tight days on market across recent selling seasons, which reflects the depth of the buyer pool that the suburb's infrastructure combination consistently generates.

For owners in Cannon Hill who have been watching the market, the suburb's practical strengths are your selling story. That is a strong position to be in.
