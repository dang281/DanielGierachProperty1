## Caption

Annerley sits 5km south of the Brisbane CBD, yet it still surprises buyers who expect to pay premium inner-city prices.

The suburb is anchored by Queenslander character homes on generous blocks, many backing onto quiet side streets well away from Ipswich Road. It's close to the PA Hospital precinct, one of Queensland's largest employers, which keeps consistent rental demand and a steady stream of buyers who want to walk or cycle to work.

The median house price in Annerley is currently around $1,350,000, with annual growth of approximately 8% over the past 12 months. For buyers priced out of Yeronga or Fairfield, Annerley is often where the conversation starts.
