## Caption

Greenslopes is a suburb that often flies under the radar compared to its inner-south neighbours, but buyers who do their homework tend to find value here.

Sitting 5km from the Brisbane CBD, Greenslopes offers the Queenslander character homes and larger blocks that buyers associate with Coorparoo and Holland Park, at a median house price currently around $1,350,000 to $1,400,000. The suburb is close to the Greenslopes Private Hospital precinct, which drives consistent employment-linked demand, and it has solid bus connections to the CBD along Ipswich Road and Logan Road corridors.

For homeowners, the suburb has a mix of long-held properties and newer buyers who have renovated significantly. The character stock on the eastern side of the suburb, close to the ridge line, tends to attract the stronger prices.
