## Caption

Holland Park West is the smaller, quieter sibling of Holland Park, and for buyers who know both suburbs, that distinction often matters.

The suburb sits west of Logan Road in the same general precinct as Holland Park, offering similar block sizes and character home stock but with slightly less through-traffic. It shares the benefits of Holland Park's location, including proximity to Holland Park State High School catchment, good freeway access via the South-East Freeway, and bus connections on Cavendish Road, without being a destination suburb in its own right. That lower profile tends to keep competition a fraction more manageable.
