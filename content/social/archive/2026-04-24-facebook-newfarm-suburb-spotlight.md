## Caption

Two kilometres from the CBD, riverfront, New Farm Park, the Brisbane Powerhouse, and a dining and cafe scene that buyers actively seek out. New Farm does not need to sell itself. Buyers know it.

What that means in practice is that when a well-presented home comes to market here, it attracts buyers from a wide catchment, many of whom have been waiting for the right property to come up. The competition is real, and the results reflect it.

If you own in New Farm and you have been thinking about what the current market looks like for your home, I am happy to sit down and have that conversation.
