## Caption

Camp Hill has quietly become one of the more consistent performers in Brisbane's south-east inner ring.

Six to seven kilometres from the CBD, a mix of original Queenslanders and renovated family homes, leafy streets, and a local community feel that attracts the same buyer type over and over: families who have done their research and decided this is the spot.

Over the past decade, Camp Hill house values have more than doubled. That is not a fluke. It reflects what happens when a suburb sits in the right location, offers the right lifestyle, and does not oversupply.

If you own in Camp Hill and you have been thinking about what your home is worth in this market, I am happy to have that conversation.
