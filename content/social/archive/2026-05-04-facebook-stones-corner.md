## Caption

Stones Corner has a dual identity that buyers are increasingly recognising: a walkable village centre with genuine retail and cafe character, surrounded by residential streets that offer real proximity to the CBD.

Sitting roughly 5km south of the Brisbane CBD at the intersection of Old Cleveland Road and Logan Road, Stones Corner provides direct bus access to the city and easy freeway entry via the nearby South-East Freeway. The commercial strip along Old Cleveland Road has developed significantly over recent years, and the neighbourhood feel that results is a genuine point of difference for buyers who want urban access without full inner-city density.

The suburb sits between Coorparoo and Greenslopes in both geography and price. Character homes on the residential streets off the main roads attract buyers who are comparing across the inner-south corridor.
