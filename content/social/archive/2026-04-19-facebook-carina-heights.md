## Caption

Carina Heights often gets overlooked in favour of its more prominent neighbour, but buyers who look closely tend to come back.

The typical house value in Carina Heights is around $1,627,000, sitting above the broader Carina median and reflecting the premium that elevated, quieter streets command in Brisbane's eastern suburbs. The suburb is smaller and less densely developed than Carina, which means less through-traffic and a more settled streetscape. Many homes here have been held by the same families for a long time, which keeps supply tight.

For buyers, Carina Heights offers the combination of a short drive to Westfield Carindale, proximity to Camp Hill State School catchment, and the kind of streets where you actually know your neighbours. That lifestyle factor counts.

For homeowners who have held property here through recent years of growth, your equity position has strengthened materially. If you've been considering a move but haven't tested what the market would pay right now, it's worth finding out.
