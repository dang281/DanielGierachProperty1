## Caption

Hamilton is one of Brisbane's most consistently regarded prestige addresses, sitting alongside Ascot at the top end of the city's residential market.

The typical house value in Hamilton is currently around $2,600,000, reflecting a suburb where heritage homes, river proximity, and genuine lifestyle amenity all converge. Hamilton Hill provides elevated positions with CBD and river views, and the Portside Wharf precinct at the base of the suburb has transformed what living here means, restaurant and entertainment access within walking distance of residential streets is rare in Brisbane.

Hamilton Northshore and the broader Portside area continue to attract significant investment, which maintains buyer interest and keeps the broader suburb profile strong. For buyers at this price point, the fundamentals that matter are consistent: quality of housing stock, school catchments, and long-term capital preservation.

For homeowners in Hamilton, the market for well-presented prestige property remains competitive when stock is scarce. If you're considering your position, a frank conversation about current values is where to start.
