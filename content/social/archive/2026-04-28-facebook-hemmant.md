## Caption

Hemmant sits east of Cannon Hill along the Brisbane River, and it's the kind of suburb where buyers who do their research are finding ground that hasn't moved as dramatically as the inner-east suburbs closer to the CBD.

The typical house price in Hemmant is around $1,260,000, with a rental yield of approximately 2.6%. The suburb has a mix of older homes on generous blocks and properties with river access, which attract a specific buyer profile. Access to the Gateway Motorway makes Hemmant practical for those who commute to the airport, Port of Brisbane, or the south-east corridor.

Hemmant is not trying to be Bulimba or Cannon Hill, and that's part of its appeal for certain buyers. The space-to-price ratio is genuinely different, and for families or buyers who want the riverfront setting without the Bulimba premium, it remains a considered choice.
