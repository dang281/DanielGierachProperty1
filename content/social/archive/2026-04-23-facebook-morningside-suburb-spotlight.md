## Caption

Morningside has been one of the quiet achievers of Brisbane's inner east.

Flat blocks, character homes, easy access to the CBD and the Hawthorne dining strip, and a price point that still attracts buyers who have been priced out of Bulimba and Hawthorne. Over the past year, houses have been selling in a median of 22 days. That is not a slow market.

The Cannon Hill Shopping Centre redevelopment nearby is also adding to the area's long-term appeal, bringing new retail and dining that lifts the entire precinct.

If you own in Morningside and have been thinking about selling, it is worth knowing what your home is worth in the current market before you make a decision.
