## Caption

Carindale has one of the more practical amenity profiles of any suburb in Brisbane's inner east. Westfield Carindale sits within the suburb boundary, which means residents have major retail, dining, and services within a short drive or walk. For families in particular, that kind of everyday convenience is factored into the buying decision just as much as school catchments or block size.

And on those fronts, Carindale also delivers. Carindale State School and Citipointe Christian College give the suburb a broad school appeal, and the housing stock skews toward larger family homes on lots that are noticeably more generous than what you find closer to the CBD. That combination of space, amenity, and school access is what keeps upgrade buyer activity consistent here.

The buyer profile in Carindale is typically a family stepping up from a smaller inner east home or relocating from outside the area with a clear brief: space, school zone, and lifestyle access. They are not impulsive buyers. They have usually been looking for several months, they know the comparables, and when the right home appears they tend to move decisively.

For owners who have been in Carindale for a while, current values may be meaningfully higher than the last time you checked. A fresh appraisal is worth the conversation.
