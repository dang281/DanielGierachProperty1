## Caption

One of the most common questions I get from sellers preparing for spring is how much work to do before going to market. The honest answer depends on the property, the suburb, and what comparable sales look like right now. Over-capitalising is a real risk. So is underselling a property that needed two weeks of work and proper styling. Fresh paint and good presentation can shift buyer perception significantly without a big spend. Full kitchen and bathroom renovations rarely return dollar for dollar in a short-selling timeframe. If you were selling your home this spring, which approach would you be most likely to take?

## Poll options
