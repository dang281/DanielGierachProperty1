## Caption

Auctions are a significant part of the inner east property landscape. In suburbs like Hawthorne, Balmoral, and Norman Park, a large proportion of sales in the spring season go to auction. They create transparency on the day but require a level of preparation that private treaty does not: finance unconditional, building and pest inspection complete, solicitor review done, all before you bid a dollar.

For buyers who have never bought at auction, the process can feel opaque. For buyers who have done it once, it tends to be less intimidating the second time.

Have you ever purchased a property at auction?
