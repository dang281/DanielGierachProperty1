The suburb draws the first-home buyer who has stretched to make the numbers work, the professional couple who has been watching the inner east since 2030 and is ready to commit, the investor who understands that Morningside's train station access and proximity to the Wynnum Road café strip create a rental story that holds across market conditions, and the upgrading family who has looked at Hawthorne and Bulimba and found themselves one suburb short of what their budget will reach. All four of those buyers are at Morningside open homes in March, and they are competing for a limited pool of character properties.
