## Caption

Cannon Hill is one of Brisbane's inner-east suburbs that genuinely earns its reputation with families, and the market reflects that demand.

Sitting 9km east of the CBD, Cannon Hill has a median house price of around $1,520,000, with values up approximately 10% over the past 12 months. The suburb has built a loyal buyer base around a few key fundamentals: the catchment for Cannon Hill State School, access to the Eastern Busway at Cannon Hill station, and character homes on blocks that still offer room to extend or add a pool.

The suburb is well connected without feeling like a through-road. Many of the better streets sit quietly between Wynnum Road and Creek Road, close to the Cannon Hill Kmart precinct for daily convenience. Buyers who compare Cannon Hill to Morningside or Norman Park often find they can stretch their budget further for a comparable lifestyle.
