## Caption

Spring brings more properties to market and more buyers through doors. Most buyers form their view in the first few minutes of an inspection, before they have read the contract or looked at the building report.

The reasons buyers walk away from a property are rarely just about price. They are often about something they spotted or felt in the first walkthrough that made them less confident about the decision.

What is the most common reason you have walked away from a property at an inspection?

## Poll options

- Poor street appeal or presentation on arrival
- Layout or room size that did not suit
- Something in the building report
- Price expectation too far from reality
