## Caption

Brisbane has had a strong 2026. Inner east suburbs have seen consistent buyer demand, limited stock, and prices that have held well relative to the rest of the country.

The question heading into 2027 is whether that trajectory continues, moderates, or faces a correction as affordability limits tighten further and interest rates stay where they are.

The forecasters are not fully aligned. Some see continued growth driven by Olympic infrastructure momentum and interstate migration. Others point to affordability ceilings and softening demand at higher price points.

Where do you think Brisbane residential property is heading in 2027?
