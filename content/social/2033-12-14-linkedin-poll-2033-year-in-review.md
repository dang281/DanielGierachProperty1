## Caption

Every year has a story that shapes how buyers and sellers remember the market. The story is rarely one thing. It is usually a combination of macro factors and local dynamics that played out differently across different price points and suburbs throughout the year. The autumn window was strong. School catchment suburbs continued to hold a premium that the broader market did not fully explain. Stock levels and interest rate direction both had their moments. But if you had to distil 2033 in Brisbane's inner east down to a single theme, what would it be?
