## Caption

Inherited property is one of the more complicated decisions people face. The emotional weight is real, the practical questions are immediate, and the financial stakes are significant. It is also a situation that more Australians are navigating as inter-generational wealth transfer accelerates.

The decision of what to do with an inherited property rarely fits into a clean financial model. It depends on the property, the family structure, the tax position, and how much emotional attachment people have to what the property means.

I am curious how people in my network think about it.

If you inherited a property in Brisbane, what would you do?

Daniel Gierach
Ray White Bulimba · Inner East Brisbane

## Poll options

- Sell and take the proceeds
- Hold and rent it out
