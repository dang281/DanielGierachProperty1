## Caption

Three numbers run in parallel on every campaign. Sellers who confuse them rarely get the result they wanted.

Market value is what a willing buyer and a willing seller land on today, on comparable evidence from the last 90 days. Not what the neighbour sold for in 2022. Not what the vendor paid. Not what the vendor needs.

List price (the price guide in Queensland) is the number that attracts the right buyers to the campaign. Honest guides bring the buyers who can actually transact. Inflated guides bring browsers, and then silence.

Reserve is set on auction morning, not disclosed to bidders, and is the minimum the vendor will accept under the hammer. Too high, the property passes in. At market, the room finds the number.

In a well-run campaign, all three numbers are close. When they are far apart, the campaign is usually telling the vendor something the vendor has not been ready to hear.
