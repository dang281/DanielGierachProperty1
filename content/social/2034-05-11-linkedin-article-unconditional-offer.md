## Caption

An unconditional offer in Queensland means the buyer is committing to the purchase with no finance condition and no building and pest condition. If the vendor accepts, both parties are immediately bound. There is no 2 to 3 week window where the buyer can walk away.

That certainty has real value to a vendor. It eliminates the risk that the buyer's bank valuation comes in short, the building report finds something that reopens the negotiation, or the buyer loses confidence and finds a reason to exit.

Because of that certainty, vendors will often accept a lower unconditional offer over a higher conditional one. The margin depends on the property and the vendor's circumstances, but a $20,000 to $30,000 discount on a $1.2 million property is not unusual when the conditional buyer's alternative is a three-week window of uncertainty.

For buyers who want to go unconditional, the preparation is straightforward. Pre-approval with a lender that has assessed the property type you are targeting, combined with an independent building and pest inspection done before making the offer, converts you from a conditional buyer to an unconditional one. The cost of that preparation is a few hundred dollars. The negotiating advantage it creates is worth substantially more.
