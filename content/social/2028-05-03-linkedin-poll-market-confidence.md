## Caption

Mid-year is a good time to take a temperature check on where people actually sit with Brisbane property. Interest rates are still part of the conversation. Population growth into South East Queensland continues to outpace new housing supply. Olympic 2032 infrastructure investment is reshaping how buyers think about certain inner east pockets. At the same time, affordability pressures are real and serviceability is not what it was a few years ago. These forces are pulling in different directions, and sentiment among buyers, investors, and owners tends to be more varied than headline commentary suggests. How confident are you in Brisbane property over the next 12 months?
