## Caption

Sale method is one of the most consequential decisions a seller makes, and it is often made quickly based on familiarity rather than strategy. Auction is well understood in Brisbane and performs reliably in competitive conditions. Private treaty gives sellers more control over timing and conditions. Expression of interest sits between the two and suits properties where the buyer pool is harder to predict.

The right method depends on the property, the price point, the buyer profile, and market conditions at the time. What works for a renovated Queenslander in Bulimba may not be the right structure for a character home in Camp Hill with a complex buyer pool.

If selling your inner east home this autumn, which sale method would you choose?

## Poll options

- Auction (transparent, competitive, works in a strong market)
