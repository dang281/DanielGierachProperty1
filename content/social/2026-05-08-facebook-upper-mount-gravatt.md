## Caption

Upper Mount Gravatt is a suburb that offers Brisbane families a practical combination of highway access, retail convenience, and established residential character at a median house price currently around $1,230,000.

The suburb sits directly on the South-East Freeway corridor, 12km from the Brisbane CBD, and is home to Westfield Garden City, one of Queensland's largest shopping centres. Griffith University's Nathan campus is close by, which keeps a consistent educational and employment anchor in the area. MacGregor State School and Marshall Road State School serve the local family market.

The price point here is lower than Mount Gravatt or Coorparoo for comparable land sizes, which is why buyers who need more space tend to look at Upper Mount Gravatt when they've been priced out of suburbs closer to the city.
