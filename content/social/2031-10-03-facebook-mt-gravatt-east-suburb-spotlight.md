## Caption

Greenslopes Private Hospital sits just to the north, and that proximity shapes who buys in Mt Gravatt East more than most people realise. Nurses, specialists, allied health workers, and hospital administrators make up a meaningful part of the buyer pool here, and they value the short commute above almost everything else.

Mt Gravatt East sits in that useful middle ground between Holland Park and Carindale. Mt Gravatt State School is the local primary draw. The suburb has a garden character, good block sizes, and a mix of families and empty nesters who've lived here for decades alongside younger buyers who priced into the suburb ahead of neighbouring Holland Park.

What sells fastest here is the renovated character home with a clean floorplan. Garden-oriented buyers, particularly those downsizing from larger properties further out, are drawn to blocks on the quieter streets off Newnham Road and Mt Gravatt-Capalaba Road. Properties sitting on main road exposure take longer to move and typically require price adjustment to find a buyer.
