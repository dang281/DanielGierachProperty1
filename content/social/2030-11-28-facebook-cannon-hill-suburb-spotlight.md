## Caption

Cannon Hill sits at the inner east's eastern edge, with the Gateway Motorway access that makes it a more practical location than its postcode might initially suggest for buyers who spend time travelling to the airport, port Logan corridor, or Gold Coast.

The suburb's train station on the Cannon Hill line gives residents direct rail access to the CBD, which positions Cannon Hill better on the transport matrix than many Brisbane suburbs at equivalent or higher price points. Buyers who have run the commute calculation find Cannon Hill competitive.

The Cannon Hill Anglican College campus adds an amenity layer that shapes the family buyer profile. As a private school, it draws families from across the inner east who choose to live near the school for practical morning logistics. That family buyer demand keeps the suburb's detached housing stock in consistent demand.

The elevated streets on the higher ground east of the railway line, particularly in the section toward Murarrie, deliver the outlooks and the natural light that the flat commercial strip along Creek Road and Wynnum Road does not. Buyers who know the suburb understand the distinction quickly.

Late November is still within the active buyer period for Cannon Hill, and properties on the elevated streets are attracting consistent open home attendance.
