## Caption

Auctions in Queensland are transparent by design. Every bid is declared, vendor bids are announced, and the result is clear on the day. But the experience of bidding at auction, or sitting in the room watching it happen, tends to be something people have strong feelings about.

Some buyers love the clarity. Others find the pressure uncomfortable. Sellers often feel differently once they have been through the process and seen what competitive bidding can do to the final result.

Have you ever bought at auction?
