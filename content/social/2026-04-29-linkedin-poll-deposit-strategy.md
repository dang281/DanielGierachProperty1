## Caption

Entry-level homes in Brisbane are now averaging above $800K. With the QLD first home owner grant reducing from $30K to $15K after June 2026, the question of how to enter the market is more complicated than it has been.

Saving a 20% deposit on an $800K property means $160K before you count stamp duty, legal fees, and moving costs. That is a long runway when prices are moving.

What do you think is the smartest strategy for a first home buyer right now?

Daniel Gierach
Ray White Bulimba · Inner East Brisbane

## Poll options

- Wait (prices will fall)
- Buy now with what you can afford
- Build new (access the QLD grant)
- Look in the regions or outer suburbs
