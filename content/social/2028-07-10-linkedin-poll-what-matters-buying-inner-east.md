## Caption

Brisbane's inner east covers a wide range of suburbs, prices, and property types. But when buyers are choosing between two addresses that tick most of the same boxes, a few factors tend to break the tie.

I see patterns in what buyers are prioritising at the moment. School catchment has been the dominant conversation for the past few years. Flood overlay has gone from a minor footnote to a genuine filter. Character homes continue to attract a premium that new builds struggle to justify to the same buyer.

Curious where my network sits on this. When you are buying in Brisbane's inner east, what is the single biggest factor?

## Poll options

- School catchment
- Land size and character
- Flood overlay / risk profile
- Distance to CBD or transport
