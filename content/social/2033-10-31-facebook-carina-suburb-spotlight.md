## Caption

Carina is an established residential suburb that holds onto its residents at an above-average rate. Low turnover and strong owner-occupier retention are consistent features here, which tells you something useful about what the suburb delivers to the people who choose it.

The proximity to Westfield Carindale gives residents daily convenience at a scale that is hard to replicate. Grocery, retail, health services, and dining all accessible without leaving the immediate area. For families and owner-occupiers who factor practical daily convenience into their suburb decision, Carindale's presence on the doorstep is a real advantage.

Block sizes in Carina are larger than what is available at comparable prices in suburbs closer to the city. That is a straightforward land-value proposition. Buyers who want useable outdoor space, the potential to extend, or simply more room on a residential block find that Carina delivers what the inner east suburbs at higher price points offer, at more accessible entry values.

The Mount Gravatt State High School catchment covers much of Carina, which adds family appeal and supports sustained demand from buyers with secondary-school-aged children. For families comparing Carina to Bulimba or Hawthorne, the school access combined with the block size and the Westfield proximity makes Carina a straightforward value argument rather than a compromise.

Within 10 kilometres of the Brisbane CBD, the suburb suits residents who want suburban space without paying inner-city prices or accepting a long commute.
