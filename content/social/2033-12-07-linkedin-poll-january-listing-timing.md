## Caption

Working backwards from a late-February settlement tells a story that most vendors find surprising. You need roughly 30 to 45 days for settlement after the contract is signed. Add four to five weeks for the campaign to run. That puts the property needing to be on the market by early to mid-January at the latest. Before that, preparation: styling, photography, disclosure statement, agent appointed. That takes four to eight weeks. The maths consistently puts the start of preparation in November or early December. How many vendors actually know the answer when they first start thinking about selling?

## Poll options

- Early to mid-December (to allow full preparation time)
- Late December to early January (an accelerated but workable timeline)
- Mid-January at the latest (tight on preparation time)
- Anytime in January is fine for a February settlement (incorrect)
