## Caption

Mt Gravatt East sits on the eastern slope of the Mount Gravatt escarpment, offering a combination of elevated blocks with outlook potential, proximity to the Logan Road retail and dining corridor, and access to the Mount Gravatt and Griffith University employment and education precinct that makes it consistently attractive to a practical and research-led buyer pool.

The suburb is positioned between the Westfield Garden City retail anchor to the south and the Coorparoo to Carina inner east corridor to the north, which gives residents access to two distinct retail and services networks without committing to either as the suburb's primary identity. That in-between positioning reflects Mt Gravatt East's character more broadly: a suburb that delivers inner east adjacency without being fully inside the price band of the closer-in suburbs.

The housing stock is predominantly postwar brick and timber construction on blocks that are generous by inner east standards, with a growing number of renovations and contemporary infills from buyers who have identified the suburb's value proposition and invested accordingly. Elevated blocks on the escarpment's eastern face command premiums for the outlook they provide, a consistent feature of the suburb's comparable sales evidence.
