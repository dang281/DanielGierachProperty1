## Caption

The Brisbane 2032 Olympics start in July. That is four months away, and the question I am hearing most often from inner east homeowners right now is whether it is better to sell before the Games begin, during the lead-up, or after.

The honest answer is that there is not a clear historical consensus on what Olympics do to property values in host cities. Sydney, Athens, London, and Paris all produced different results, and the local context matters as much as the global event.

What I do know is that autumn 2032 is already showing a buyer pool with unusual urgency. Whether that is specifically Olympic-driven or just the normal March dynamic amplified by the year, the conditions for a well-run campaign are genuinely strong right now.

If you had the option to sell your inner east home before or after the Brisbane 2032 Olympics, what would you do?
