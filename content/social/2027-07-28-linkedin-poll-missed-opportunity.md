## Caption

Almost everyone who has been an active buyer in Brisbane's property market has at least one property they chose not to make an offer on and later wished they had. Sometimes the price felt too high at the time. Sometimes the timing was not right. Sometimes a building report or a gut feeling gave you pause. Looking back, the ones that got away have a way of looking different than they did on the day. Has this happened to you?
