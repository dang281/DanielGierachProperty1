## Caption

The renovation wave in Carina has been running for several years now and it isn't finished. Original homes that were considered tired or unremarkable have been coming back to market with full kitchen and bathroom renovations, updated exteriors, and landscaped gardens, and the results in price terms are significant.

That transformation is reshaping the suburb's identity. Carina still offers an accessible price point relative to Camp Hill to the north, but the discount has been narrowing as the underlying quality of the housing stock lifts. Buyers who bought five or six years ago with a renovation plan have largely done well.

Carina State School remains the primary catchment draw for family buyers. The freeway access makes the suburb work for buyers who commute south toward the university precinct or out toward the industrial employment areas. Carina Leagues Club continues to function as the social hub it has always been.

The investor profile in Carina remains active. Rental yields here are reasonable relative to the purchase price, and the suburb's improving trajectory gives investors a capital growth case alongside the income return.

In November, Carina benefits from the spring seller window. Properties that have been held back for the right moment tend to come to market, and that's when the best buyer competition appears.

Median prices in Carina have been rising consistently. ⚠️ VERIFY current median and days on market before posting.

If you're considering listing your Carina property, November is one of the better months to do it.
