## Caption

The buyers arriving in Mt Gravatt East have usually come from somewhere else in the inner east. They have been in a townhouse in Coorparoo or a unit in Greenslopes, and they have decided that what they actually want is land.

Mt Gravatt East gives them that. Blocks here tend to run larger than the suburb's neighbours to the north, and the price point sits below what comparable space costs in Camp Hill or Norman Park. The area around Creek Road and Mt Gravatt-Capalaba Road carries most of the residential activity, with good access to daily retail and services along both corridors.

For families, the school story matters. Mt Gravatt East State School serves the primary catchment, and the suburb's southern sections bring Runcorn High within range for secondary buyers.

Westfield Garden City is a short drive south. The CBD is accessible without sitting on a motorway. This is a suburb that works practically for most household configurations, which is why the buyers who arrive here tend not to leave quickly.
