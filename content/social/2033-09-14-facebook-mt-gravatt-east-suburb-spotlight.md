## Caption

Mt Gravatt East sits at a price point that a growing number of inner east buyers are reaching as comparable suburbs have moved. The suburb offers block sizes that are increasingly rare within this distance of the CBD, a school catchment that satisfies the requirements of family buyers researching state school options, and a price relative to Holland Park and Camp Hill that buyers with a clear budget ceiling identify as workable. That combination is bringing a buyer profile to Mt Gravatt East that is noticeably better resourced than it was five years ago.

The Griffith University Mt Gravatt campus creates a consistent secondary rental demand that investment buyers in Mt Gravatt East factor into their purchase calculus. For vendors selling properties that have operated as investment holdings, the ability to demonstrate rental yield history to buyer-investors is part of the campaign strategy that distinguishes Mt Gravatt East from the purely owner-occupier suburbs to the west.

The Mt Gravatt Showgrounds and the surrounding parkland give the suburb a recreational amenity base that family buyers use to calibrate daily quality of life alongside the commute and school access questions. Weekend activity at the Showgrounds is part of what makes Mt Gravatt East feel like a suburb with its own community character rather than simply an address on the motorway corridor.

Spring is when Mt Gravatt East vendor campaigns attract the most active buyer pool of the year.
