## Caption

Murarrie is a suburb in transition that has attracted attention from buyers who understand what infrastructure investment does to a surrounding residential market over time. The suburb sits between Cannon Hill and Tingalpa on the eastern edge of the inner east, with direct access to the Gateway Motorway that makes it one of the better-positioned suburbs for commuters heading to the airport or the southern growth corridor.

The ongoing industrial and commercial precinct surrounding Murarrie has historically kept residential buyer interest measured relative to its inner east neighbours. That is now changing. As the residential pockets within Murarrie have developed and the suburb's access and proximity to the Cannon Hill and Morningside amenity strip have become better understood, the buyer pool has shifted.

The buyers pursuing Murarrie today are typically value-focused and practical. They are looking for land content and house size at a price point they cannot achieve in Cannon Hill or Morningside, with access to the same infrastructure network. The suburb's position on the Gateway makes it particularly attractive to buyers with a frequent airport or interstate commute requirement.
