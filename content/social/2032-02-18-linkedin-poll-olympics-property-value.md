## Caption

With the Brisbane Games five months away, the Olympic effect on inner east property values is no longer theoretical. Infrastructure is in place, the athlete village precinct has changed the Northshore corridor, and buyer interest in suburbs near competition venues has been measurable for several years.

The question for buyers and sellers right now is whether that effect is already priced in, or whether July still represents a demand catalyst. People buying, selling, and watching this market have different views on this, and all of them are worth hearing.

Does the Brisbane 2032 Olympics change how much you would pay for a property in the inner east?
