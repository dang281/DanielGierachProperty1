## Caption

Brisbane's inner east has two strong selling windows each year: autumn (February to May) and spring (September to November). Both have merit, but they produce different buyer profiles and different conditions for sellers.

Autumn tends to attract more owner-occupier buyers making deliberate moves. Spring brings higher volume but also more competition from other listings. The right window depends on your property, your situation, and sometimes your school calendar.

If you are planning to sell your home this year, which window are you leaning toward?
