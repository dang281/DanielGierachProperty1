## Caption

Most sellers face a genuine triage problem. There is time, money, and energy to spend, but rarely enough of all three to tackle everything at once. The right call depends heavily on the property itself and the buyer profile most likely to walk through the door. A tired kitchen in a family home in Bulimba is a different conversation to a unit in Hawthorne where street appeal barely applies. There is no universal answer, but knowing where buyers in your price range actually focus their attention changes the calculation considerably. If you were selling your home this autumn, what would you tackle first?
