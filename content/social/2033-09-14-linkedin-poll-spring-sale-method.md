## Caption

Sale method in Brisbane's inner east is not one-size-fits-all. Auction works well for properties where the buyer pool is broad and competitive bidding is likely to push price above what a private treaty negotiation would achieve. Private treaty makes more sense where the buyer pool is narrower, conditions are important to the likely buyer, or the vendor has a strong sense of their floor. Expression of interest sits between the two. Understanding which method suits a specific property requires knowing the comparable sales, the likely buyer profile, and what the vendor's priorities are. If you were listing an inner east property this spring, which method would you prefer?

## Poll options

- Auction (transparent, competitive, clear timeline)
