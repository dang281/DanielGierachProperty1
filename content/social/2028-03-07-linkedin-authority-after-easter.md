## Caption

Every year around this time, I start hearing the same sentence from sellers who have been thinking about listing since January.

"We'll wait until after Easter."

Easter is in April. A seller who waits until after Easter is launching their campaign in late April or early May, which is the tail end of the autumn window. They are not missing the market entirely. But they are giving up four to six weeks of the strongest buyer activity of the year.

The buyers who are active right now in the inner east are not on holiday. They are not waiting until Easter either. They are watching new listings, making offers, and moving on when the right property appears.

The properties that sell in March in this part of Brisbane do not sit around waiting to be discovered. The campaigns that launch well, at the right price, with the right presentation, produce outcomes before the end of March because the buyer pool that exists right now is ready.

If you have been waiting for after Easter, the question worth asking is: what specifically changes after Easter? The answer, in most cases, is that more listings come on and the buyer pool gets spread across more supply.
