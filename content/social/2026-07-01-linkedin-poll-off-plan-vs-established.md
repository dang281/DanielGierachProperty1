## Caption

Off the plan offers a lower deposit, new build warranties, and potential stamp duty savings. The risk: valuations can come in short at settlement, and building quality is hard to assess until the project is complete.

Established gives you what you see before you sign. Older homes have character, known defects, and a track record. The price is the price.

If everything else was equal, which would you choose?
