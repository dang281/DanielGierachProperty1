## Caption

Camp Hill has a median house price of around $1.76 million, with annual growth of approximately 10 per cent and a 10-year capital growth rate of 122 per cent, according to current suburb data.

That long-run number is worth pausing on. It is not driven by speculation. It reflects the fundamentals of a well-located middle-ring suburb with limited land release, consistent family demand, and good access to the CBD. Camp Hill sits 6 to 7km from the city, close enough for a practical commute but with the space and amenity that buyers leave the inner suburbs to find.

The current market adds nuance to that story. House values grew 10.1 per cent over the past year, but the pace is moderating as the rate environment bites into borrowing capacity. The buyers are still there, and they are still motivated. But they are moving through the process more carefully.

For homeowners in Camp Hill, the opportunity is in positioning. A home that is well-presented and priced accurately based on recent comparable sales will find competition. One that is chasing an aspirational number risks sitting while the motivated buyers move on.

Happy to walk through what that looks like specifically for your Camp Hill property.
