## Caption

Autumn campaign preparation is underway for many inner east vendors right now. With six to eight weeks of lead time needed before a first open home, late January is exactly when these decisions should be made.

Every vendor has a finite amount of time, money and energy to put into getting a property ready. That means prioritising. What gets attention first shapes what buyers see when they walk in.

In my experience, different vendors default to different priorities, and the results vary significantly depending on what the property actually needs most.

If selling your inner east home this autumn, what would be your number one priority in preparing the property?
