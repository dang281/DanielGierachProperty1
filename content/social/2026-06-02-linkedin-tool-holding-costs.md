## Caption

Most sellers in Brisbane's inner east plan for the agent commission and the marketing campaign. Fewer plan for what the property costs them while it sits on the market.

Interest on the mortgage. Rates and water. Insurance. Utilities if the home is still occupied. Cleaning and lawn care if it has been vacated for the campaign.

For a home worth around $1.5M on a typical mortgage, the monthly run-rate can sit anywhere from $4,000 to $8,000 depending on the loan balance and how the property is presented during the campaign. A 90-day campaign is not unusual in this market. The numbers add up.

This is not a reason to rush the campaign. It is a reason to plan it properly: realistic pricing from day one, presentation decisions that earn their cost, and a clear pricing review cadence so you are never drifting.

I built a calculator that runs the numbers for you. Link in the comments.
