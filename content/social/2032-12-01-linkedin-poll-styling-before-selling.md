## Caption

Professional styling before sale is now a standard recommendation from most inner east agents. Costs typically range from $3,000 to $8,000 or more depending on home size and how much furniture is brought in. The core argument for it is that staging is primarily a photography investment: better photos generate more inquiry, more inquiry creates competition, and competition is what drives price. Not every seller is convinced, and the case is not identical for every property. What is your honest view?
