## Caption

Bulimba's spring market is one of the more instructive periods to observe buyer behaviour in the inner east. The buyers who pursue Bulimba in September and October are not first-time browsers. They are buyers who have been tracking the suburb through winter, who know what the comparable sales say about the price range they need to be in, and who have pre-approved finance aligned with that range. When the right property appears, they move.

Oxford Street is one of the best urban restaurant strips in Brisbane. Buyers who place the lifestyle dimension of Bulimba high in their decision-making are buying into a precinct as much as a property. The combination of waterfront park access at Bulimba Riverside Park, the Oxford Street dining and retail offer, and the ferry connections to New Farm and the CBD creates a daily living experience that commands the premium Bulimba consistently achieves.

The Bulimba State School catchment is a persistent driver of demand from family buyers. Catchment properties in the streets close to the school, particularly in the western part of the suburb, attract specific competition from buyers who have identified the catchment as a non-negotiable.

For vendors who are preparing for a spring campaign in Bulimba, the window from late August through mid-September is the point at which the best preparation effort produces the most return.
