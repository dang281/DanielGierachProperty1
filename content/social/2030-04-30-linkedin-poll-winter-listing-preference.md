## Caption

Most spring-selling advice in Australia is built around the Melbourne and Sydney markets, where there is a genuine seasonal swing from slow winters to active springs. In Brisbane's inner east, the pattern is different. Autumn is the most consistent window, and the spring uplift is less pronounced than the southern states would suggest.

If you are thinking about selling in 2030, when are you planning to go to market?

## Poll options

- May or June (still in the autumn-winter run)
- August or September (early spring)
