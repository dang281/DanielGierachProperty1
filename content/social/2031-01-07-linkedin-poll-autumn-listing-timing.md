## Caption

The first week of January is when a lot of sellers in Brisbane's inner east start having serious conversations about timing.

Autumn is the most consistent selling window in this market. Buyer numbers are strong, the heat has broken, and properties photograph well. But within autumn there are real differences: February campaigns run before school routines fully settle, while March and April tend to bring more competing stock. Getting the timing right matters.

If you are planning to sell your inner east home in 2031, when would you go to market?
