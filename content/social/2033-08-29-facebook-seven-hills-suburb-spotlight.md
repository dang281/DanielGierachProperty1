## Caption

Seven Hills offers an inner east address at a price point that rewards buyers who have done their research. The suburb sits within the same corridor as Coorparoo and Holland Park, with comparable access to the city and the same school catchment geography, but consistently at a more accessible price. Buyers who have identified this gap tend to move with conviction when the right property appears.

The elevated sections of Seven Hills produce outlooks over the eastern suburbs that are distinctive within this part of Brisbane. Properties on the ridge with clear lines toward the ranges or back toward the CBD give Seven Hills a visual character that buyers from the inner suburbs often find unexpectedly appealing when they first explore the area.

The suburb's position at the western edge of the Coorparoo corridor gives residents practical access to Coorparoo's retail and dining strip on Cavendish Road, without carrying the Coorparoo premium. That adjacency is part of how Seven Hills buyers frame their decision: the lifestyle access is largely shared, and the price difference is real.

Spring is the period when Seven Hills vendor campaigns typically attract the widest buyer pool. Buyers who have been tracking the suburb through winter tend to engage quickly when listings emerge in September.
