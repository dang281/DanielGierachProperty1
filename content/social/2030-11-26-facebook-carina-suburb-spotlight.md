## Caption

Carina's sales data in 2030 has reflected a pattern that buyers and vendors in the suburb have experienced across several market cycles: properties in the character home streets north of Creek Road perform with a different profile to the mixed-density and newer stock south of it.

The northern streets attract the buyer who is specifically targeting a freestanding home on a full block, within range of the Carindale shopping precinct and with access to the Norman Park school catchment corridor. That buyer is often coming from Morningside or Cannon Hill, where rising prices have pushed the full-block character home beyond their ceiling.

The suburb's access to both the Gateway Motorway and Old Cleveland Road makes it a practical location for buyers with employment outside the CBD. That external buyer cohort, often from the Gold Coast corridor or airport precinct, keeps demand competitive in Carina without being concentrated into one buyer profile.

Days on market in Carina through 2030 have been reasonable, sitting in the mid-to-high twenties for well-presented character homes. Properties that have sat longer have typically been overpriced relative to the comparable sales evidence for the specific street.

November remains an active period for the Carina buyer pool, particularly for families who want to be settled before the new school year.
