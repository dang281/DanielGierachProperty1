## Caption

Mt Gravatt offers something that becomes harder to find the closer you get to the city: genuine land. The larger lots here give families room to grow into a property, and Westfield Garden City provides the kind of daily convenience that removes friction from life. It is a suburb where people make long-term decisions rather than short-term ones.

The family demographic in Mt Gravatt is consistent and deliberate. Buyers here are typically upgrading from a smaller home nearby or relocating from outside the area with a specific brief: space, good schools, access to services. They take their time, but when they commit they are well-prepared and motivated.

Mid-December sits at an interesting point in the selling calendar. The households most ready to act before Christmas have already been searching for months; new listings catch their attention precisely because fresh supply in December is limited. For owners who have wanted to avoid the February crowd, this window has a logic to it. The summer heat is a real consideration in how you present, but it also creates conditions where a well-maintained garden and a properly cooled home stand apart.
