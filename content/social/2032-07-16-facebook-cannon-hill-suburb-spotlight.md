## Caption

Cannon Hill has a combination of infrastructure that is genuinely hard to replicate at its price point: a train station on the Cleveland line, a Coles anchored by Cannon Hill Plaza, Gateway Motorway access, and a clear run to the airport corridor, all within a compact suburban radius.

For buyers whose lives require regular travel, whether to the Gold Coast, the Sunshine Coast, or the airport precinct for work, this suburb's practical position is not incidental. It is the reason they are here.

The housing stock reflects a broad buyer base. Post-war brick and tile homes on generous blocks anchor the established residential streets. More recent townhouse development serves buyers who want the access story without the maintenance commitment of a large lot. Cannon Hill State School covers the primary catchment for families. Cannon Hill Anglicans holds a significant community presence in the suburb and draws owner-occupier buyers with an interest in that network.

The suburb does not carry the lifestyle premium of the river corridor addresses to its north, but it does not need to. The buyers who choose Cannon Hill are making a different calculation, and that calculation is well supported by the infrastructure around them.

For owners in Cannon Hill, the suburb's practical strengths translate directly to buyer motivation.
