## Caption

Carina Heights in December is a suburb where the summer quietness of the open home schedule is offset by the quality of the buyers who remain active. Buyers who are attending open homes in the middle of December in Carina Heights have not come for a casual look. They have a property decision to make and a timeline that the Christmas break has not deferred.

The suburb's appeal in the second half of the year is consistent. The elevated streets above the Carina and Carindale retail corridors offer residents a separation from the busier activity below that the flat sections of those adjacent suburbs cannot provide. In summer, the elevated position and the generally generous tree cover on the established streets gives Carina Heights a greenery and shade quality that buyers from the more exposed suburbs find appealing.

The school catchment question for families in Carina Heights remains the most consistent subject of due diligence. The Whites Hill State College boundary, which runs through parts of the suburb, creates a meaningful pricing divergence between inside-boundary and outside-boundary properties that buyers with secondary school-aged children research carefully before making an offer.

For homeowners in Carina Heights who are planning a February autumn campaign, December is the right time to begin the preparation conversation. A February launch prepared in January tends to achieve better results than one prepared in February itself.
