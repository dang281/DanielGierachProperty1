## Caption

Carina Heights occupies an interesting position in the inner east property market. The suburb sits adjacent to Carindale and Carina, shares access to the Westfield Carindale retail precinct, and offers block sizes and housing stock that compete well on value against suburbs further west. The buyers who arrive at Carina Heights open homes in spring tend to have assessed the full eastern corridor and identified Carina Heights as the suburb where price, block size, and location intersect at the most favourable point.

The elevated sections of Carina Heights deliver ridge outlooks that are consistently noted by buyers at open homes. Properties with uninterrupted views toward the ranges or the city frame Carina Heights in a light that some buyers, particularly those coming from the flat eastern suburbs, find compelling when they first see the suburb from the right vantage point. That topography is part of what differentiates the suburb within the broader Carina corridor.

Family buyers in Carina Heights benefit from proximity to a school catchment geography that covers both primary and secondary options without requiring significant travel. That combination of school access, block size, and price relative to comparable inner east suburbs positions Carina Heights as one of the more practical family suburb choices in this corridor.

For vendors preparing a spring campaign, September is when the Carina Heights buyer pool is most active and best financed.
