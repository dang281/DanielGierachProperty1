## Caption

I talk to a lot of sellers about timing, and the reasons people choose when to list are more varied than most agents let on. The Olympics, interest rates, the school calendar, personal circumstances, the state of the market in their specific street: all of it feeds into the decision.

In an Olympic year it is tempting to assume every seller is thinking about July. Some are. But plenty of others are focused on entirely different things, and they are right to be. The best time to sell is the time that works for your situation and gives you the best chance of the right buyer being in the market.

What is the single biggest factor driving your thinking about when to list this year?
