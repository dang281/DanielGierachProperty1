## Caption

The December settlement goal shapes buyer and vendor behaviour in ways that are distinct from any other period in the year. The combination of school enrolment deadlines, Christmas and January holiday planning, and the psychological desire to be settled before the new year creates real time pressure for a significant portion of the market. Buyers who want to be in their next home for the school year need to have exchanged contracts before Christmas. Vendors who want to avoid relisting in February have a similar calculation. It is one of the few points in the year when the buyer's timeline and the vendor's timeline often run in the same direction at the same time. Have you ever made a property decision driven by end-of-year timing?
