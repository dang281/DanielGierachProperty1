## Caption

Selling a property involves weekly open homes, ongoing buyer inquiries, and an outcome that is not certain until the contract is signed and the conditional period has passed. Most sellers who have been through a campaign will recognise at least one of these pressure points very clearly, and many will say the hardest part was not the one they expected going in. If you have sold before, or are currently selling, which part of the process would you say is the hardest?

## Poll options

- Not knowing whether offers will come in at the right level
- Preparing the property for open homes each week
- Managing negotiations after an offer is made
- Waiting through the conditional period
