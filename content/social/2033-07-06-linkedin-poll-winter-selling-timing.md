## Caption

The advice to wait for spring is common enough that a lot of inner east vendors follow it without questioning whether it applies to their specific situation. Winter in Brisbane is not the same as winter in Melbourne. The fundamentals here, school catchments, riverside access, proximity to the CBD, do not pause for July. The buyers pursuing those fundamentals keep looking year-round. If you owned in Brisbane's inner east and needed to sell, which approach would you take?
