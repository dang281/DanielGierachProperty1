## Caption

Brisbane's winter is mild enough that waiting for warmer weather is often waiting for no practical reason. The inner east market in July and August has a smaller buyer pool than spring, which means less competition for a buyer who is ready to act. That dynamic can work in a buyer's favour, particularly for properties where vendor motivation is higher than it would be in a competitive spring campaign. The question is whether buyers actually see it that way or whether the spring habit runs deeper than logic.

## Poll options

- Yes, motivated sellers and less competition
- Yes, but I would want to see the property in warmer weather too
