## Caption

Carina Heights is at its most active in the autumn selling season. The suburb's family buyer cohort, which is consistent and well-informed, tends to time its property search around the school calendar, and the autumn window aligns well with the practical reality of settling and relocating before the mid-year school break.

The suburb's block sizes are one of its quiet advantages. In a market where families are consistently looking for functional outdoor space and room for off-street parking, Carina Heights has stock that delivers this at price points that have remained accessible relative to Camp Hill and Norman Park. The Westfield Carindale proximity is worth stating plainly: the convenience of having a major retail and services centre within a short drive shapes the daily life of residents here in a practical way that younger families particularly appreciate. It reduces the friction of suburban life in a way that walkable inner suburbs do more expensively.

For vendors in February 2033, the advice is the same as it is in any active market: preparation first, then pricing, then the campaign. The buyers looking at Carina Heights right now have done their research. A property that presents as well as it is priced will find a buyer quickly.
