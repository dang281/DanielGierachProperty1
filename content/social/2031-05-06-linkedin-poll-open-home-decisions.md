## Caption

Open home behaviour in Brisbane's inner east has become increasingly decisive over the past several years. Buyers who are prepared and ready to act often form a clear view within the first walkthrough. A second inspection, when it happens, tends to be a confirmation exercise rather than a discovery one. Vendors and their agents pay close attention to how quickly buyers request that second visit. The speed of that signal matters to how a campaign is managed. How quickly do you typically know whether a property is one you'd consider making an offer on?
