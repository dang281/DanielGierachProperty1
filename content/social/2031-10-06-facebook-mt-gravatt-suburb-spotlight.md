## Caption

From the higher streets in Mt Gravatt Outlook, you can see across to the CBD on a clear day. That view adds real money to the right property, and buyers who find it tend not to let go easily.

Mt Gravatt covers more ground than most people expect. The Griffith University campus at Upper Mt Gravatt brings an academic and professional buyer demographic that other inner south suburbs don't have. The Plaza shopping precinct anchors the commercial centre. South East Freeway access is one of the suburb's practical advantages for buyers who commute, and that gets mentioned in almost every inspection on the busier streets.

The mix of long-term residents and newer arrivals creates a market where you see genuine renovation uplift alongside established homes that haven't been touched in years. Both sell, but they attract very different buyers. Renovated homes on elevated blocks, particularly those with outlook toward the east, attract the most competition. Flat blocks near the main road corridors move more on price than on emotion.
