## Caption

Bulimba's October market window builds on the buyer activity that September generates. The buyers who are still actively searching in late September and early October for a Bulimba property are the most committed segment of the buyer pool: buyers who have attended multiple open homes, made and missed offers, and are ready to pay what it takes for the right property. Vendors who launch in late September and take their campaign through the first week of October often find these buyers waiting for them.

Oxford Street is one of Brisbane's most consistently excellent urban dining and retail strips. The combination of neighbourhood scale, independent quality, and the street's energy across the full week gives Bulimba a lifestyle character that buyers purchasing in this suburb are explicitly choosing. It is not incidental to the Bulimba purchase decision; for most buyers here it is central to it.

The Bulimba State School catchment and the ferry connections to New Farm and the CBD create the family buyer argument for Bulimba that is straightforward to make. School access, river access via Bulimba Riverside Park, Oxford Street within walking distance, and ferry connections to the city: each element reinforces the others, and buyers understand the full package when they buy here.

Spring in Bulimba rewards vendors who present their property at the highest standard their budget allows.
