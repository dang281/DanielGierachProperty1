## Caption

In Brisbane's inner east, school catchment boundaries can affect a property's value by $100,000 or more compared to an identical home on the wrong side of the line.

The demand for homes within catchments like Whites Hill State College, Coorparoo State School, and Bulimba State School is consistently reflected in sale prices. At auction, it is visible in real time: multiple bidders, sharper competition, results above reserve.

For buyers, this creates a genuine decision: pay the premium for the catchment, or compromise on school and find a better price point elsewhere. For sellers, being in the right catchment is a genuine selling point worth understanding and communicating in a campaign.

Has a school catchment ever been a deciding factor in where you bought or are looking to buy?
