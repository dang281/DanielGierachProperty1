## Caption

Spring is the season when auction numbers increase significantly across Brisbane's inner east. Agents and vendors often default to auction in spring because the buyer pool is larger.

But the best method of sale depends on the property and the buyer profile, not the season.

Quick question for the Brisbane property community:

If you were selling your home, which method would you prefer?

## Poll options

- Auction: maximum competition
- Private treaty: control and flexibility
- Expression of interest: suits my property type
- Whatever the agent recommends
