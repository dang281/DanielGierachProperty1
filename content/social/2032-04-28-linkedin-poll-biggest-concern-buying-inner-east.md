## Caption

I spend a lot of time talking with buyers at the moment, and the concerns I hear vary quite a bit depending on where someone is in the process.

Some buyers are worried about paying too much in a market with limited supply and real competition. Others are more focused on what happens to values once the Olympics are done and the attention moves elsewhere. A few are simply struggling to find the right property because stock is low. And some are still working through the financing picture with everything that has shifted over recent years.

It is useful to understand what is weighing on people's minds, because it shapes the kind of advice that's actually relevant. If you were buying in the inner east right now, what would be your biggest concern?
