## Caption

Days on market is the number of days a property has been listed for sale before going under contract. In Brisbane's inner east, a healthy campaign tends to produce meaningful inquiry, multiple inspections, and at least one serious offer conversation in the first two to four weeks. Past that window without a real offer, sellers face a decision that splits opinion.

What should the seller do first when a property has been on the market for four weeks without a serious offer? Vote your gut answer.
