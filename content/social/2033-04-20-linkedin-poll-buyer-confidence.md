## Caption

Buyer confidence in Brisbane's inner east is always harder to read than the headline numbers suggest. Some buyers who have been looking for months feel the market is moving away from them. Others feel the current conditions are the most reasonable buying window they've seen in two years. Both views exist simultaneously right now, and both are defensible depending on what you're buying and where. I'm genuinely curious where people sit. If you are currently looking to buy in Brisbane, how would you describe your confidence in the market right now?
