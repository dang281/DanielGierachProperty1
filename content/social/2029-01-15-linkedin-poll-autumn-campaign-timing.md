## Caption

Brisbane's inner east autumn selling window runs from late February through to mid-May. It is the most consistent selling period in this market, and the buyer depth in March and April typically outperforms spring in terms of both volume and quality.

If you are considering listing your home this year, timing your campaign around this window can make a meaningful difference to both the result and the experience of selling.
