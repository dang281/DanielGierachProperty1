## Caption

Open home attendance in Brisbane's inner east peaks in spring, and the profile of the buyer walking through the door shifts noticeably from the winter open home crowd. Spring buyers include more early-stage browsers alongside the committed buyers who remained active through winter. Understanding what different buyers prioritise at a first inspection helps vendors think about where to focus presentation investment. The data from what buyers notice first is often surprising: it is rarely the obvious things. If you were walking through a property for sale in Brisbane's inner east this spring, what would you register first?

## Poll options

- Light and space (natural light, room proportions)
- Condition and maintenance (what needs work)
- The flow and layout (how the rooms connect)
- Street appeal and first impression before I walked in
