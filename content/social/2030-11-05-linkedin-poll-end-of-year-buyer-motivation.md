## Caption

Late October and early November is when buyer motivation in the inner east tends to sharpen. The lookers become the deciders.

Some of that is seasonal: the pre-Christmas settlement deadline focuses minds. Some of it is financial: borrowing capacity calculations completed, pre-approval letters in hand. And some of it is simply fatigue from a search that has been running longer than the buyer expected.

Curious what is actually driving decisions for buyers in the market right now.
