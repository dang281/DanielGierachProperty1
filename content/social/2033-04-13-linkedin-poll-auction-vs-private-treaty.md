## Caption

Method of sale is one of the most consequential decisions a vendor makes, and it is often made too quickly. In Brisbane's inner east, auction works well when buyer competition is strong and the property has broad appeal. Private treaty suits a narrower buyer pool or a vendor who needs flexibility on timing and conditions. Neither method is universally better. The right choice depends on the property, the price point, and the market conditions at the time. If you were selling your home in the inner east this autumn, which method would you prefer?
