## Caption

Many inner east homeowners have held their property for a decade or more. Values in suburbs like Hawthorne, Bulimba, and Norman Park have moved substantially over that time. Most of these owners haven't modelled what a sale would mean for their tax position because they haven't needed to yet. The main residence exemption provides real protection in straightforward cases, but it is not unlimited, and it has conditions most people haven't read. Before you decide to sell, it's worth knowing where you actually stand. Do you know your approximate capital gains tax position if you sold your main property today?
