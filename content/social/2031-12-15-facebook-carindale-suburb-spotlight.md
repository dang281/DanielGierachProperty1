## Caption

The combination of Westfield Carindale and direct Gateway Motorway access makes Carindale one of the most practically functional suburbs in the southeast. Residents can get almost everything done without leaving the suburb, and the motorway means commuting in multiple directions is genuinely straightforward. That practical package is a consistent draw for buyers relocating to Brisbane from interstate.

Large blocks are a defining feature. In an inner Brisbane context, where 400 square metres has become the norm and 600 is considered generous, Carindale regularly turns up blocks in the 700 to 800 square metre range. For families with school-aged children, that land size changes how they think about outdoor space, about renovation potential, and about the long-term fit of a property.

December in Carindale sees families in active buying mode, particularly those who want to be in a new home before the school year begins in late January. The window between now and Christmas is when those decisions happen. Buyers who have been through the spring market and have their finance in place are ready.

The investor side of the market here remains present. Rental yields hold up well relative to purchase price, and the suburb's strong infrastructure makes it easy to tenant. Dual-appeal suburbs like Carindale tend to hold value well across the full cycle.
