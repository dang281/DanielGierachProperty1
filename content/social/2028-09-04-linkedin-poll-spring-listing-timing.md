## Caption

Spring is the season most sellers in Brisbane aim for, even though the evidence for autumn campaigns in the inner east is equally strong. The instinct toward spring is understandable: it is what everyone does, the gardens look better, and buyers feel more active.

The practical question for sellers considering spring right now is which part of spring. September means you are competing with the sellers who have been planning this for six months and are ready to go. November means you are racing the pre-Christmas window. October has historically been the sweet spot in the inner east: enough buyers active, enough stock on market to create context, and enough time before the December slowdown.

If you were listing your home for spring, when would you aim to launch?
