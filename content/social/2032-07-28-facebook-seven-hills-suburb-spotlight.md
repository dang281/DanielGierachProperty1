## Caption

Seven Hills is the inner-east suburb that the broadest slice of buyers have not yet noticed. That is unusual for a suburb this close to Coorparoo and Stones Corner, and it creates a straightforward situation: patient buyers can find value here that is not available in the suburbs immediately to its north and east.

The suburb sits between Holland Park to the west and Coorparoo and Stones Corner to the east. The Seven Hills shopping precinct on Boundary Road anchors daily retail. The residential character is post-war homes on generous blocks, quieter streets, and an absence of the price premium that comes with the address recognition of its neighbours.

Buyers arriving in Seven Hills are usually being priced out of Camp Hill or Coorparoo but want to stay inside the inner-east boundary. Some sections of Seven Hills sit within the Coorparoo State School catchment, which is a consistent draw for families who want the school access without paying the full Coorparoo price.

For owners in Seven Hills, the suburb's position as an underserved market can work in both directions. Buyers who find it here often find it on merit rather than by accident, which means they tend to be motivated once they arrive.
