## Caption

One of the most common preparation errors I see before a spring campaign is vendors choosing styling before they have addressed the underlying maintenance. Professional furniture and fresh cushions are visible in the photos. So is the cracked cornice and the bathroom grout that was never sealed. Buyers notice the styling. Their building inspector notices everything else.

Preparation order matters. Most vendors get it wrong by at least one step.

If you were listing for spring, which would you tackle first?

## Poll options
