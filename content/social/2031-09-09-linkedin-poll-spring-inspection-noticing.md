## Caption

What a buyer notices in the first 60 seconds of a property inspection is not always what they think drives their decision. Research into buyer behaviour consistently shows that emotional response to light, space, and atmosphere comes before the analytical assessment of condition and value. Vendors who present their property with an understanding of what buyers register first tend to put their preparation effort where it matters most rather than where it is most visible to them as the owner. What do you notice first when you walk in?

## Poll options

- The light, aspect, and how the rooms feel
- The condition and whether things need work
- The layout and how the spaces flow
- The outdoor areas and connection to the garden or view
