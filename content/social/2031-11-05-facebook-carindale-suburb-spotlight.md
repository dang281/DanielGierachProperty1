## Caption

Westfield Carindale is one of the largest regional shopping centres in Brisbane, and it sits right in this suburb's backyard. That means residents have retail, dining, and services covered without leaving the postcode. For buyers coming from outer suburbs, that convenience is a significant draw in the relocation decision.

Carindale's Gateway Motorway access makes it highly practical for buyers who work in the south-east corridor, the airport precinct, or the city. That flexibility in commute direction broadens the buyer pool considerably. Families make up the core owner-occupier demand, and the good school options in the area keep that demographic active through most market conditions.

The blocks here are generous by Brisbane's inner standards. Large yards, double garages, and the kind of family layout that disappears as you move closer to the CBD. Investors have historically been drawn to Carindale for its rental yield profile and strong tenant demand. That dual appeal of owner-occupiers and investors is part of what gives the suburb resilience.

What sells well here is the family home in good condition with strong indoor-outdoor flow and good off-street parking. Properties that need significant capital work do move, but they require patient vendors and realistic pricing.
