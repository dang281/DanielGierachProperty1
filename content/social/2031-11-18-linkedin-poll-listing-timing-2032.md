## Caption

For vendors who are planning to sell their inner east home in early 2032, the timing decision is more interesting than usual. The Olympic year adds a layer to what is normally a straightforward seasonal question.

The conventional answer for inner east vendors is February or March, when the autumn buyer pool is building and competition from other sellers is manageable before the April and May peak. That logic still holds. But 2032 is not a conventional year. The question of whether Olympic attention creates a stronger buyer pool earlier in the year, or whether it simply amplifies the existing autumn pattern, is one that will only be answered in real time.

There is also the preparation question. Waiting for a specific month only makes sense if the property will genuinely be ready by then. Vendors who aim for late January but need until March to get presentation right are better served by targeting the March market and getting it right.

If you are planning to sell your inner east home in early 2032, when would you aim to list?

## Poll options

- Late January to catch the post-holiday buyer rush
- February when serious buyers are back in numbers
- March or April for the full autumn selling window
- Whenever preparation is complete, not around a specific month
