## Caption

Carindale is anchored by Westfield Carindale, one of Brisbane's largest and most complete retail centres, and that anchor does real work in defining the suburb's practical appeal. Buyers who come to Carindale have identified it as a suburb where the retail and services infrastructure matches their daily requirements without the need to drive to multiple destinations. For families in particular, that consolidation of everyday logistics into a walkable or short-drive radius is a genuine quality of life factor.

The housing stock in Carindale is predominantly postwar and contemporary, reflecting the suburb's 1970s and 1980s development history. Large block sizes are one of Carindale's consistent selling points. Buyers who have been priced out of the closer-in suburbs and who are prioritising land content and house size find Carindale's fundamentals align with those priorities.

The suburb's position on the eastern edge of the inner east, with direct access to the Pacific Motorway and the Gateway Motorway, makes it particularly practical for buyers managing commutes to the airport, the Gold Coast corridor, or outer south-east Brisbane employment centres. That dual motorway access is a consistent part of the suburb's buyer value proposition.
