## Caption

2029 was a significant year for property in Brisbane's inner east. Stock remained tight, the buyer pool stayed competitive, and the 2032 Olympics shifted from distant headline to visible reality. The Gabba precinct and the Chandler corridor are showing infrastructure that confirms the timeline is real.

We are now less than two years from the Games opening. That is close enough for buyers who were sceptical in 2026 to start paying attention to the inner east with a different kind of urgency.

Heading into 2030, the fundamentals that have defined this precinct for the past several years remain intact: constrained land supply, strong school catchments, proximity to the city, and a buyer pool that consistently runs deeper than available stock suggests it should.

What is your primary property goal for 2030?
