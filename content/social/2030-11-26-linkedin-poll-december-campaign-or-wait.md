## Caption

We are in the last week of November. For homeowners considering a sale, the question of whether to launch in December or wait for the February autumn market is now pressing.

Both have legitimate arguments. A December campaign targets a motivated but thinning buyer pool. A February campaign arrives at the start of the most consistent selling season in Brisbane's inner east with a fresh buyer cohort.

The answer is different for every property and vendor. But the instinct of most homeowners at this time of year tends in a clear direction.

If you were selling your inner east home, which would you choose?
