## Caption

Preparation time before going to market is one of those decisions that looks straightforward but has real consequences for the campaign outcome.

Too little time and the property goes live before it is ready: photography is rushed, styling is absent, and the first open home sets a low benchmark that is hard to recover from. Too much time and sellers miss the best window in the season, or talk themselves into unnecessary renovation work. The four to six week mark is standard for a reason, but sellers land all over the map on this one.

If you were listing your home for sale, how long would you want to prepare before the first open home?

## Poll options

- 2 to 3 weeks: I can move quickly
- 4 to 6 weeks: the standard window
- 8 weeks or more: I want it right
- As long as it takes, I will not rush
