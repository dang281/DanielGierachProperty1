## Caption

January in Brisbane property is genuinely two different things depending on who you ask.

Some agents will tell you it is quiet, everyone is at the beach, and serious activity does not resume until late January or February. Others will tell you the first two weeks of January are when interstate buyers who visited over Christmas make decisions, and that properties going live in that window face almost no competition from other sellers.

Both are correct. It depends on the suburb, the price point, and whether you are a buyer or a seller.

As you head into the Christmas break, what is your read on January?
