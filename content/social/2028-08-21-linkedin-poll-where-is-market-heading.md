## Caption

Brisbane's inner east has outperformed most comparable city markets over the past several years. The question I get asked most often right now is whether that trajectory continues, or whether the market is entering a different phase.

The factors driving the inner east are not identical to the broader Brisbane or national picture. Population growth into the inner suburbs, a constrained supply of character homes, school catchment demand, and the infrastructure investment associated with the 2032 Games all point to continued demand. The counterweight is affordability: at current price levels, the buyer pool narrows, and it narrows from the bottom up.

What is your read on the direction of Brisbane's inner east over the next twelve months?
