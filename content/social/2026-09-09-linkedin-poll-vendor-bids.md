## Caption

In QLD, vendors are legally allowed to make a declared vendor bid at auction up to the reserve price. The intention is to keep bidding moving. But many buyers find it confusing or feel it creates false competition. Dummy bids from undisclosed third parties are completely illegal. Vendor bids are different, they must be declared by the auctioneer, but the line between "keeping it moving" and "manufacturing momentum" is one buyers reasonably debate.

What's your view on vendor bids at auction?
