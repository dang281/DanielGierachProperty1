## What Each Suburb Spotlight Must Include

**Structure:**
1. Strong opening line — specific, not generic
2. Buyer profile (who buys here and why)
3. Price trend or recent sales insight (median, growth %, days on market if available)
4. Lifestyle appeal (schools, cafes, transport, proximity to CBD)
5. Supply/demand observation (what's selling, what's not, what buyers compete for)
6. Closing line + CTA: "danielgierach.com"

**Rules:**
- Every stat must have a source noted in "Notes for Daniel" with ⚠️ VERIFY flag
- No fluff. No "hidden gem" language. No generic phrases.
- Write like you know the streets — specific streets, specific schools, specific selling points
- 250–350 words for the caption

**Visual Brief Required:** Yes — describe what the Canva graphic should show (map, stats card, suburb headline)
