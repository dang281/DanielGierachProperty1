## section: transport

Fortitude Valley has its own railway station with multiple line connections. Off-peak travel into the CBD is short. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

The Brunswick Street Mall is a major bus interchange.

By car, the Inner City Bypass and Story Bridge sit on the doorstep. The CBD is around 3 to 5 minutes away off-peak.

For active transport, the inner-CBD walking and cycling routes connect the Valley to the CBD, James Street, and Newstead.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)

## section: pockets

The Valley breaks into three pockets buyers consider distinctly.

The James Street precinct is premium apartment stock and boutique retail. Premium positioning.

The Brunswick Street and entertainment precinct includes apartments close to the bar and dining strip.

The TC Beirne and Chinatown area is mixed-use redevelopment.

## section: lifestyle

James Street is the premium dining and retail precinct. Brunswick Street has the Valley's casual dining and bar scene. Howard Smith Wharves and Newstead Gasworks are within walking distance for additional dining and lifestyle.

For open space, Centenary Place and New Farm Park nearby provide green-space access.

The lifestyle here is dense urban. Walk-to-everything is the proposition, and the trade-off is density.

## section: buyer profile

Three buyer types drive the Valley market.

The largest is inner-CBD professionals wanting walk-to-work and walk-to-dining lifestyle.

The second group is investors. Strong rental demand from professionals and students supports consistent investor activity.

The third group is downsizers moving to apartments close to the CBD and James Street.

## section: selling approach

Selling in the Valley means matching the apartment campaign to the building, the floor, and the buyer profile.

Photography is non-negotiable in the apartment market. The view, layout, and building amenity all need to be captured clearly.

On method, both auction and private treaty work for apartments depending on building and buyer pool depth.
