## section: transport

Toowong has its own railway station on the Ipswich line. Off-peak travel to Central runs around 8 to 12 minutes. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

For ferry, the Toowong CityCat runs to the CBD and South Bank.

Bus routes serve Toowong via Milton Road, Coronation Drive, and Sylvan Road.

By car, Coronation Drive and Western Freeway access put the CBD around 6 to 10 minutes away off-peak.

For active transport, the Bicentennial Bikeway runs along the river to the CBD, and the Western Suburbs bikeway connects through the area.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)

## section: schools

Toowong State School is the local primary state school. Catchment boundaries should be confirmed through the Queensland Government's catchment lookup at [qgso.qld.gov.au/maps/edmap](https://www.qgso.qld.gov.au/maps/edmap).

For secondary state schooling, Indooroopilly State High School or Toowong State High School catchment apply depending on the address.

For private schooling, Brisbane Boys' College and Stuartholme School both sit within Toowong.

## section: pockets

Toowong breaks into three pockets buyers consider distinctly.

The family streets are quieter residential character with established family homes. Toowong State School catchment is the central buyer driver.

The riverside / Sylvan Road pocket is apartment market with river outlooks. Premium positioning.

The High Street and station precinct includes a mix of apartments and townhouses close to transport and shopping.

## section: lifestyle

Toowong Village is the suburb's main shopping centre with full-line retail and dining. High Street has additional cafes and restaurants.

For open space, Toowong Memorial Park and Anzac Park provide walking and weekend-park options. The Brisbane River frontage along Sylvan Road extends the lifestyle network.

The lifestyle here is established inner-west family suburb with strong transport, retail, and river access.

## section: buyer profile

Three buyer types drive the Toowong market.

The largest is established families. Toowong State School catchment access is the central driver.

The second group is hospital and university professionals. Wesley Hospital nearby and UQ via the river make Toowong a natural choice for medical and academic professionals.

The third group is apartment buyers. Riverside apartments and station-adjacent apartments support a strong professional buyer pool.
