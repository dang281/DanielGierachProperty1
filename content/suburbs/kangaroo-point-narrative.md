## section: transport

Kangaroo Point's closest stations are South Brisbane and Park Road, walkable for parts of the suburb. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

For ferry, multiple CityCat terminals along the Kangaroo Point cliffs run to the CBD and South Bank. The river commute is one of the practical lifestyle assets of the suburb.

Bus routes serve Kangaroo Point via Main Street and Lytton Road.

By car, the Story Bridge puts the CBD around 3 to 6 minutes away off-peak. Pacific Motorway and South-East Freeway access handle southern travel.

For active transport, the Kangaroo Point Bridge for pedestrians and cyclists connects directly to the CBD, and the Brisbane Riverwalk runs through the suburb.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)

## section: pockets

Kangaroo Point breaks into three pockets buyers consider distinctly.

The riverside cliffs pocket includes apartment stock with CBD and river views. Premium pocket of the suburb.

The Main Street precinct includes a mix of apartment and townhouse stock close to the dining strip.

The Park Road area sits closer to the train and the Gabba precinct.

## section: lifestyle

Kangaroo Point has dining clusters along Main Street and the riverside precinct. Howard Smith Wharves sits across the river. South Bank cultural precinct is a short walk via the Goodwill Bridge.

For open space, the Kangaroo Point Cliffs and Mowbray Park nearby provide walking, picnicking, and the outdoor climbing scene the cliffs are known for.

The lifestyle here is inner-CBD river apartment living. CBD walkability via the new pedestrian bridge is a meaningful asset.

## section: buyer profile

Three buyer types drive the Kangaroo Point market.

The largest is inner-CBD professionals wanting walk or short drive to the CBD with river views.

The second group is apartment downsizers moving from larger family homes to apartment living with the CBD outlook.

The third group is investors. Strong rental demand close to the CBD supports consistent investor activity.

## section: selling approach

Selling in Kangaroo Point means matching the apartment campaign to the building, the floor, and the view. View-line apartments suit a different campaign to internal apartments at the same price level.

Photography is non-negotiable. The view is the asset for premium apartments and capturing it correctly is central.

On method, both auction and private treaty work for apartments depending on building and pool depth.
