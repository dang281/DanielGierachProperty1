## section: transport

Holland Park does not have its own railway station. The closest station is Coorparoo on the Cleveland line, a drive for most addresses. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

Bus routes serve Holland Park via Logan Road and the South-East Busway corridor.

By car, the M3 corridor handles southern travel and the CBD sits around 13 to 17 minutes away off-peak.

For active transport, the South-East Busway corridor connects through the area.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)

## section: pockets

Holland Park breaks into two pockets.

The family streets near Holland Park State School are the catchment-driven family-home market.

The Logan Road corridor sits closer to the arterial with a mix of stock types.

## section: lifestyle

Logan Road has the suburb's main retail and dining strip. Westfield Carindale and Mt Gravatt Garden City are within a short drive for big-format retail.

For open space, Whites Hill Reserve and the Holland Park Tennis Centre provide local recreation options.

The lifestyle here is family-suburb southside Brisbane.

## section: buyer profile

Two buyer types drive the Holland Park market.

The largest is family upgraders targeting the catchment and the family-suburb feel.

The second group is buyers priced out of Coorparoo or Camp Hill who want similar family access at a more accessible price.

## section: selling approach
