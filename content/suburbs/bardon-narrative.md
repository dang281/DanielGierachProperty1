## section: transport

Bardon does not have its own railway station. Auchenflower and Toowong stations are the closest options for southern Bardon streets. For most Bardon addresses, the train is a short drive rather than a walk. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

Bus routes serve Bardon via MacGregor Terrace, Boundary Road, and Stewart Road. Routes change with service updates.

By car, the Western Freeway handles southern travel and the CBD sits around 10 to 14 minutes away off-peak.

For active transport, the Western Suburbs bikeway and Mount Coot-tha walking and mountain bike trails are accessible from much of the suburb.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)
