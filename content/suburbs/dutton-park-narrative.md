## section: transport

Dutton Park has its own railway station on the Beenleigh and Cleveland line corridor. Off-peak travel into the CBD is short. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

For ferry, the Eleanor Schonell Bridge connects pedestrians and cyclists to UQ St Lucia and the CityCat terminal there. The river crossing is one of the practical lifestyle assets of the suburb.

Bus routes serve Dutton Park via Annerley Road and the inner-CBD corridor.

By car, the M3 corridor handles southern travel and the Captain Cook Bridge route puts the CBD around 5 to 9 minutes away off-peak.

For active transport, the Eleanor Schonell Bridge and the South-east Bikeway / Veloway 1 connect to UQ and the broader cycling network.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)

## section: schools

Dutton Park State School is the local primary state school. Catchment boundaries should be confirmed through the Queensland Government's catchment lookup at [qgso.qld.gov.au/maps/edmap](https://www.qgso.qld.gov.au/maps/edmap).

For secondary state schooling, parts of Dutton Park may fall within the Brisbane State High School catchment. BSHS access is one of the most-checked catchments by buyers.

For private schooling, Somerville House in South Brisbane is accessible within a short drive.

## section: pockets

Dutton Park breaks into two pockets.

The riverside and hospital precinct includes streets close to the river and the PA Hospital. Mix of character and apartment stock with a strong professional buyer base.

The Annerley Road corridor sits closer to the arterial with a different character and price profile.

## section: lifestyle

West End and South Brisbane precincts are within walking distance for dining, retail, and cultural access. PA Hospital sits adjacent and drives a substantial professional resident base.

For open space, Dutton Park and the Brisbane River frontage provide walking and weekend-park options.

## section: buyer profile

Three buyer types drive the Dutton Park market.

The largest is hospital and university professionals. PA Hospital and UQ proximity drive a strong professional buyer base.

The second group is inner-CBD apartment buyers attracted to the apartment market close to the river and the CBD.

The third group is BSHS catchment buyers where the catchment applies for the address.
