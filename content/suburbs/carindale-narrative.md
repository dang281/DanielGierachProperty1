## section: transport

Carindale does not have its own railway station. The closest stations are Cannon Hill and Manly West on the Cleveland line, both a drive from most addresses. Buses are the practical public transport, with a major interchange at Westfield Carindale. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

Bus routes serve Carindale via Old Cleveland Road, Creek Road, and Pine Mountain Road. The Westfield bus interchange connects to the CBD and other suburbs.

By car, Gateway Motorway access via Carindale and Tilley Roads handles airport and M1 routes. The CBD sits around 16 to 20 minutes away off-peak.

For active transport, the Bulimba Creek bikeway provides cycling and walking connections.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)

## section: schools

Carindale State School is the local primary state school. Belmont State School serves parts of the suburb. Catchment boundaries should be confirmed through the Queensland Government's catchment lookup at [qgso.qld.gov.au/maps/edmap](https://www.qgso.qld.gov.au/maps/edmap).

For secondary state schooling, Whites Hill State College is the common catchment.

For private schooling, Citipointe Christian College sits within Carindale, and San Sisto College in Carina is within a short drive.

## section: lifestyle

Westfield Carindale is one of the largest shopping centres in Brisbane with full-line retail, supermarkets, dining, and cinema. The convenience is genuine and is a central reason buyers shortlist the suburb.

For open space, Minnippi Parklands and the Bulimba Creek parkland provide walking and weekend-park options.

The lifestyle here is suburban family east Brisbane. Larger blocks, parking, retail convenience, and a quieter feel than the inner east suburbs to the west.
