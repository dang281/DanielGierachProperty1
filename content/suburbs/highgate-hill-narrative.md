## section: transport

Highgate Hill is served by South Brisbane railway station and Dutton Park railway station depending on which part of the suburb the address sits in. Off-peak travel into the CBD is short. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

Bus routes serve Highgate Hill via Annerley Road, Vulture Street, and Boundary Street. The South-East Busway is on the doorstep.

By car, the M3 corridor and CBD access via Captain Cook Bridge handle the commute. The CBD sits around 5 to 8 minutes away off-peak.

For active transport, the Eleanor Schonell Bridge connects to UQ St Lucia for cyclists and pedestrians. The South-east Bikeway runs through the area.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)

## section: schools

West End State School and Dutton Park State School serve different parts of Highgate Hill as primary state catchments. Catchment boundaries should be confirmed through the Queensland Government's catchment lookup at [qgso.qld.gov.au/maps/edmap](https://www.qgso.qld.gov.au/maps/edmap).

For private schooling, Somerville House and St Laurence's College in South Brisbane are accessible within a short walk or drive.
