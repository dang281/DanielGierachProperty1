## section: transport

New Farm's closest stations are Fortitude Valley and Bowen Hills, walkable for parts of the suburb. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

For ferry, the New Farm Park CityCat terminal runs to the CBD, South Bank, and UQ. The river commute is one of the practical lifestyle assets of the suburb.

Bus routes serve New Farm via Brunswick Street and James Street.

By car, the Story Bridge and Inner City Bypass put the CBD around 4 to 7 minutes away off-peak.

For active transport, the Brisbane Riverwalk runs along the river through New Farm Park, and walking access to James Street and Howard Smith Wharves is genuine for many addresses.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)
