## section: transport

Upper Mount Gravatt does not have a train station. The Garden City busway interchange sits within the suburb and is one of the busiest bus interchanges in Brisbane. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

Bus routes serve the suburb via Logan Road and the South-East Busway.

By car, the M3 and Pacific Motorway corridor handle southern travel. The CBD sits around 18 to 22 minutes away off-peak.

For active transport, the South-East Busway corridor connects through the suburb.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)
