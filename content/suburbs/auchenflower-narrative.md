## section: transport

Auchenflower has its own railway station on the Ferny Grove and Doomben line corridor. Off-peak travel to Roma Street runs around 5 to 8 minutes. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

Bus routes serve the suburb via Milton Road and Coronation Drive.

By car, Coronation Drive puts the CBD around 5 to 10 minutes away off-peak. Western Freeway access handles southern travel.

For active transport, the Bicentennial Bikeway runs along the river to the CBD, and the Coronation Drive bikeway is one of the most-used commuting routes in inner Brisbane.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)
