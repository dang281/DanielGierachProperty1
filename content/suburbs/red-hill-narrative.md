## section: transport

Red Hill's closest station is Windsor on the Ferny Grove line, walkable for parts of Red Hill. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

Bus routes serve Red Hill via Musgrave Road and Waterworks Road.

By car, the Inner City Bypass puts the CBD around 5 to 9 minutes away off-peak.

For active transport, the Western Suburbs bikeway connects through the area.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)

## section: schools

Kelvin Grove State College serves much of Red Hill as a P-12 catchment. Petrie Terrace State School serves parts of the suburb. Catchment boundaries should be confirmed through the Queensland Government's catchment lookup at [qgso.qld.gov.au/maps/edmap](https://www.qgso.qld.gov.au/maps/edmap).

For private schooling, Marist College Ashgrove is within a short drive.

⚠️ VERIFY: Catchment boundaries vary by street.

## section: pockets

Red Hill breaks into two pockets.

The hilltop / view streets are elevated streets with city outlooks. Premium pocket.

The Musgrave Road precinct includes streets close to the village strip and Inner City Bypass.

Red Hill is named for its topography, with many streets sitting on slopes and ridges.

## section: lifestyle

Musgrave Road has a small village strip with cafes and casual dining. Paddington's Latrobe Terrace and Newmarket are within a short drive for additional dining.

For open space, Victoria Park and local pocket parks provide the closest options.

## section: buyer profile

Three buyer types drive the Red Hill market.

The largest is inner-CBD professionals wanting walk-to-CBD or short-drive lifestyle.

The second group is renovators. Character home stock attracts buyers looking for renovation projects.

The third group is catchment-led families targeting Kelvin Grove State College catchment.

## section: selling approach

Selling in Red Hill leans on the topography and the character home stock. Drone work and twilight shots that capture the elevation matter for view-line properties.

On method, both auction and private treaty work depending on the property and the buyer pool.
