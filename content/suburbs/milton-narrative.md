## section: transport

Milton has its own railway station on the Ferny Grove and Doomben line corridor. Off-peak travel into the CBD is short. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

Bus routes serve Milton via Milton Road and Coronation Drive.

By car, Coronation Drive puts the CBD around 3 to 6 minutes away off-peak. Western Freeway access handles southern travel.

For active transport, the Bicentennial Bikeway runs along the river to the CBD, and walking access to the CBD via Coronation Drive is genuine for many addresses.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)

## section: pockets

Milton breaks into three pockets.

The Park Road precinct is the apartment market and dining strip.

The Stadium precinct includes mixed-use stock close to Suncorp Stadium.

The riverside / Coronation Drive pocket includes apartments with river outlooks.

## section: lifestyle

Park Road is the suburb's village dining strip with cafes, restaurants, and bars. Suncorp Stadium sits within the suburb and shapes the suburb's rhythm on event nights.

For open space, the Suncorp Stadium precinct and Frew Park nearby provide walking and weekend options. The Brisbane River frontage along Coronation Drive offers riverside walking and cycling.

The lifestyle here is inner-CBD walk-to-everything for apartment buyers.
