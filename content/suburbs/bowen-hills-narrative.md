## section: transport

Bowen Hills has its own railway station with multiple line connections. The station is becoming a major interchange for the Cross River Rail project. Off-peak travel into the CBD is short. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

Bus routes serve Bowen Hills via Brunswick Street and Abbotsford Road.

By car, the Inner City Bypass and the Story Bridge sit on the doorstep. The CBD is around 3 to 6 minutes away off-peak.

For active transport, the inner-CBD walking and cycling routes connect Bowen Hills to Fortitude Valley and the city.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)
