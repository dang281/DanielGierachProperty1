## section: transport

Carina Heights does not have its own railway station. The closest stations are Coorparoo and Cannon Hill on the Cleveland line. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

Bus routes serve Carina Heights via Old Cleveland Road and Creek Road.

By car, the M3 corridor handles southern travel and the CBD sits around 14 to 18 minutes away off-peak.

For active transport, the Whites Hill Reserve walking trails are accessible from much of the suburb.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)

## section: schools

Carina State School is the local primary state school. Catchment boundaries should be confirmed through the Queensland Government's catchment lookup at [qgso.qld.gov.au/maps/edmap](https://www.qgso.qld.gov.au/maps/edmap).

For secondary state schooling, Whites Hill State College is the common catchment.

For private schooling, San Sisto College in Carina and Citipointe Christian College in Carindale are within a short drive.
