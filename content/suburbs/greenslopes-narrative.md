## section: transport

Greenslopes does not have its own railway station. The closest stations are Coorparoo and Buranda, both a drive for most addresses. The South-East Busway is on the doorstep and provides high-frequency CBD connection. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

Bus routes serve Greenslopes via Logan Road and the South-East Busway interchange.

By car, the M3 corridor handles southern travel and the CBD sits around 10 to 14 minutes away off-peak.

For active transport, the South-East Busway corridor and the Norman Creek bikeway connect through the suburb.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)

## section: schools

Greenslopes State School is the local primary state school. Catchment boundaries should be confirmed through the Queensland Government's catchment lookup at [qgso.qld.gov.au/maps/edmap](https://www.qgso.qld.gov.au/maps/edmap).

## section: pockets

The family streets near Norman Creek are quieter residential streets with established family-home stock.
