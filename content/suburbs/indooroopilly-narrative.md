## section: transport

Indooroopilly has its own railway station on the Ipswich line. Off-peak travel to Central runs around 12 to 16 minutes. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

Bus routes serve Indooroopilly via Moggill Road, Coonan Street, and the Walter Taylor Bridge corridor.

For active transport, the Western Suburbs bikeway and the Walter Taylor Bridge to Chelmer provide cycling and walking connections.
