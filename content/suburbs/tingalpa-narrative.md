## section: transport

Tingalpa does not have its own railway station. Hemmant station and Cannon Hill station on the Cleveland line are the closest options depending on which part of the suburb the address sits in. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

Bus routes serve Tingalpa via Wynnum Road, New Cleveland Road, and Manly Road. Routes change with Translink service updates.

By car, direct Gateway Motorway access is the standout transport asset. Brisbane Airport sits approximately 12 to 15 minutes off-peak via the Gateway, and the CBD is around 14 to 18 minutes off-peak via Wynnum Road.

For active transport, the Tingalpa Creek parkland and the Bulimba Creek bikeway connect through parts of the suburb.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)

## section: pockets

Tingalpa breaks into three working pockets.

The western residential pocket sits closer to Cannon Hill. Residential character, post-war and contemporary stock. Buyers looking for proximity to Cannon Hill amenity often start here.

Central Tingalpa includes larger residential streets with established family-home stock. Accessible entry point into the inner east corridor.

The industrial-adjacent pocket sits along the New Cleveland Road and Tingalpa light industrial corridor. The market prices the adjacency accurately, which means properties here trade at a discount compared to the rest of the suburb.

When buyers ask about Tingalpa, the immediate environment of the specific street matters more than the suburb name.

## section: buyer profile

Three buyer types drive the Tingalpa market.

The largest is first home buyers. Tingalpa is one of the most accessible entry points into the inner east corridor.

The second group is buyers priced out of Cannon Hill, Morningside, or Bulimba who want similar transport access at a more accessible price level.

The third group is investors. Motorway access and rental demand from the broader inner-east corridor support yield numbers that work for investor buyers.

A campaign here speaks to all three. The pocket and the immediate environment determine which segment leads.

## section: selling approach

Selling in Tingalpa means addressing the geography honestly and identifying which pocket the property sits in.

A western residential property runs the proximity-to-Cannon-Hill campaign with the value-against-Cannon-Hill story.

A central residential property runs the family-home or first-home campaign depending on the stock type.

An industrial-adjacent property has to be honest about the immediate environment in the marketing. Buyers who arrive without knowing leave disappointed; buyers who have understood the geography arrive informed and motivated.

On method, both auction and private treaty work in Tingalpa depending on the property and the buyer pool.
