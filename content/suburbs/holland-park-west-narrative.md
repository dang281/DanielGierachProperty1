## section: transport

Holland Park West does not have its own railway station. The closest station is Coorparoo on the Cleveland line, a drive from most addresses. Live timetables at translink.com.au/plan-your-journey.

Bus routes serve the suburb via Logan Road and the South-East Busway corridor.

By car, the M3 corridor handles southern travel and the CBD sits around 12 to 16 minutes away off-peak.

For active transport, the South-East Busway corridor connects through the area.

For full timetables, route maps, and live updates: [translink.com.au](https://translink.com.au)
