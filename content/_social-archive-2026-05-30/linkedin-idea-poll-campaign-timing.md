## Notes for Daniel

Evergreen poll. No time-sensitive references.
Suggested follow-up comment: The spring narrative is largely built on Sydney and Melbourne data. In Brisbane's inner east, the autumn window (late February through May) has historically been the stronger period for campaign competition. A well-prepared property launched in any month tends to outperform an unprepared one launched in peak season.
