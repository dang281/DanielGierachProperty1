# Cross River Rail Confirmed to Open 2029 — Not 2026

**Category:** infrastructure
**Relevance:** 9
**Post worthy:** no
**Suburbs:** Woolloongabba, East Brisbane, Fortitude Valley, New Farm
**Source:** https://statements.qld.gov.au/statements/104235
**Published date:** 2026-04-15 (correction logged — original announcement December 2024, confirmed October 2025)

Cross River Rail will not open until 2029. The Crisafulli Government confirmed in December 2024 that the project would not be complete until 2029, a three-year delay from the original 2026 target. The projected cost has exceeded $17 billion. Services will commence by 2029 following tunnelling completion, then a mandatory two-year testing and commissioning phase required by the National Rail Safety Regulator.
