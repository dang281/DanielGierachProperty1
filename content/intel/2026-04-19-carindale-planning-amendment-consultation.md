# BCC Carindale Tailored Amendment Package: Consultation Opens 24 April 2026

Brisbane City Council's tailored amendment package for Carindale (covering the major centre precinct) opens for public consultation on 24 April 2026. The specific proposed changes will not be published until that date. Amendments to major centre precincts typically address building height, land use mix, and transitional zones adjacent to residential streets. Directly relevant to property owners in Carindale: the submission period is the window to formally respond. Council is required to consider all submissions.
