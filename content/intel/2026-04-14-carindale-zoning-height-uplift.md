# Carindale Shopping Centre Precinct: Height Uplift to 30 Storeys Proposed

**Category:** planning
**Relevance:** 8
**Post worthy:** yes
**Suburbs:** Carindale
**Source:** https://www.brisbane.qld.gov.au/building-and-planning/planning-and-design/city-plan-amendments/tailored-amendment-package-indooroopilly-carindale-and-nundah
**Published date:** 2026-04-14

Brisbane City Council is proposing to lift the height cap around Carindale Shopping Centre from 10 storeys to up to 30 storeys, part of a tailored amendment package also covering Indooroopilly and Nundah. Public consultation opens 24 April 2026. The change is framed as an anti-sprawl measure to deliver more housing near established infrastructure ahead of the 2032 Olympics. For property owners and investors in Carindale, this signals a medium-term shift in the suburb's density profile and land value dynamics near the commercial core. Residential land close to the shopping centre precinct typically re-rates when height limits increase, as development potential is priced in.
