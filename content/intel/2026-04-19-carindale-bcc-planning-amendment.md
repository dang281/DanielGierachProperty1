Brisbane City Council has confirmed a formal City Plan amendment specifically targeting Carindale's major centre, alongside Indooroopilly and Nundah. Public consultation is scheduled to open on 24 April 2026, at which point the full amendment documents will be released.

Major centre amendments in Brisbane typically address building height limits, land use mix, and the density of commercial and residential development zones adjacent to existing residential streets. For Carindale property owners, the amendment could affect what can be built nearby, how the suburb's character evolves, and ultimately long-term values.

The submission window is the formal mechanism for residents and owners to influence the outcome. Post written and scheduled for April 21 (Tuesday 07:30) to alert Daniel's audience before consultation opens April 24.
