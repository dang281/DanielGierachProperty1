# NAB Housing Monitor April 2026: Brisbane +20% as Sydney and Melbourne Fall

**Category:** data
**Relevance:** 8
**Post worthy:** yes
**Suburbs:** 
**Source:** https://news.nab.com.au/tag/economic-market/nab-housing-monitor-april-2026
**Published date:** 2026-04-15

Brisbane dwelling prices rose approximately 20% on a three-month annualised basis to April 2026, continuing to lead capital city performance. Sydney fell 0.8% and Melbourne fell 2.5% on the same annualised basis. Combined capitals rose 9.3% over the past year with March up 0.6% month-on-month. The two-speed market divergence is now clearly reflected in NAB's monthly data, reinforcing the Brisbane supply-demand story.
