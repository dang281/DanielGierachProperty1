# QLD $30K First Home Owners Grant Expires June 30, 2026

**Category:** policy
**Relevance:** 8
**Post worthy:** yes
**Suburbs:** all
**Source:** https://spotonconveyancing.com.au/the-qld-first-home-buyer-windfall-how-to-claim-the-30000-grant-and-new-stamp-duty-removal-before-june-2026/
**Published date:** 2026-04-15

The QLD First Home Owners Grant is currently $30,000 for eligible first home buyers, but it reverts to $15,000 after contracts signed beyond June 30, 2026. The grant applies only to new homes under $750,000. Combined with zero stamp duty on new builds (from May 1, 2025) and the federal 5% deposit guarantee scheme, the total government assistance available before July 2026 can exceed $100,000 in equivalent value.

This is a genuine, time-sensitive incentive for inner-east buyers considering a new build or house-and-land package. With around 2.5 months remaining, a May or June LinkedIn post specifically addressing the deadline would be timely and useful for first home buyers in Daniel's audience.
