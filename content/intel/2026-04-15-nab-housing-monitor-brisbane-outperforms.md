# NAB Housing Monitor April 2026: Brisbane +20% While Melbourne Contracts

**Category:** data
**Relevance:** 8
**Post worthy:** yes
**Suburbs:** 
**Source:** https://news.nab.com.au/tag/economic-market/nab-housing-monitor-april-2026
**Published date:** 2026-04-15

NAB's April 2026 Housing Monitor shows Brisbane dwelling prices growing at approximately 20% on a three-month annualised basis, while Sydney fell 0.8% and Melbourne contracted 2.5% over the same measure. Across combined capitals, prices rose 9.3% over the past year and 0.6% month-on-month in March.

The divergence is significant: Brisbane is running roughly 22 percentage points ahead of Melbourne on the same metric. The driver is structural: inner-east listing stock remains tight (total listings down approximately 26% year-on-year as of early 2026), finance-ready buyer demand is strong, and Olympic and Cross River Rail infrastructure investment continues to underpin medium-term confidence. For inner-east sellers, this data supports positioning — the conditions that produce strong auction results are still present.
