# QLD Government Releases Hamilton Northshore Land for 3,000 Homes

**Category:** development
**Relevance:** 7
**Post worthy:** no
**Suburbs:**
**Source:** https://statements.qld.gov.au/statements/104539
**Published date:** 2026-04-14

The Crisafulli Government is releasing five parcels at Northshore Hamilton PDA (6 hectares of riverfront) for up to 3,000 homes plus mixed-use retail and hospitality. Infrastructure investment exceeds $100 million. Proposal deadline is 16 April 2026; sites will be ready for development from 2028, with riverfront parklands construction commencing 2029. The land was previously earmarked for the Brisbane Athletes Village. Hamilton is north of the river from Bulimba and Hawthorne, so not directly in Daniel's core market, but the project reinforces the broader Brisbane riverfront narrative and signals sustained government commitment to inner-city housing supply. Watch for flow-on demand effects on the south bank inner east as Hamilton supply comes to market from 2028.
