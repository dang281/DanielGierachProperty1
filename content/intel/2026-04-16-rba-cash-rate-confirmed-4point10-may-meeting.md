# RBA Cash Rate Confirmed at 4.10% — May 5 Decision Approaching

**Category:** policy
**Relevance:** 8
**Post worthy:** no
**Suburbs:**
**Source:** https://www.rba.gov.au/media-releases/2026/mr-26-08.html
**Published date:** 2026-03-17

Rate verified 2026-04-16 for April 21 post accuracy check. Two hikes in 2026: February 3 (to 3.85%) and March 17 (to 4.10%). The March decision was a 5-4 split vote, indicating a divided board. Next meeting is May 4-5, 2026. Big-4 bank forecasts are mixed: some expect a hold, others see further tightening risk if inflation remains elevated. The split vote is significant for the April 21 post framing — the board is genuinely divided, not leaning strongly in either direction. Any post published between now and May 5 that references the RBA should note the next decision date and the board division, not assume a clear direction. The 4.10% figure is confirmed current as of today.
