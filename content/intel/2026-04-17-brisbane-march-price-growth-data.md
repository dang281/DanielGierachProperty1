Brisbane recorded monthly dwelling value growth of approximately +1.8% in March 2026, outpacing other mid-sized capitals in that month. The Brisbane median reached $1,054,555 as of January 2026 with annual growth running at approximately +15.7%. Days on market for Brisbane sits at approximately 17 days. New listings are running approximately 3.3% ahead of the same period last year, but total advertised stock on an annual basis has fallen, keeping supply constrained relative to demand.
