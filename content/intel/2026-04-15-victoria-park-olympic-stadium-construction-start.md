# Victoria Park Olympic Stadium: Construction Confirmed to Start June 2026

**Category:** infrastructure
**Relevance:** 8
**Post worthy:** yes
**Suburbs:** East Brisbane, Fortitude Valley, Spring Hill, New Farm
**Source:** https://brisbanedevelopment.com.au/work-set-to-commence-on-brisbanes-63000-seat-olympic-stadium/
**Published date:** 2026-04-15

A $3.8 billion, 63,000-seat Olympic stadium at Victoria Park is confirmed to begin construction on 1 June 2026. The Japanese-Australian design consortium of Cox Architecture, Hassell and Azusa Sekkei was selected on 5 January 2026. Geotechnical investigations began on site in October 2025. The stadium will host the 2032 opening and closing ceremonies and athletics events.
