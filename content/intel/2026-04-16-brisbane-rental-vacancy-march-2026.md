# Brisbane Rental Vacancy Rate March 2026: 1.6%

**Category:** market
**Relevance:** 7
**Post worthy:** no
**Suburbs:** 
**Source:** propertyupdate.com.au citing CoreLogic/SQM data, April 2026
**Published date:** 2026-04-16

Brisbane rental vacancy rate held at 1.6% in March 2026, remaining well below the balanced-market threshold of around 3%. Vacancy rates in many inner-east suburbs are below 1%. This sustained undersupply is supporting rental yields and maintaining investor demand for well-located inner-east properties despite the higher interest rate environment. Useful context for investor-focused content and for reinforcing the demand-supply imbalance narrative in authority posts.

**Additional verified data points from the same search (CoreLogic, April 2026):**
- Brisbane median dwelling value: approximately $1,054,555 (January 2026)
- Monthly growth rate (March 2026): +1.8%
- Median days on market (quarter to January 2026): 17 days
- Sales volume: down approximately 3.7% year-on-year as of January 2026 (stock constraint, not demand weakness)
